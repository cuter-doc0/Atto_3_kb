Left Front Door Wiring Harness - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Raise the left front door glass to the limit position.

  2. Remove the instrument panel left lower guard assembly. See Instrument Panel Left Lower Guard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove the glove box assembly. See Glove Box Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  4. Remove the instrument panel lower left sound absorbing baffle assembly. See Instrument Panel Lower Left Sound Absorbing Baffle Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  5. Remove the left front door sill guard assembly. See Left Front Door Sill Guard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  6. Remove the left front door sealing plug I. See Left Front Door Sealing Plug I - Removal and Installation 

  7. Remove the left front door sealing plug II. See Left Front Door Sealing Plug II - Removal and Installation 

  8. Disconnect 5 connectors of the left front door wiring harness.

  9. Remove 1 ground bolt from the left front door wiring harness.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  10. Detach 2 fixing clips from the left front door wiring harness.

  11. Detach 6 fixing clips, and detach the left front door wiring harness from the vehicle body.

    Caution
    Detach the left front door harness cover fixing clip on the right side of the instrument panel with a slot type screwdriver, and push out the right front door harness cover.

  12. Disconnect 6 connectors of the left front door wiring harness.

  13. Detach 27 fixing clips from the left front door wiring harness.

  14. Detach 2 wiring harness plugs, and take out the left front door wiring harness.

Installation

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
