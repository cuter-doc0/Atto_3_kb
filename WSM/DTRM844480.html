Visual inspection of high-voltage components and wires

Caution

  • Risk of electric shock! Before operation, high-voltage components in the operating area shall be visually checked.

  • Any work on a vehicle equipped with a high-voltage system shall be only carried out by qualified personnel.

  • In case of any abnormality or doubt, handling shall be only carried out by qualified personnel in HV or electrical aspect.

Reminder

Be sure to open the hood before inspection.

Inspection steps

  1. Power off the vehicle.

  2. Check the high-voltage wiring harness and connector.

    1. Check that the high-voltage wiring harness and connector are free of obvious dust deposits.

    2. Check that the high-voltage wiring harness is connected reliably, without damage or abnormal deformation.

    3. Check that the high-voltage wiring harness is routed correctly and fixed firmly, without interference.

    4. Check that the high-voltage connector is free of heating and discoloration, and connected firmly.

    Reminder

    In case of any defect, it must be handled immediately by qualified personnel.

  3. Check the high-voltage components.

    Reminder

    • Attention must be paid to all high-voltage components during inspection.

    • All high-voltage components shall be free of external damage.

    1. Check that the high-voltage components are free of deformation and leakage.

    2. Check that the high-voltage components are free of water seepage marks. If any, remove dirt and dry them.

    Reminder

    In case of any defect, it must be handled immediately by qualified personnel.

  4. Obtain a new work order for repair or replacement.
