Calibration of standard mileage condition

Calibration Procedure

Caution

  • After replacing the new power battery assembly, VDS is required to calibrate the standard mileage of the vehicle.

  • Before executing the calibration, check and confirm VDS software has been updated to the latest version.

  1. Use VDS to connect the vehicle OBD interface. Refer to the VDS Instructions

    Caution
    OBD interface is located at left lower corner of left lower guard plate of instrument panel.

  2. Set the vehicle power supply to OFF, enter “Vehicle Diagnostic System”, and select the vehicle model.

  3. After scanning the vehicle module, enter the "BASU module".

  4. Select "Standard Operating Condition” in the diagnosis interface.

  5. Select the operating condition to be calibrated in the "Action Execution" interface on the left (based on the calibration of local range).

  6. Click the "Execute" button at the bottom of the "Action Execution" interface on the left to calibrate the standard mileage operating condition.

  7. Once the calibration is successful, "Operation Succeeded" will pop up.

  8. Click the "Execute" button at the bottom of the "Data Information" interface on the right to confirm the calibration results. If the information on the left and right is consistent, the calibration is successful. If not, repeat the calibration process steps.
