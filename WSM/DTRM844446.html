Lubrication of Door Brake

Reminder

The inspection and lubrication methods of the four door brakes are the same. Take the front left one as an example.

Inspection steps

  1. Open the front left door, check that the brake is free of abnormal sound or damage, and replace it if damaged. Refer to Front Door Stopper Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove dust from the pull rod with a clean soft cloth, and apply 0.3-0.8 g grease on the pressure-bearing surface of the pull rod and the rotating shaft.

    Reminder

    • It is recommended to use original grease for best results.

    • Be careful not to soil other components.

  3. Close and open the front left door once or twice to remove excess grease.

  4. Obtain a new work order for repair or replacement.
