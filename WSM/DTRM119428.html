Inspection of Free Clearance of Steering Wheel

  1. Check the free clearance of steering wheel.

    1. Position the front wheels for driving in a straight line.

    2. Power off the vehicle.

    3. Mark the top of the steering wheel.

    4. Place a ruler against the top of the steering wheel.

    5. Measure the maximum distance to which the steering wheel can turn left under the condition that front wheels do not rotate.

    6. Turn the steering wheel back to its original position.

    7. Measure the maximum distance to which the steering wheel can turn right under the condition that front wheels do not rotate.

    8. If the sum of the maximum left and right distances is not greater than 30 mm, the free stroke of the steering wheel is normal; otherwise, check the steering system.
