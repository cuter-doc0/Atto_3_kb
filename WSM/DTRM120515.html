Adjustment of Front Door Assembly

Adjustment procedure

Caution
The removal and installation methods of the left and the right front door assembly are the same. The left front door assembly is taken as an example.

  1. Remove the left fender assembly. See Left Fender Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Loosen 1 fixing bolt of the front door stopper assembly and the body.

    • Tightening torque: 20±2N•m

  3. Adjust the front door assembly (body side).

    1. Loosen the fixing bolts of the front door upper hinge assembly, the front door lower hinge assembly and the body.

    2. Move the front door assembly longitudinally and vertically, so that the clearances are even, without sinking or protruding and with the contour properly aligned, when the front door assembly is closed. It indicates that the front door assembly is properly adjusted.

    3. Tighten 4 fixing bolts of the front door upper hinge assembly, the front door lower hinge assembly and the body after the adjustment.

      • Tightening torque: 28±2N•m

  4. Adjust the front door assembly (door side).

    1. Loosen the fixing bolts of the front door upper hinge assembly, the front door lower hinge assembly and the front door assembly.

    2. Move the front door assembly longitudinally and vertically, so that the clearances are even, without sinking or protruding and with the contour properly aligned, when the front door assembly is closed. It indicates that the front door assembly is properly adjusted.

    3. Tighten the fixing bolts of the front door upper hinge assembly, the front door lower hinge assembly and the front door assembly after the adjustment.

      • Tightening torque: 28±2N•m

  5. Tighten 1 fixing bolt of the front door stopper assembly and the body.

    • Tightening torque: 20±2N•m
