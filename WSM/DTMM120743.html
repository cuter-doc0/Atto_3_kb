System Function Principle

The body control module is used to control the electrical system of the vehicle. This vehicle adopts three body control modules to control different electrical systems respectively. Control Modules communicate with each other through CAN lines.

  1. Left body control module

    1. PEPS

    2. Instrument control

    3. Ventilation and heating of driver seat

    4. Turn signal/hazard warning light

    5. Signal light system

    6. Left window anti-pinch motor

    7. Headlight system

    8. Left outside rearview mirror

    9. Left window motor

    10. Ambient light

    11. Reversing radar

    12. Front and rear wiper

    13. Column adjustment

    14. Left child lock

    15. Direct tire pressure system

    16. Driver seat adjustment

    17. Left foot light, interior light

    18. Electric lock of charging port

    19. Left door lock

    20. Horn

    21. Electric defrosting system

  2. Right body control module:

    1. Gateway

    2. Heat pump system

    3. Ventilation and heating of front passenger seat

    4. Automatic anti dazzle inside rearview mirror

    5. Front and rear wiper washing system

    6. Right window anti pinch motor

    7. Air Conditioning System

    8. Turn signal/hazard warning light

    9. Right window motor

    10. Right door unlocking and locking

    11. Right charging port light

    12. Right cap release and close

    13. Ambient light

    14. Coolant circulating pump system

    15. Right foot light, interior light

    16. Front Passenger Seat Adjustment

    17. Drive sunroof and blinder motor

    18. Tailgate lock

    19. Right outside rearview mirror

  3. Rear body control module:

    1. EPB

    2. Electric tailgate system
