Tailgate Hinge Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

Reminder
The removal and installation methods of the tailgate hinge assembly on the two sides are the same. The left side tailgate hinge assembly is taken as an example.

  1. Remove the left C-pillar upper guard assembly. See Left C-Pillar Upper Guard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Detach partial ceiling assembly from the roof.

    Caution
    Only lift to a height enabling the removal of fixing nuts.

  3. Remove 2 fixing nuts of the tailgate hinge assembly.

    Caution

    • Care should be taken when removing the tailgate left hinge assembly to avoid scratching the surface material.

    • When removing the tailgate left hinge assembly, the help of another technician is required.

    • Tightening torque: 25±3N•m

  4. Remove 2 fixing bolts, and take out the tailgate hinge assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 25±3N•m

Install

  1. The installation is performed in reverse order of removal.
