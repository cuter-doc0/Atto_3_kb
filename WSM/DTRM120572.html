Tailgate Left Air Spring - Removal and Installation

Removal

Reminder
The removal and installation methods of the tailgate left air spring body and the tailgate right air spring body are the same. The tailgate left air spring body is taken as an example.

  1. Open the tailgate.

  2. Pry up the pin with a minisize flat-head screwdriver, and disconnect from the tailgate left gas spring body bracket.

    Caution

    • When detaching the tailgate left air spring body, the help of another technician is required to support the tailgate assembly.

    • When prying up the pin with a minisize slot type screwdriver, do not pry up the pin completely from the ball cage.

  3. Pry up the pin with a minisize flat-head screwdriver, and disconnect from the tailgate left gas spring door bracket, and take out the left air spring body.

Installation

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
