Front Bumper Beam Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Remove the front module assembly. See Front Module Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the engine sound simulator. See Engine Sound Simulator - Removal and Installation 

    Reminder

    This step is only applicable to the disassembly and installation of models equipped with an engine sound simulator.

  3. Remove the engine sound simulator 2. See Engine Sound Simulator 2 - Removal and Installation 

    Reminder

    This step is only applicable to the disassembly and installation of models equipped with an engine sound simulator type 2.

  4. Detach 4 wiring harness clips from the front bumper beam assembly.

  5. Remove 8 fixing bolts, and take out the front bumper beam assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 70±7N·m

Installation

  1. Follow the removal steps in reverse order to install.
