Rear Bumper Body Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Remove the rear bumper assembly. See Rear Bumper Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the rear small probe. See Rear Small Probe - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove the rear millimeter wave radar. See Left Rear Millimeter Wave Radar - Removal and Installation 

  4. Remove the rear fog light. See Rear Fog Light- Removal and Installation 

  5. Remove the rear bumper line. See Rear Bumper Line - Removal and Installation 

  6. Remove the left rear retro-reflector. See Left Rear Retro-reflector - Removal and Installation 

  7. Remove right rear retro-reflector. See Right Rear Retro-reflector - Removal and Installation 

  8. Take out the rear bumper body assembly.

Installation

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
