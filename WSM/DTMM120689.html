System Function Principle

The USB charging connector on the vehicle is used to provide standard power supply of the vehicle to external electrical equipment, or used as a fixed power interface for electrical equipment. It can be used for charging mobile phones, MP3, tablet computers and other electrical equipment, or as a power interface for e-dogs and digital video recorder (DVR).

The main function of the USB interface on the vehicle is to plug in the USB flash disk to listen to songs or to transmit data for devices such as mobile phones. The USB interface is generally a universal interface for USB devices such as USB flash disk and MP3. It can also charge electric devices with small power such as mobile phones and MP3. USB interface is a vehicle-mounted transmission line, which has the advantages of fast transmission speed, convenient use, hot plug support, flexible connection, independent power supply, etc.
