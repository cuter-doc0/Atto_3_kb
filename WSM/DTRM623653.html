PM2.5 Quick Tester Assembly (Optional) - Removal and Installation

Removal

Reminder

This removal and installation procedure is only applicable to models equipped with a PM2.5 quick tester assembly.

  1. Remove the ventilation cover assembly. See Ventilation Cover Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Detach 4 fixing clips, and detach the connection between PM2.5 quick tester assembly and front wall.

  3. Remove the instrument panel upper body assembly. See Instrument Panel Upper Body Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  4. Remove the PM2.5 quick tester assembly.

    1. Detach one fixing clip, and detach the instrument panel lower body.

    2. Detach one fixing clip of PM2.5 quick tester assembly pipe

  5. Remove the PM2.5 quick tester assembly.

    1. Disconnect 1 connectors.

    2. Remove 2 fixing bolts, and take out the PM2.5 quick tester assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

Installation

  1. Follow the removal steps in reverse order to install.
