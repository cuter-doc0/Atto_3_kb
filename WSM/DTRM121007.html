Right Front Door Wiring Harness - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Lift the right front door glass to the limit position.

  2. Remove the instrument panel right lower guard assembly. See Instrument Panel Right Lower Guard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove the right front door sill guard assembly. See Right Front Door Sill Guard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  4. Remove the right front door sealing plug I. See Right Front Door Sealing Plug I - Removal and Installation 

  5. Remove the right front door sealing plug II. See Right Front Door Sealing Plug II - Removal and Installation 

  6. Disconnect 4 connectors of the right front door wiring harness.

  7. Remove 1 ground bolt of the right front door wiring harness.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  8. Detach 3 fixing clips from the right front door wiring harness.

  9. Detach 6 fixing clips, and detach the right front door wiring harness from the body.

    Caution
    Detach the left front door harness cover fixing clip on the right side of the instrument panel with a slot type screwdriver, and push out the right front door harness cover.

  10. Disconnect 6 connectors of the right front door wiring harness.

  11. Detach 27 fixing clips from the right front door wiring harness.

  12. Detach 2 wiring harness rubber plugs, and take out the right front door wiring harness.

Installation

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
