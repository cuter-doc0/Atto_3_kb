System Function Principle

The electric outside rearview mirror system can be controlled with the switch on the door trim panel of the driver's side. When an object strikes the electric outside rearview mirror, the shell will expand at a certain angle along the force direction to prevent the rearview mirror from being damaged. For example, when the mirror glass is impacted and broken, small fragments will not be separated from the mirror support plate, which is safe.

Main functions of rearview mirrors

Lens adjustment

The driver can get the best vision from the rearview mirror by adjusting the lens up, down, left and right.

Manual folding function

The switch controls the folding and unfolding of the outside rearview mirrors to improve the trafficability characteristic and the protect the outside rearview mirrors.

Heating function

Defrost the outside rearview mirrors through the heating function.

Automatic folding function

  1. Set the vehicle power supply to OFF. Press the remote locking button. The door will be locked. BCM (integrated into the left domain) will drive the electric outside rearview mirrors to fold automatically.

  2. Set the vehicle power supply to OFF. Press the remote unlocking button. The door will be unlocked. BCM will drive the electric outside rearview mirror to unfold.

Panoramic camera monitoring function

The cameras installed on the outside rearview mirrors can obtain the field of vision of the left and right blind spots, providing assistance for driving and parking.

Outside rearview mirror footlight function

Courtesy light, providing light source for panoramic cameras of outside rearview mirrors.
