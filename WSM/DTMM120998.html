System Function Principle

Harnesses are the basic carrier of the internal communication of the vehicle, and the main body of the vehicle circuit network, which connects the electrical and electronic components of the vehicle and makes them function. Without the harnesses, there is no vehicle circuit. They are composed of wires, connectors and wrapping tape, which ensure the transmission of electrical signals and the reliability of the circuit connection, supply the specified current value to the electronic and electrical components, prevent electromagnetic interference to the surrounding circuits, and eliminate short circuits.
