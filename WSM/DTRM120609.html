Removal of Sunroof Assembly

Disassemble

  1. Remove the sun visor motor. See Sunroof Blinder Motor - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the rear blinder motor. See Rear Blinder Motor - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove the front glass assembly. See Front Glass Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  4. Remove the rear glass assembly. See Rear Glass Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  5. Remove the sunroof assembly. See Sunroof Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  6. Detach 7 fixing clips in the front of the air guide rod assembly.

  7. Detach 2 fixing clips on the back of the air guide rod assembly.

  8. Take out the air guide rod assembly.

  9. Remove 8 fixing screws.

  10. Detach the sunroof blinder from the slide rail.

    1. Pull out the sunroof blinder front bracket from the slide rail on both sides.

    2. Detach the sunroof rear traverse frame, and pull out the sunroof blinder.

  11. Detach 2 hangers on the right side of the sunroof blinder.

  12. Detach 2 hangers on the left side of the sunroof blinder.

  13. Remove 2 fixing bolts, and take out the blinder extension guide rails on both sides.

  14. Take out the sunroof rear traverse frame and blinder.

  15. Take out the sunroof frame.

Assemble

  1. The assembly is performed in reverse order of disassembly.

  2. Align the fixing clip with the bottom limit groove when installing the air guide rod assembly.
