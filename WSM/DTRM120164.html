Left Rear Door Guard Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Power off the low voltage electrical system. See Power-off and Power-on of Low Voltage Electrical System 

  2. Remove the left rear window control panel assembly. See Left Rear Window Control Panel Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove the left rear door handle box cover. See Left Rear Door Handle Box Cover Plate - Removal and Installation 

  4. Remove one fixing bolt from the left rear door guard assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 5±0.5N•m

  5. Rotate 45 ° clockwise, and take out the left rear door snap handle ambient light.

  6. Remove 2 fixed bolts from the left rear door guard assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 5±0.5N•m

  7. Pry up the left rear door guard assembly.

  8. Disconnect one connector of the left tweeter speaker.

  9. Disconnect one connector of the left rear door snap handle ambient light assembly.

  10. Disconnect one connector of the left rear door ambient light assembly.

  11. Disconnect one connector of the left door light.

  12. Disconnect 2 connections between the door lock cable and the left rear door inner handle assembly.

  13. Take out the left rear door guard assembly.

  14. Remove the left rear door snap handle ambient light assembly. See Right Rear Door Snap Handle Ambient Light Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  15. Remove the left door light (door). See Left Door Light (Door) - Removal and Installation 

Installation

Caution

  • Before installation, it is necessary to check whether all the clips are missing or damaged, and if so, install new clips, and then install the trim panel;

  • After installation, check whether all clips are installed in place. The installation position of the trim panel may deviate, resulting in warping or abnormal gaps.

  1. Follow the removal steps in reverse order to install.
