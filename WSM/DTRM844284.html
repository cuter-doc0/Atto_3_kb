Reading and Clearing of Fault Records

Repair tools and equipment required

  • VDS

Caution

  • During the test drive, the VDS must be fixed on the rear seat to ensure safety.

  • Only one accompanying person is allowed to operate the VDS during driving.

  1. Check and clear the fault record.

    1. Connect and turn on the VDS.

    2. Power on the vehicle (OK mode).

    3. Check all electrical systems of the vehicle.

      Reminder

      Follow the instructions on the screen to activate the required functions.

      • Check all electrical systems of the vehicle for any DTC, and clear the DTC if any.

      • Read the DTC again. If any DTC still exists, clear it after eliminating the fault.

Caution

Be sure to delete all DTCs before delivering the vehicle to the customer.

Caution

If any module needs to be updated, please inform the customer and carry out updates after obtaining the customer's consent.

Static fault

If one or more static fault records exist in the fault memory, it is recommended to communicate with the customer and eliminate the fault(s).

Occasional fault

If there are only occasional faults or prompt descriptions in the fault memory and the customer has not reported any fault related to the vehicle electrical system, all DTCs will be cleared.
