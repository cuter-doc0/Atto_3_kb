Compressor Discharge Pipe Assembly 1 - Removal and Installation

Removal

Caution
After removing pipeline connections, properly seal all pipe fittings as soon as possible to prevent dust, impurities, and foreign objects from entering the A/C system and causing system faults.

  1. Recover the refrigerant See Recovery and Filling of Refrigerant 

  2. Power off the low voltage electrical system. See Power-off and Power-on of Low Voltage Electrical System 

  3. Remove the front motor lower mudguard assembly, See Front Motor Lower Mudguard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  4. Remove the lower bracket of the battery. See Lower Bracket of Battery - Removal and Installation 

  5. Remove 1 fixing bolt, and detach the refrigeration tube assembly 4 from the gas-liquid separator assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  6. Remove 1 fixing bolt, and detach the compressor discharge pipe assembly 1 from the electric compressor assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  7. Remove 1 fixing bolt, and detach the compressor discharge pipe assembly 1 bracket from the electric compressor assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 22±2N•m

  8. Disconnect 1 connector of compressor discharge pipe assembly 1.

  9. Disconnect 1 connector of pressure sensor.

    1. Disconnect the rubber cover of pressure sensor.

    2. Disconnect 1 connector.

  10. Remove 1 fixing bolts and take out the compressor discharge pipe assembly 1.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

Installation

Caution
For the installation, all “O-rings” of the pipeline must be replaced, and refrigerant oil shall be applied on the surface of the seal rings and the seal rings shall be connected according to the stipulated torque.

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
