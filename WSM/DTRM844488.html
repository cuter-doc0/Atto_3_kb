Inspection of Warning Labels for High-Voltage Components

Warning labels are available on all high-voltage components.

Do not damage or soil these labels during maintenance, and ensure that all high-voltage components are provided with such labels.

Reminder

  • During routine maintenance, only check the status of the warning labels within the visible range!

  • When any high-voltage component has no warning label, inform the customer of this situation and of paying special attention to safety at relevant positions during his/her self-maintenance of the vehicle.
