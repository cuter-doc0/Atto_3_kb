Carpet Assembly

System Function Principle

The carpet assembly gets the functions of keeping-warm and adjustment, sound absorption, comfort, and beauty.

  1. Keeping-warm and adjustment: The gap between the fibers of carpet fabric can adjust the air humidity. When the humidity in the vehicle is high, it can absorb the moisture in the air. When the vehicle is dry, the moisture will be released, so that the humidity in the vehicle can be balanced. Carpet fabrics are mostly made from various fibers with good thermal insulation performance. Laying carpets on a large area can reduce the loss of heat through the ground, block the invasion of cold air on the ground, and make passengers feel warm and comfortable.

  2. Sound absorption: The carpet has rich texture and tufted surface, which has good sound absorption effect and can appropriately reduce the impact of noise. As the carpet absorbs the sound, it reduces the multiple reflections of the sound, thereby improving the performance of audio equipment.

  3. Comfort: The carpet is full of elastic fibers. It has a plump, thick and soft texture. It feels comfortable and soft, which is conducive to eliminating fatigue and tension.

  4. Beauty: The carpet is plump in texture and gorgeous in appearance. The vehicle looks dignified and rich with it. Excellent decorative effect is obtained.
