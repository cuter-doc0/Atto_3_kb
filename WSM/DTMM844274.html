Additional Maintenance Information

Vehicle maintenance shall be based on the mileage and months, whichever comes first.

In addition to periodic maintenance or periodic inspection, other maintenance work shall be carried out according to the service conditions and vehicle configuration.

According to the records in the maintenance manual (or the next maintenance record on the label), additional maintenance operations can also be carried out outside the maintenance cycle.
