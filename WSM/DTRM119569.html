High Voltage Distribution Wiring Harness Sub-assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

Danger
For the maintenance of high pressure system, (on the vehicle, wiring harnesses of the high voltage system is in orange). Make sure to wear protective equipment, including insulation gloves, shoes and masks, and make sure to power off the high voltage system before carrying out the maintenance.

  1. Power off the low voltage electrical system. See Power-off and Power-on of Low Voltage Electrical System 

  2. Power off the high voltage electrical system. See Power-off and power-on of high voltage electrical system 

  3. Remove the front motor lower mudguard assembly. See Front Motor Lower Mudguard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  4. Press to detach the connector tab upwards.

  5. Pull the connector locking bracket upward to detach the high voltage distribution wiring harness sub-assembly from the drive motor control module assembly.

    Caution
    After the high voltage components are removed, they shall be sealed and insulated to prevent dust and impurities from entering or accidentally touching the high voltage terminals.

  6. Remove 2 fixing bolts, and detach the high voltage distribution wiring harness sub-assembly from the powertrain.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  7. Remove 2 fixing bolts of the high voltage distribution wiring harness sub-assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  8. Remove 2 fixing bolts, detach the high voltage distribution wiring harness sub-assembly from the power battery pack, and take out the high voltage distribution wiring harness sub-assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 5±0.5N•m

Installation

  1. Follow the removal steps in reverse order to install.
