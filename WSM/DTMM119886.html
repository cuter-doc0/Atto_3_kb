System Function Principle

The brake applies pressure to the brake caliper through the hydraulic system, making the brake pads rub against the brake discs that rotate with the wheels, thereby realize braking. The rear brake of this vehicle uses a motor caliper, which clamps the brake pads by the motor caliper to realize parking brake.
