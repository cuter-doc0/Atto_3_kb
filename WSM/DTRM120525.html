Left Rear Door Glass Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Remove the left rear door inner waist belt assembly. See Left Rear Door Inner Waist Belt - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the left rear door outer waist belt. See Left Rear Door Outer Waist Belt - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove the left rear door rear movable guide rail and mud tank assembly. See Left Rear Door Rear Movable Guide Rail and Mud Tank Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  4. Remove the left rear door glass assembly.

    1. Peel off 1 bolt patch, and remove 2 fixing bolts.

      • Tightening torque: 8~10N•m

    2. Take out the left rear door glass assembly.

    Caution

    Before removing the two fixing bolts, connect the negative electrode of the battery and connect relevant electrical components, and then adjust the fixing bolts of the rear door glass to the removal position.

    In the event the removal process cannot be proceeded due to window regulator motor fault, remove the fixing bolts of the rear window regulator, so as to adjust the rear door glass to the removal position.

Installation

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
