Precautions for maintenance

Caution

  • Operators shall wear workwear, insulating gloves, insulated shoes, goggles and other insulating protective equipment.

  • Ensure that the work site is dry and ventilated, tools and instruments are placed neatly, and fire extinguishers are provided when necessary.

  • Check the use safety of instruments and equipment, such as lifts and jacks.

  • Check the safe driving and operation conditions of the vehicle to ensure smooth maintenance.

  • Please put on the seat cover and steering wheel cover when the vehicle enters the site. Avoid contaminating the vehicle during operation.

  • Before driving into the lift frame, make sure that there is sufficient distance between the bottom and both sides of the vehicle and the lift frame to avoid scratching.

  • Before lifting the vehicle, make sure that the vehicle weight does not exceed the rated load of the lift.

  • When lifting the vehicle, be sure to select the correct lifting point.

  • When lifting the vehicle, it is not allowed to start the vehicle and engage a gear with driving wheels on the ground; otherwise, vehicle damage or personal injury may be caused.

  • Operators shall strictly abide by various safety rules and use rules during maintenance; otherwise, vehicle damage or personal injury may be caused.

  • Use original spare parts when replacing components and wearing parts to ensure the safety and performance of the vehicle.

  • After maintenance, a test drive must be carried out for inspection, with special attention to the brake system and passenger protection system.

  • The DTCs must be cleared before the vehicle can be handed over to the customer.
