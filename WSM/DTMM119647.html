System Function Principle

The main functions of suspension: to mitigate the impact of rugged road on wheels, determine the position of wheels and axles, and improve the grip of wheels on the road. The suspension has a certain influence on the comfort and handling stability of the vehicle. The front suspension of this vehicle is a MacPherson independent suspension, which installs the coil spring and the shock absorber on the same axle, forming an almost vertical state with the wheel, so as to support the wheel. The lower arm is basically parallel to the road surface, so as to fix the position of the axle, and improve the comfort of the vehicle.
