PAD Display Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Power off the low voltage electrical system. See Power-off and Power-on of Low Voltage Electrical System 

  2. Remove the PAD upper cover assembly. See PAD Upper Cover Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove 2 fixing bolts of the PAD display assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 3±0.5N•m

  4. Turn the PAD display assembly 45 ° clockwise, and take it out carefully.

  5. Disconnect 1 connector, and take out the PAD display assembly.

    Caution
    Properly place the removed PAD display assembly to prevent heavy objects from squeezing, hitting or scratching the screen.

Installation

Caution

  • Before installation, it is necessary to check whether all the clips are missing or damaged, and if so, install new clips, and then install the trim panel;

  • After installation, check whether all clips are installed in place. The installation position of the trim panel may deviate, resulting in warping or abnormal gaps.

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
