System Function Principle

The tailgate trim is an important part of the vehicle. It is not only a decorative part, which reflects the refinement of the interior and exterior trim, but also shields the internal structure such as sheet metal and harness. The structural reliability of the tailgate trim is very important.

The spoiler assembly refers to the protruding object shaped like a duck tail on the rear end of the trunk lid, which is part of the vehicle aerodynamic kit.

Function of spoiler assembly:

  1. In the process of fast driving, it generates downward pressure, making the vehicle produce greater grip, which can effectively reduce the air resistance generated.

  2. It counteracts part of the lift force, reduces the influence of wind resistance, makes the vehicle close to the road and improves the driving stability.

  3. Make the vehicle look more fashionable, beautiful and sporty.
