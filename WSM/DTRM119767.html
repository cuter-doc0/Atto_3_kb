IPB System Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

Caution

  • The brake fluid has a strong corrosive effect on the paint. If it is accidentally splashed on the paint of the vehicle body, it must be cleaned with a rag as soon as possible.

  • Avoid oil contact with skin. If it is accidentally splashed into the eyes, immediately rinse with plenty of water and seek medical attention as soon as possible.

  • Do not discharge the oil at will, and recycle it according to local regulations.

  1. Set the IPB system assembly to "Installation mode". Refer to Installation Mode of Intelligent Power Braking System 

  2. Remove the brake fluid reservoir mounting bracket assembly. Refer to Brake Fluid Reservoir Mounting Bracket Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove the brake fluid reservoir connecting hose assembly. Refer to Brake Fluid Reservoir Connecting Hose Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  4. Remove the lower right shield assembly of instrument panel. Refer to Instrument Panel Right Lower Guard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  5. Handle the IPB system. Refer to Handling of IPB System Assembly 

    Caution

    Strictly follow this step during removal or handling.

  6. Remove 4 fixing nuts from the brake pedal assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 25±2 N•m

  7. Lock the crankshaft with a special tool (tool name: IPB CV joint disassembly tooling; Material No.: 13461862-00) Disconnect the connection of IPB system assembly

  8. Disconnect 2 low-voltage connectors and move the front compartment wiring harness aside.

  9. Detach the brake tube, and remove the intelligent power brake system assembly.

    1. Remove two M12 pipe joints and detach the brake pipe.

      • Tightening torque: 20±2 N•m

    2. Remove two M10 tube connectors, detach the brake tube, and take out the intelligent power brake system assembly.

      • Tightening torque: 18±2 N•m

Installation

Caution

  • Ensure that the IPB system assembly will not be impacted during installation, and that the mounting gasket of the IPB system assembly and the mounting surface of the firewall are clean and free of foreign matters.

  • After ensuring that the positions of the components of the IPB system assembly are correct, insert the four studs into the mounting holes, manually tighten the mounting nuts and wait until the position is relatively stable before applying torque.

  • When connecting the thrust rod with the brake pedal, avoid pushing the thrust rod from the side.

  • Do not pull up the pedal after the thrust rod is connected with the brake pedal to avoid damage to the internal structural parts.

  • Install the brake pipeline correctly, and do not interchange brake pipes.

  1. Handle the IPB system. Refer to Handling of IPB System Assembly 

    Caution

    Strictly follow this step during installation or handling.

  2. Connect the IPB thrust rod with the brake pedal. After installation, the angle of the thrust rod shall be less than 3° in all directions.

    Caution

    • Do not pull up the pedal after the IPB thrust rod is connected with the brake pedal to avoid damage to the internal structural parts of IPB.

    • Avoid pushing the IPB thrust rod from the side during connection.

  3. Install it in the reverse order of removal.

  4. Fill brake fluid. Refer to Replacement of Brake Fluid 

  5. After installation is completed, the brake system needs to be bled. Refer to Air Discharge of Braking System 

  6. After installation, perform model configuration writing for the IPB system and calibrate the sensor. Refer to Intelligent Power Braking System Model Configuration Write and Sensor Calibration
