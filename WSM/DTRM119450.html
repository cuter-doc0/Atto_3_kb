Inspection of Front compartment cover lock body assembly

Check

  1. Open the front compartment cover. assembly,Check the front compartment cover lock and its fasteners.If damaged,replace it. See Front Compartment Cover Lock Body Assembly - Removal and Installation 

    • Tightening torque: 10±1N•m

  2. When performing maintenance, lubricant should be applied above the front hood latch to reduce friction on the latch and avoid the risks of inadvertent operation and abnormal noise.

  3. Apply lubricant to the latch of the front hood lock using a brush, repeating two to three times.

    Caution

    • Note that lubricant should not be applied to the grille cover.

    • When applying the lubricant with a brush, be gentle to avoid rotating or shifting the latch.

    • After completing the operation, check the position of the latch, or pull the opening handle twice to ensure that the latch is in the normal position, and then close the front hood.

    • If the lubricant is accidentally wiped off or insufficient during inspection, it should be replenished in time.

    Reminder

    • Use the lubricant available at the repair shop, prioritizing the lubricant for the limiter maintenance.

    • Alternatively, the lubricant for sunroof or window lifter maintenance can be used.

    • The lubricant is in a paste form, with no visible flow or dripping after application.

    • Other recommended greases:

      Grease_MP

      Grease_AROSTA-471

  4. Check the position of the latch. If the latch is in the locked position, pull the opening handle twice.
