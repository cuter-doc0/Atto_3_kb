Precautions

Caution

The power battery is a dangerous high voltage product. The maintenance personnel shall pay attention to the following precautions during removal and installation:

  • The connection points indicated with yellow line or parts with high-voltage signs may only be dismantled by personnel without professional training.

  • When the power battery is removed, the socket should be covered with insulation protection.

  • Power battery output socket shall be insulated to protect it from foreign objects which may cause the electric shock;

  • During the removal, pay attention that the sampling wire shall not be pulled violently and bent excessively to prevent the signal wire from being damaged;

  • Pay attention to the identification of parts during removal to avoid omission or incorrect installation;

  • During the removal and installation, the power battery shall be prevented from the violent removal, dropping, collision, module tilting, artificial short circuit and other abnormal operations; it can only be removed by non-professional workers.

  • The power battery is a high voltage device, where improper operation is easy to cause casualties. For the removal and installation procedure and the precautions, see the removal and installation specifications.

  • Tighten the screws with professional tools according to the designed torque; the bolts must be marked for fastening after installation.
