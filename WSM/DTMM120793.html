Precautions

Caution

Important notes on electronic component:

  • When removing electronic parts, prevent the same from falling and resulting in parts damage.

  • Avoid touching the cone-shaped paper basin of the speaker.

  • Replace the speaker with the same specification.

The reception of satellite signals will be affected when the vehicle is driving in the following environments:

  • Driving in rainy days or tall buildings.

  • Driving in tunnels, underground parking lots, overpasses, viaducts or boulevards.

  • There are metal objects above the vehicle.

Poor antenna grounding connection will affect normal reception of satellite signals.

  • Poor antenna grounding connection will result in noise when receiving radio information. Replacing the antenna still cannot effectively solve this problem.
