Inspection of Wheel Fixing Nut Torque

Repair tools and equipment required

  • Torque wrench (60-340 N•m)

Steps

Reminder

Before unscrewing or tightening the wheel nuts, be sure to take out their protective trim covers.

  1. Take out the protective trim cover of wheel nuts with a removal clamp for wheel nut cap.

  2. Check and tighten the wheel nuts with a torque wrench with the specified torque in a cross manner.

    • Tightening torque: 120±5 N•m

    Caution

    Never use an impact wrench to tighten the wheel nuts!

  3. Obtain a new work order for replacement.
