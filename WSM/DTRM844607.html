Inspection of high-voltage battery SOC

Inspection and determination of SOC

There is a high-voltage battery SOC indicator bar on the instrument panel cluster display screen.

When the SOC indicator bar turns red, it indicates that the high-voltage battery is low.

Reminder

It is recommended to charge the vehicle in time when the SOC indicator bar turns red. Charging after the battery runs out is not recommended, because it will affect the service life of the high-voltage battery.

Reminder

  • If the high-voltage battery is found to be low during routine maintenance, remind the customer that the vehicle needs to be charged.
