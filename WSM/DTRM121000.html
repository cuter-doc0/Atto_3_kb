Front Compartment Wiring Harness - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Remove the front subframe and power assembly. See Front Subframe and Power Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the left front wheel mudguard assembly. See Left Front Wheel Mudguard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove the right front wheel mudguard assembly. See Right Front Wheel Mudguard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  4. Remove the front bumper assembly. See Front Bumper Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  5. Remove the front compartment distribution box body. See Front Compartment Distribution Box Body - Removal and Installation 

  6. Remove the battery cooling plate heat exchanger assembly. See Battery Cooling Plate Heat Exchanger Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  7. Remove the integrated thermal management module. See Integrated Thermal Management Module - Removal and Installation 

  8. Remove compressor suction pipe assembly 1. See Compressor Suction Pipe Assembly 1 - Removal and Installation 

  9. Remove the refrigeration tube assembly 1. See Refrigeration Tube Assembly 1 - Removal and Installation 

  10. Remove the refrigeration tube assembly 5. See Refrigeration Tube Assembly 5 - Removal and Installation 

  11. Remove the refrigeration tube assembly 4. See Refrigeration Tube Assembly 4 - Removal and Installation 

  12. Remove the refrigeration tube assembly 3. See Refrigeration Tube Assembly 3 - Removal and Installation 

  13. Remove compressor discharge pipe assembly 2. See Compressor Discharge Pipe Assembly 2 - Removal and Installation 

  14. Remove the right front brake tube assembly. See Right Front Brake Tube Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  15. Remove the left front brake tube assembly. See Left Front Brake Tube Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  16. Remove the left rear brake tube I assembly. See Left Rear Brake Tube I Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  17. Remove the right rear brake tube I assembly. See Right Rear Brake Tube I Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  18. Remove the lower bracket of the battery. See Lower Bracket of Battery - Removal and Installation 

  19. Remove the ventilation cover assembly. See Ventilation Cover Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  20. Remove the brake fluid reservoir assembly. See Brake Fluid Reservoir Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  21. Remove the brake fluid reservoir mounting bracket assembly. See Brake Fluid Reservoir Mounting Bracket Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  22. Remove the console body assembly. See Console Body Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  23. Remove the instrument panel lower body assembly. See Instrument Panel Lower Body Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  24. Remove 49 connectors from the front compartment wiring harness.

  25. Remove 12 ground bolts from the front compartment wiring harness.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  26. Remove the front compartment wiring harness.

    1. Remove 81 fixing clips.

    2. Remove 2 through-hole rubber sleeve.

Installation

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
