Power Battery Assembly - Removal and Installation

Warning

  • When repairing the high voltage system (the high voltage system harnesses are identified by orange on the vehicle), wear insulation protection equipment, including insulating gloves, insulating shoes and masks, and power down the high voltage system of the vehicle.

  • To avoid the personal injury, only the professionals may remove the power battery.

  • Do not touch or operate the power battery without appropriate protective equipment.

  • Please switch the vehicle power supply to OFF position before operation.

  • Remove the battery according to the process.

  • During the removal, pay attention to the high-voltage warning signs on the power battery and the vehicle.

  • During the removal, do not damage the parts with locking function.

  • During the removal, properly protect the power battery.

Removal

  1. Adjust the two front seat assemblies to the most forward position.

  2. Power off the low voltage electrical system. See Power-off and Power-on of Low Voltage Electrical System 

  3. Power off the high voltage electrical system. See Power-off and Power-on of High Voltage Electrical System 

  4. Discharge the refrigerant. See Recovery and Filling of Refrigerant 

  5. Remove the front motor lower mudguard assembly. See Front Motor Lower Mudguard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  6. Remove the front subframe rear crash bar. See Front Subframe Rear Crash Bar - Removal and Installation 

  7. Remove the rear floor mudguard. See Rear Floor Mudguard - Removal and Installation 

  8. Remove the rear floor left mudguard. See Rear Floor Left Mudguard - Removal and Installation 

  9. Remove the rear floor right mudguard. See Rear Floor Right Mudguard - Removal and Installation 

  10. Press the tab, and turn the lock pin to the unlocked position.

  11. Disconnect the connector of low voltage.

  12. Disconnect 2 connectors of front compartment wiring harness.

  13. Remove 1 fixing bolt, and detach the refrigeration tube assembly 1, the compressor suction pipe assembly 4 and the power battery assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

    Caution

    • Remove the A/C pipeline involving the O-rings. Install new O-rings after each removal. Apply compressor lubricating oil to the new O-rings before installation.

    • After removing the components of the A/C system, all components need to be sealed to prevent foreign matters such as dust and impurities from entering the A/C system and causing abnormalities.

  14. Remove 2 fixing bolts of high voltage distribution wiring harness assembly bracket.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  15. Remove 2 fixing bolts,and detach the connection between high voltage distribution wiring harness sub-assembly and power battery assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 5±0.5N•m

    Caution
    After the high voltage components are removed, they shall be sealed and insulated to prevent dust and impurities from entering or accidentally touching the high voltage terminals.

  16. Remove 1 fixing bolt, and take out the ground wiring harness of battery pack.

    • Tightening torque: 18±2N•m

  17. Support the power battery assembly with a lifting device with a lifting platform.

    1. Place the lifting device of the lifting platform under the power battery assembly.

    2. Slowly raise the lifting platform, and ensure that the power battery assembly is firmly supported.

    3. Never make the lifting platform cover the fixing bolts of the power battery assembly.

    Warning

    The power battery assembly must be firmly supported to prevent personal injury and damage to the power battery assembly due to its falling.

    Caution
    Place soft support materials (such as thick wooden frame and rubber pad) on the lifting equipment of the lifting platform to prevent damage to the power battery assembly caused by the lifting platform.

  18. Remove 2 carpet plugs under the left/right front seats with a plastic crow plate.

  19. Remove 2 fixing bolts of power battery assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 60±5N•m

  20. Remove 12 fixing bolts, adjust the power battery dismantling tool, and slowly lower the power battery assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 135±5N•m

    Caution

    • When removing the power battery assembly, more than one people is needed to cooperate with each other. When lowering the power battery assembly, observe whether there is interference with the body.

    • After removing the power battery assembly, seal all connectors to prevent the dust and impurities from entering and causing pollution, blockage or the risk of electric shock when touching the high voltage connector by mistake.

Installation

Caution
Pay attention to standardized operation when installing the lifting power battery assembly. Slowly lift the power battery assembly and observe whether there is interference with the body. Pay attention to align the mounting hole of the adjusting bolt with the mounting hole of the vehicle body. Screw in 14 fixing bolts in sequence and tighten them according to the specified torque.

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.

  2. After the installation, fill in the refrigerant. See Recovery and Filling of Refrigerant 

  3. Power on the vehicle high voltage system. See Power-off and Power-on of High Voltage Electrical System 

  4. Conduct the calibration process of standard mileage operating condition. See Calibration of standard mileage condition
