System Function Principle

The main functions of suspension: to mitigate the impact of rugged road on wheels, determine the position of wheels and axles, and improve the grip of wheels on the road. The suspension has a certain influence on the comfort and handling stability of the vehicle. The rear suspension of this vehicle adopts a multi-link independent suspension. Its advantage is that the left and right wheels bounce separately, which can reduce the inclination and vibration of the vehicle body, reduce the impact on the vehicle body, and improve the comfort of the rear seats.
