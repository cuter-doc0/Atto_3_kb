Right Front Door Guard Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Power off the low voltage electrical system. See Power-off and Power-on of Low Voltage Electrical System 

  2. Remove the right front window control panel assembly. See Front Right Window Control Panel Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove the right front door handle box cover. See Right Front Door Handle Box Cover Plate- Removal and Installation 

  4. Remove 1 fixing bolt from the right front door guard assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 5±0.5N•m

  5. Turn 45 ° counterclockwise, and take out the right front door snap handle ambient light.

  6. Remove 2 fixing bolt from the right front door guard assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 5±0.5N•m

  7. Pry up the right front door guard assembly.

  8. Disconnect 1 connector of the right front tweeter horn

  9. Disconnect 1 connector of the right front door ambient light assembly.

  10. Disconnect 1 connector of the right front door snap handle ambient light assembly.

  11. Disconnect 2 connections of door lock cable.

  12. Disconnect 1 connector of the left door light.

  13. Disconnect 1 connector of Electric tailgate opening switch

  14. Take out the right front door guard assembly.

  15. Remove the right front door snap handle ambient light assembly. See Right Front Door Snap Handle Ambient Light Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  16. Remove the left door light (door). See Left Door Light (Door) - Removal and Installation 

  17. Remove the electric tailgate opening switch. See Electric Tailgate Opening Switch - Removal and Installation 

Installation

Caution

  • Before installation, it is necessary to check whether all the clips are missing or damaged, and if so, install new clips, and then install the trim panel;

  • After installation, check whether all clips are installed in place. The installation position of the trim panel may deviate, resulting in warping or abnormal gaps.

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
