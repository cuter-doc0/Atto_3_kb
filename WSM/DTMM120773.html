System Description

  1. The power window motor applies the latest processes, technologies and materials. For example, in terms of waterproof, it adopts the fully sealed design, and the breathable film is used (when the high temperature caused by the motor operation, it is easy to cause the pressure difference inside and outside the motor and break the seal at the weakest part. At this time, the motor needs a breathing organ to balance the pressure difference. The breathable film plays this role, which balances the pressure difference through gas, and block the liquid from flowing into the motor, so that the motor can truly be full sealed and water proof).

  2. For motor overheat protection, polymer PTC overcurrent protector is adopted, which can quickly and effectively protect the motor from burning due to external faults (such as switch fault).

  3. For motor noise treatment, special design is applied on the motor rotating shaft, and high wear-resistant damping materials are used to ensure that the motor will not produce metal impact and friction noise when rotating at high speed.

  4. For motor power wiring, the terminal interface is adopted, and the power lead wiring is removed, so as to avoid poor contact caused by the lead wire.

  5. The motor adopts DC double pole permanent magnet structure, two-way rotation, and is internally equipped with overheat protection device. External circuit protection is not required. After the motor is powered on, due to the effect of magnetic field force, the motor rotates, and then decelerates through a large turbine reducer to obtain low-speed and large torque on the output gear. When the motor is stuck or the circuit fails, the overheat protection device will cut off the power supply to protect the motor.

System Function Principle

The power window system raises and lowers the windows via the switch on the door trim panel. The driver may operate the main switch on the window regulator switch trim panel to control each window. The power window locking switch is located in the driver’s side door trim panel. The driver can use it to disable all passenger window switches. The power window system can work only when the start button is set to OK. The automatic window lowering feature can automatically lower the driver’s side window to the bottom. Press the window switch on the driver's side until it reaches the second gear. The window will start to lower automatically. At this time, press or pull up the switch will make the window stop, and the automatic window lowering action is canceled. The automatic window raising feature can automatically raise the driver’s side window. Pull up the window switch on the driver's side until it reaches the second gear. The window will start to raise automatically. At this time, press or pull up the switch will make the window stop, and the automatic window raising action is canceled.

Some functions and characteristics of the power window system depend on its electronic modules, which are integrated into the right front window regulator switch assembly.

The power window system components are as follows:

  • Window regulator switch

  • Window regulator motor

  • Left body control module

  • Right body control module

Power window delay function:

When the front door is closed, within 10 minutes after switching the vehicle power supply from OK to OFF, the window control switch still works, and the switch backlight goes up. You may control the glass to rise and fall. Once any front door is opened, the delay function fails.
