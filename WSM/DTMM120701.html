Combination meter assembly

System Function Principle

The combined instrument system is a multi-functional display 5-inch LCD instrument located in front of the driver and on the upper part of the steering column.

The combination instrument applies LCD, which can provide the necessary visibility. Each indicator of the combination instrument is displayed through the LCD. The connecting circuit connects the combination instrument to the electrical system of the vehicle. The connecting circuit is integrated in the vehicle harness, routed in different positions, and fixed in many different ways.

Meters:

Name

Description

Speedometer

Based on the wheel speed sensor, ABS converts the wheel speed signal into vehicle speed signal and transmits the data to the combination instrument through CAN.

Power meter

The power meter calculates according to the power of the battery manager. The combination instrument calculates the power by collecting the total voltage and current sent by the power battery management module on the CAN, and determines the positive and negative.

Electricity meter

The combination instrument collects the CAN information of the power battery management module and displays the battery capacity.

The instrument collects the turn signal of the combination switch through hard wire.

High beam indicator

When the combination instrument receives the CAN message that the high beam is "on", this indicator will turn on. When receiving the CAN message that the high beam is "off", this indicator will go out. The indicator and the high beam work synchronously.

Main warning light

Receive and prompt fault information (except backlight adjustment, door and trunk state information).

Charging system fault warning light

The CAN line transmits the DC and charging system fault signals, and the combination instrument controls to the indicator to turn on.

Low battery indicator

CAN communication transmission. The power battery management module sends the low battery pack alarm signal to the combination instrument. Instrument CPU controls the indicator to turn on, which is synchronized with the electricity meter entering into the red area.

Power battery charging connection indicator

Hard wire transmission. When the charge sensitive switch is closed, the instrument turns on the indicator. When the charging induction switch is disconnected, this indicator goes out.

Motor overheat warning light

CAN communication transmission. The motor control module sends the power motor over temperature alarm signal to the combination instrument, and combination instrument CPU commands to turn on the indicator.

Powertrain fault warning light

When the fault signals of battery manager and M2 motor control module are collected by CAN communication, CPU commands to turn on the indicator.

OK indicator

The M2 motor control module sends the "OK” indicator on signal to the combination instrument through CAN, and the combination instrument CPU commands to turn on the indicator.
