Inspection of Hood Lock Body Assembly

Inspection

Reminder

During maintenance, apply grease to the latch plate of hood, so as to reduce friction at the latch plate and avoid risks such as accidental touch and abnormal sound.

  1. Apply grease to the latch plate of hood lock with a brush for two or three times.

    Caution

    • Note that grease shall not be applied to the grille upper cover.

    • Apply grease gently with a brush to avoid rotation and displacement of the latch plate.

    • After operation, check the latch plate position or pull the release handle twice to ensure that the latch plate is in the normal position, and then close the hood.

    • Reapply grease if it is accidentally wiped off or insufficient application is found during inspection.

    Reminder

    • Use the existing grease in the repair shop, and give priority to the grease for stopper maintenance.

    • The grease for sunroof or window regulator maintenance can also be used.

    • The grease is pasty, so there is no visible flow or dripping after application.

    • Other recommended greases:

      Grease_MP

      Grease_AROSTA-471

  2. Check that the latch plate is in the open position. If it is in the locked position, pull the release handle twice to ensure that it is in the open position.

  3. Obtain a new work order for repair or replacement.
