Front Compartment Thermal Insulation - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Power off the low voltage electrical system. See Power-off and Power-on of Low Voltage Electrical System 

  2. Power off the high voltage electrical system. See Power-off and power-on of high voltage electrical system 

  3. Remove the front subframe and power assembly. See Front Subframe and Power Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  4. Remove the integrated thermal management module. See Integrated Thermal Management Module - Removal and Installation 

  5. Remove the intelligent power brake system assembly. See IPB System Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  6. Remove compressor suction pipe assembly 4. See Compressor Suction Pipe Assembly 4 - Removal and Installation 

  7. Remove the refrigeration tube assembly 1. See Refrigeration Tube Assembly 1 - Removal and Installation 

  8. Remove the refrigeration tube assembly 5. See Refrigeration Tube Assembly 5 - Removal and Installation 

  9. Remove the refrigeration tube assembly 4. See Refrigeration Tube Assembly 4 - Removal and Installation 

  10. Remove the refrigeration tube assembly 3. See Refrigeration Tube Assembly 3 - Removal and Installation 

  11. Remove compressor discharge pipe assembly 1. See Compressor Discharge Pipe Assembly 1 - Removal and Installation 

  12. Remove compressor discharge pipe assembly 2. See Compressor Discharge Pipe Assembly 2 - Removal and Installation 

  13. Remove the integrated thermal management module bracket 1. See Integrated Thermal Management Module Bracket 1 - Removal and Installation 

  14. Remove the right front brake tube assembly. See Right Front Brake Tube Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  15. Remove the left front brake tube assembly. See Left Front Brake Tube Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  16. Remove the left rear brake tube I assembly. See Left Rear Brake Tube I Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  17. Remove the right rear brake tube I assembly. See Right Rear Brake Tube I Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  18. Detach the front compartment wiring harness from the power battery assembly.

    1. Remove 1 fixing clip.

    2. Pull down the connector locking bracket to detach the front compartment wiring harness assembly from the power battery assembly.

  19. Remove 4 fixing clips from the front compartment wiring harness.

  20. Remove 4 fixing clips, and put aside the front compartment wiring harness.

  21. Remove 7 fixing clip, and take out the front compartment thermal insulation.

Installation

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
