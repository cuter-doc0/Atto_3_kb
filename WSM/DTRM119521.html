Drive Motor Control Assembly Maintenance Kit - Removal and Installation

Removal

Caution
After removing maintenance kit of drive motor control module assembly,it need to be cleared anti-theft matching data if it used on other car model or exchanged control module assembly.Never forget to match again after the installation. See Anti-Theft Matching of Drive Motor Control Module Assembly 

  1. Remove the front motor lower mudguard assembly. See Front Motor Lower Mudguard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Discharge coolant. See Change of Coolant 

  3. Recover the refrigerant See Recovery and Filling of Refrigerant 

  4. Power off the low voltage electrical system. See Power-off and Power-on of Low Voltage Electrical System 

  5. Power off the high voltage electrical system. See Power-off and Power-on of High Voltage Electrical System 

  6. Remove the integrated thermal management module. See Thermal management Integration module - Removal and Installation 

  7. Remove the right front wheel mudguard assembly. See Fright Front Wheel Mudguard Assembly - Removal and Installation .

  8. Remove the front drive electric assembly package III. See Front Drive Electric Assembly Package III - Removal and Installation 

  9. Remove drive motor control module access cover.

    1. Remove 5 fixed bolt.

      • Tightening torque: 3.5±0.3N•m

    2. Remove 2 fixing bolts, and take out the drive motor control module access cover.

      • Tightening torque: 3.5±0.3N•m

  10. Remove 4 fixing bolts from the integrated AC/DC charging vehicle socket sub-assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  11. Remove one fixing bolt, and detach the integrated AC/DC charging vehicle socket sub-assembly from the drive motor control module assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  12. Disconnect one high voltage connector of the integrated AC/DC charging vehicle socket sub-assembly.

  13. Detach the positive insulating cover.

  14. Remove one fixing nut, and detach the positive power line of the front compartment wiring harness from the drive motor control module assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  15. Detach 3 fixing clips from the front compartment wiring harness.

  16. Disconnect 4 low voltage connectors, and take out the front compartment wiring harness.

  17. Remove one fixing bolt, and detach the compressor suction pipe assembly 1 from the electric compressor assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

    Caution
    To replace the air conditioner pipeline involves the O-ring of the component, you need to replace the O-ring with a new one every time.

  18. Remove one fixing bolt of the electric compressor assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 22±2N•m

  19. Remove one fixing bolt, and detach the compressor discharge pipe assembly 1 from the electric compressor assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

    Caution
    To replace the air conditioner pipeline involves the O-ring of the component, you need to replace the O-ring with a new one every time.

  20. Remove one fixing bolt, and take out the ground wiring harness of main motor control module housing.

    • Tightening torque: 18±2N•m

  21. Loosen one fixing clamp, and detach the cooling pipe assembly 5 from the inlet pipe tee.

  22. Detach one fixing clip of cooling pipe assembly 5.

  23. Loosen one fixing clamp, and detach the motor coolant pump inlet pipe assembly from the inlet pipe tee.

  24. Loosen one fixing clamp, and detach the cooling pipe 1 from the inlet pipe tee.

  25. Loosen 2 fixing clamps,and take out the cooling pipe assembly.

  26. Remove the wiring harness.

    1. Disconnect one connector.

    2. Detach 2 fixing clips, and move away the wiring harness.

  27. Remove 2 fixing bolts, and move away the electric coolant pump assembly I.

    • Tightening torque: 10±1N•m

  28. Remove the electric compressor assembly.

    1. Disconnect one connector of motor compressor.

    2. Remove 3 fixing bolts, and take out the electric compressor assembly.

      • Tightening torque: 22±2N•m

  29. Remove left cover of the integrated intelligent front drive assembly.

    1. Remove 9 fixed bolt.

      • Tightening torque: 6N • m

    2. Remove one stud bolt and take out the left cover of the integrated intelligent front drive assembly.

      • Tightening torque: 6N • m

    Caution

    • Uncover the front drive electric assembly package II side.

    • Each time the left cover of the integrated intelligent front drive assembly is removed, the operation shall be standardized. The bolts should be removed from the outside to the inside.

    • To remove the motor rear cover, you may use a scraper tilted at a 25° angle from top to bottom. Press it down gently to avoid damaging the sealing surface.

  30. To disconnect the rotary transformer connector of the drive motor control module assembly maintenance kit, you may use a small slotted screwdriver.

    Caution

    Never touch the rotary transformer components and temperature sensing plug-ins to avoid damaging them.

  31. Remove 3 fixing bolts of the three-phase high voltage wiring harness of integrated intelligent front drive assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 11.5N • m

  32. Remove 6 fixing bolts and take out the drive motor control module assembly maintenance kit.

    • Tightening torque: 35±5N•m

Installation

  1. Replace the gasket after each removal.

  2. Install the drive motor control module onto the motor slowly. Fit the surface in place. Organize the motor three-phase wire connection plate to the position corresponding to the copper plate of the electric control three-phase wire. Align the bolt holes of the three-phase wire copper bar.

  3. Install the fastening bolts of the drive motor control module. First, pre-tighten bolt 6# as shown in the figure. Then, pre-tighten the remaining 5 bolts into the corresponding bolt holes in the order of 1 to 5 as shown in the figure. Finally, apply torque to the bolts.

    • Tightening torque: 35±5N•m

  4. Tighten the fixing bolts of the three-phase wire copper bar.

    • Tightening torque: 11.5N • m

  5. Install the rotary transformer connector.

    Caution

    • To ensure that the connectors are properly assembled, follow these steps: insert the connector until it clicks and then try to unplug it.

    • When pulling out the wire, gently pull the sheath and never damage the root of the wiring harness.

  6. Operation before installing the motor rear cover.

    1. Use a scraper to remove the sealing adhesive from the rear cover and then clean it with a chemical cleaning agent.

    2. Use a scraper to remove the sealing adhesive from the rear box of the motor and then clean it with a chemical cleaning agent.

    Caution

    It is recommended to use sealing adhesive (TONSAN/1596F sealant) and cleaning agent (TONSAN/1755EF).

  7. Clean the junction surface of the box and the motor rear cover with a dust-free cloth. Check for any scratches and place the motor rear cover flat on the table. Use a manual glue gun to apply adhesive on the inside of the bolt holes.

    Caution

    • Apply the adhesive evenly.

    • The aperture of the glue gun nozzle is required to be 3mm-5mm.

  8. Install the motor rear cover. Pre-tighten the bolts in the order shown in the figure, and then tighten them in the order shown in the figure.

    • Tightening torque: 6N • m

    Caution

    10# is the mounting position for the stud bolt.

  9. Follow the removal steps in reverse order to install.
