Operating Tips

Picture Color and Application

Color

Description

Purple: fastener, connector or retainer

Blue: target or main component

Green: first substitute part

Brown: second substitute part

Gray: surrounding item

Light blue: special tool, equipment or common tool

Yellow: part movement, disconnection, adjustment or highlight

Orange: high voltage harness (if the connector is the description object, the connector will be purple)

  • Blue: target or main component to be removed/installed (disassembled/assembled)

  • Green/Brown: components to be removed before or installed after the target/main component. The same applies to disassembly/assembly before/after the target/main components.

  • Yellow: just leave the parts in the vehicle. And highlight the areas to be checked or adjusted.

  • Purple: electrical connectors and fasteners (such as nuts, bolts, clamps or clips) to be separated, connected, loosened, tightened, moved, removed and installed.

  • Light blue: special tool, equipment or common tool used in an unusual way.

  • Gray: applicable to all surrounding items.

  • White: picture background color.
