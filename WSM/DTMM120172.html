Precautions

Caution

  • When removing the trim panel, wear gloves so as to avoid injuries.

  • Remove the trim panel with plastic or equivalent material tools.

  • Be careful when removing the trim panel to avoid scratching the surface material.

  • Do not apply excessive force when removing the trim panel, so as to prevent damage to other components.

  • When removing the spoiler assembly, check and conform the internal connectors are released, so as to prevent the connectors and the harness from being damaged due to violent pulling.

  • Before installation, check whether all clips are missing or damaged, if any, install new clips and install the trim panel.

  • After installation, check whether all clips are installed in place. The installation position of the trim panel may deviate, resulting in warping or abnormal gaps.
