Precautions

Caution

  • Wear gloves to protect your hands.

  • When it is necessary to work in the vehicle, the seat and other parts shall be covered to avoid oil contamination.

  • The interior trim crow plate shall be used to remove the interior trim. If there is no interior trim crow plate, it can also be replaced by a screwdriver or equivalent, but it shall be wrapped.

  • Since most interior trims are fixed by clips, ensure that all clips have been pried out before removing the interior trims.

  • When installing parts, check whether the fixing clips of parts are missing. If so, add acceptable fixing clips.

  • Check and replace all damaged clips.

  • Place trims in a clean and flat position to avoid damaging the surface.

  • Before installing the door guard, check whether all clips are lost or damaged, and replace with new ones if any. After installation, it should fit in place with the door assembly.

  • Thoroughly inspect the parts before repair or replacement.

  • The fastener must be checked in specified torque after installing.

  • When removing and installing the rear door assembly, use an assistant to prevent the rear door assembly from falling suddenly, causing personal injury and vehicle damage.

  • Wear long sleeved overalls and protective gloves to avoid being cut by the sheet metal parts when removing and installing the interior sheet metal parts of the door.

  • After overhauling the door lock, ensure that the door can be closed only when the door lock is open. Otherwise, the lock may be damaged.

  • Ensure that the door frame trim strip and weather strip are properly matched, and that the door frame trim strip and sheet metal are correctly matched.

  • When removing the door glass, lift the glass to the maintenance position in advance and then power off the low voltage system to facilitate the removal and installation of the fixing bolt.

  • After the door hinge is removed, the position of the door assembly should be adjusted to prevent movement interference from damaging the door.
