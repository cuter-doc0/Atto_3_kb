Rear Wash Line Sub-assembly III - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Remove the left C-pillar upper guard assembly. See Left C-Pillar Upper Guard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the right C-pillar upper guard assembly. See Right C-Pillar Upper Guard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove the tailgate upper guard assembly. See Tailgate Upper Guard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  4. Lift partial ceiling.

  5. Detach the rear wash line sub-assembly III from the rear wash line sub-assembly Ⅱ.

  6. Detach the rear wash line sub-assembly III from the rear wash line sub-assembly IV, and take it out.

Installation

Caution

  • When the installation is complete, add the washing fluid to the proper position.

  • When the outdoor temperature is below 0 °C, it is prohibited to fill running water or mineral water.

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
