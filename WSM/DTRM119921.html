Adjustment of the Brake Pedal and the Brake Light Switch

Brake Pedal Switch Clearance

  1. Adjust the brake light switch clearance.

    1. Rotate anticlockwise and take out the brake light switch a.

    2. Place a 1.5mm thick plate on top of the brake light switch limit b.

    3. Insert the brake light switch into the mounting bracket c, and keep the contact of the brake light switch fully pressed, turn clockwise and tighten the brake light switch, and pull out the plate.

    4. After the installation, press down and release the brake pedal several times. Measure and confirm the brake light switch clearance (i.e., the brake light switch contact exposure length) after the brake pedal returned to the home position ) is within 1.5 mm ~2.5mm. Otherwise, adjust the clearance.

    • d clearance: 1.5-2.5mm

Free Stroke of Pedal

  1. Power off the vehicle and wait for 6 min, push the pedal by hand to check the free stroke a at b position of the pedal. If the free stroke fails to meet the technical requirements, adjust the brake pedal position switch c. Insufficient free travel of brake pedal may result in brake dragging.

    • Free stroke: 1-5mm
