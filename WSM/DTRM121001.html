Instrument Panel Wiring Harness - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Remove the instrument panel lower body assembly. See Instrument Panel Lower Body Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the evaporator box air vent transition duct assembly. See Evaporator Box Air Vent Transition Duct Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  3. Disconnect 15 connectors of the host assembly.

  4. Disconnect the instrument panel wiring harness on the left.

    1. Disconnect 5 connectors.

    2. Disconnect one connector,and detach the fixing clip.

  5. Disconnect one connector of interior&exterior damper control motor.

  6. Detach one wiring harness clip of the left instrument panel wiring harness of heat pump HVAC assembly.

  7. Detach the wiring harness of left instrument panel wiring harness of heat pump HVAC assembly.

    1. Disconnect 2 connectors.

    2. Detach one wiring harness clips.

  8. Disconnect 2 connectors of the right instrument panel wiring harness of heat pump HVAC assembly.

  9. Disconnect 4 connectors, and detach the wiring harness bracket from the right body control module.

  10. Disconnect one connector between the instrument panel wiring harness and PM2.5 quick tester assembly.

  11. Detach 2 fixing clips of the electric power steering column and universal joint assembly.

  12. Disconnect the instrument panel wiring harness on the right.

    1. Disconnect 1 connectors.

    2. Disconnect one connector,and detach the fixing clip.

  13. Remove 4 fixing bolts of pipe beam welding assembly middle.

    • Tightening torque: 6N • m

  14. Remove one fixing bolt of pipe beam welding assembly rear.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  15. Remove one fixing bolt of Instrument fuse.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  16. Remove the connecting and fixing bolts of the central wiring harness of instrument panel wiring harness.

    1. Disconnect 2 connectors.

    2. Disconnect 1 connectors. Detach the fixing clips.

    3. Detach 9 fixing clips.

    4. Remove one fixing bolt.

      • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  17. Detach 27 fixing clips in the front of the instrument panel wiring harness.

  18. Take out the instrument panel wiring harness.

Installation

  1. Follow the removal steps in reverse order to install.
