Visual inspection of brake device

Caution

Any defects found must be eliminated and the customer informed.

Check the following components for leakage and damage:

  • IPB system assembly

  • Brake caliper

  • Brake pipeline

  • There shall be a dust cap on the brake fluid exhaust valve.

Caution

  • Do not twist the brake hose.

  • Note that the brake hose cannot contact other components of the vehicle at the maximum steering angle.

Inspection contents:

  • Check whether the brake pipeline is free of deformation, scratch, brittle rupture, or fracture.

  • Check whether the brake pipeline is free of visible rust.

  • Check whether the brake system hoses are free of aging or cracks.

  • Check whether the brake pipeline interfaces and fixtures are installed in the correct position without leakage or rust.

  • Check whether the brake pipeline and brake hose are installed in the correct position and fixed firmly on the bracket.

Obtain a new work order for repair or replacement.
