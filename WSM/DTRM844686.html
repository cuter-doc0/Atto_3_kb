Inspection of Chassis Fixing Bolts and Nuts

Reminder

The inspection methods for the left and right chassis fixing bolts and nuts are the same.

Warning

Operate carefully and do not tighten bolts or bolt-nut combinations related to wheel alignment at will.

Repair tools and equipment required

  • Torque wrench (60-340 N•m)

  1. Check the matching marks of fixing bolts and nuts of the front suspension system. In case of any misalignment, re-tighten the bolts or nuts with standard torque or replace them if necessary.

    Bolts or nuts

    Name

    Tightening torque (N•m)

    1

    Fixing bolts of front subframe assembly and body

    200±10

    2

    Front fixing bolts for connecting front lower control arm assembly and front subframe assembly

    200±10

    3

    Rear fixing bolts and nuts for connecting front lower control arm assembly and front subframe assembly

    200±10

    4

    Fixing bolts of front subframe assembly and body

    200±10

    5

    Fixing bolts and nuts between front shock absorber strut assembly and front steering knuckle

    200±10

    6

    Fixing nuts between front shock absorber strut assembly and stabilizer bar tie rod and ball joint assembly

    90±5

    7

    Fixing nuts between stabilizer bar tie rod and ball joint assembly and front stabilizer bar assembly

    90±5

  2. Check the matching marks of fixing bolts and nuts of the rear suspension system. In case of any misalignment, re-tighten the bolts or nuts with standard torque or replace them if necessary.

    Warning

    Operate carefully and do not tighten bolts or bolt-nut combinations related to wheel alignment at will.

    • For example: eccentric bolt-nut combinations 1 and 2.

  3. Obtain a new work order for replacement.
