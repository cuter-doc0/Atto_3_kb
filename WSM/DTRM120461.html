Front Compartment Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Remove the front washing line and nozzle assembly I. See Front Wash Line and Nozzle Assembly I - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the front wash line and nozzle assembly II. See Front Wash Line and Nozzle Assembly II - Removal and Installation 

  3. Detach the front wash line sub-assembly I from the front compartment cover assembly.

    1. Detach the 2 pipe clamps.

    2. Detach the front wash line sub-assembly I from the pipeline clamp.

  4. Remove 4 fixing nuts, and take out the front compartment cover assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 28±2N•m

    Caution
    When removing the front compartment cover assembly, use assistance to prevent it from slipping off during the removal of the fixing nuts, causing injury to maintenance personnel and objects.

Installation

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.

  2. Adjust the front compartment cover assembly. See Adjustment of Front Compartment Cover Assembly
