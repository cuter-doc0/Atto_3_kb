Left Front Door Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Remove the left front door limit block. See Front Door Limit Block - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the left front door skirt panel assembly. See Left Front Door Skirt Panel Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove the left front door weather strip. See Left Front Door Weather Strip - Removal and Installation 

  4. Remove the left front door woofer speaker. See Front Door Woofer Speaker - Removal and Installation 

  5. Remove the left front B-pillar exterior trim panel assembly. See Left Front B-Pillar Exterior Trim Panel Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  6. Remove the left front door window runchannel assembly. See Left Front Door Window Mud Tank Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  7. Remove the left front window regulator bracket assembly. See Left Front Window Regulator Bracket Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  8. Remove the left front door stopper assembly. See Front Door Stopper Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  9. Remove the left front door lock body assembly. See Left Front Door Lock Body Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  10. Remove the left front door outer handle assembly. See Left Front Door Outer Handle Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  11. Remove the left front door wiring harness. See Left Front Door Wiring Harness - Removal and Installation 

  12. Remove 4 fixing bolts, and take out the left front door assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 28±2N•m

    Caution
    For the removal of the front door assembly, several technicians shall work together.

Installation

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.

  2. Adjust the left front door assembly after the installation. See Adjustment of Front Door Assembly
