System Function Principle

The lane departure warning system includes: forward collision warning (FCW), autonomous emergency braking (AEB), adaptive cruise control (ACC), lane keeping assist (LKA), traffic sign recognition (TSR), lane departure warning (LDW), blind spot detection (BSD), high beam assist (HMA).

Forward collision warning (FCW)/autonomous emergency braking (AEB)

can adapt to a variety of emergency road conditions, issue early warning, help to realize brake gain and emergency braking, and reduce the risk.

  • When a pedestrian/cyclist moves in front of the vehicle, forward collision warning (FCW) will reminds the driver to control the steering according to the actual situation, and autonomous emergency braking (AEB) will be triggered according to the risk and determine the braking gain and automatic braking.

  • When the vehicle drives into a corner, where there is a moving cyclist in front of the vehicle, forward collision warning (FCW) and autonomous emergency braking (AEB) will be triggered.

  • At an intersection, a low speed vehicle appearing in front of the subject vehicle will trigger forward collision warning (FCW) and emergency braking (AEB).

  • In case of oncoming vehicles, forward collision warning (FCW) and autonomous emergency braking (AEB) will be triggered.

Lane keeping assist (LKA)/lane departure warning (LDW)/blind spot detection (BSD)

Automatically follow the forward vehicle, conduct lane centering control, avoid pressing the line or occupying other lane, and ensure driving safety.

  • Lane keeping assist (LKA):60km/h <vehicle speed <150km/h, when the vehicle is about to deviate from the lane, the instrument will give a correction prompt, the steering wheel vibrates, and automatically correct the vehicle back into the lane.

  • Lane departure warning (LDW):60km/h <vehicle speed <150km/h, when the vehicle is about to deviate from the lane, the instrument will give a correction prompt and automatically correct the vehicle back into the lane.

  • Blind spot detection (BSD): the 77G(HZ) millimeter wave radar installed at the rear of the vehicle detects the blind spot around the vehicle, which helps the driver to understand the surrounding situation and reduce the risk.

Adaptive cruise control (ACC)/traffic sign recognition (TSR)/high beam assist (HMA)

Highway driving, assist driving, obey traffic rules and drive safely.

  • Adaptive cruise control (ACC): The full speed adaptive cruise system, with a setting range of 30~150km/h and an operating range of 0~1km/h, can be activated from standstill, and realize adaptive endurance, follow-up and vehicle parking control.

  • Traffic sign recognition (TSR): identify the road speed limit signs through the camera sensor. The speed limit indicator icon will turn on to remind the driver, so as to avoid overspeed.

  • High beam assist (HMA): When the headlight switch is in AUTO and the speed is greater than 30km/h, HMA can be activated. The system automatically recognizes the surrounding environment, switches between high and low beams, no longer makes the high beam dazzling, and improves the safety of the subject vehicle and the oncoming vehicle.
