Integrated Intelligent Front Drive Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Remove the front motor lower mudguard assembly. See Front Motor Lower Mudguard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Discharge coolant. See Change of Coolant 

  3. Recover the refrigerant See Recovery and Filling of Refrigerant 

  4. Remove the front compartment cover assembly. SeeFront Compartment Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  5. Remove the battery assembly. See Battery Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  6. Remove the lower bracket of the battery. See Lower Bracket of Battery - Removal and Installation 

  7. Power off the high voltage electrical system. See Power-off and Power-on of High Voltage Electrical System 

  8. Remove the left front wheel. See Wheel Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  9. Remove the right front wheel. See Wheel Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  10. Remove the high voltage distribution wiring harness sub-assembly. See High Voltage Distribution Wiring Harness Sub-assembly - Removal and Installation 

  11. Remove the right front wheel mudguard assembly. See Fright Front Wheel Mudguard Assembly - Removal and Installation .

  12. Remove the left front drive half shaft assembly. See Left Front Drive Half Shaft Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  13. Remove the right front drive half shaft assembly. See Right Front Drive Half Shaft Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  14. Remove the front drive electric assembly package III. See Front Drive Electric Assembly Package III - Removal and Installation 

  15. Remove the motor radiator assembly I. SeeMotor Radiator Assembly I - Removal and Installation 

  16. Remove the condenser assembly. SeeCondenser Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  17. Remove the front module assembly. SeeFront Module Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  18. Remove the front bumper assembly. SeeFront Bumper Beam Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  19. Remove the integrated thermal management module. AttendanceIntegrated Thermal Management Module - Removal and Installation 

  20. Remove drive motor control module access cover.

    1. Remove 5 fixed bolts.

      • Tightening torque: 3.5±0.3N•m

    2. Remove 2 fixing bolts, and take out the drive motor control module access cover.

      • Tightening torque: 3.5±0.3N•m

  21. Remove 4 fixing bolts from the integrated AC/DC charging vehicle socket sub-assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  22. Remove one fixing bolt, and detach the integrated AC/DC charging vehicle socket sub-assembly from the drive motor control module assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  23. Disconnect one high voltage connector of the integrated AC/DC charging vehicle socket sub-assembly.

  24. Detach the positive insulating cover.

  25. Remove one fixing nut, and detach the positive power line of the front compartment wiring harness from the drive motor control module assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  26. Detach 3 fixing clips from the front compartment wiring harness.

  27. Disconnect 4 low voltage connectors, and take out the front compartment wiring harness.

  28. Remove one fixing bolt, and detach the compressor suction pipe assembly 1 from the electric compressor assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

    Caution
    To replace the air conditioner pipeline involves the O-ring of the component, you need to replace the O-ring with a new one every time.

  29. Remove compressor discharge pipe assembly 1. SeeCompressor Discharge Pipe Assembly 1 - Removal and Installation 

  30. Remove one fixing bolt, and take out the ground wiring harness of front motor assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 18±2N•m

  31. Remove one fixing bolt, and take out the ground wiring harness of main motor control module housing.

    • Tightening torque: 18±2N•m

  32. Loosen one fixing clamp, and detach the cooling pipe assembly 5 from the inlet pipe tee.

  33. Detach one fixing clip of cooling pipe assembly 5.

  34. Loosen one fixing clamp, and detach the motor coolant pump inlet pipe assembly from the inlet pipe tee.

  35. Loosen one fixing clamp, and detach the cooling pipe 1 from the inlet pipe tee.

  36. Loosen one fixing bolt,detach the connection between cooling pipe assembly 2 and integrated intelligent front drive motor control module assembly.

  37. Remove the wiring harness.

    1. Disconnect one connector.

    2. Detach 2 fixing clips, and move away the wiring harness.

  38. Remove 2 fixing bolts, and move away the electric coolant pump assembly I.

    • Tightening torque: 10±1N•m

  39. Remove the electric compressor assembly.

    1. Disconnect one high voltage connector of motor compressor.

    2. Remove 3 fixing bolts, and take out the electric compressor assembly.

      • Tightening torque: 22±2N•m

  40. Install lifting equipment at two lifting ear positions.

  41. Remove the powertrain rear mount bracket assembly.

    1. Remove one fixing bolt.

      • Tightening torque: 85±5N•m

    2. Remove 3 fixing bolts, and take out the powertrain rear mount bracket assembly.

      • Tightening torque: 85±5N•m

  42. Remove the powertrain left mount support assembly.

    1. Remove one fixing bolt.

      • Tightening torque: 85±5N•m

    2. Remove 3 fixing bolts, and take out the powertrain left mount support assembly.

      • Tightening torque: 85±5N•m

  43. Remove the powertrain right mount support assembly.

    1. Remove one fixing bolt.

      • Tightening torque: 85±5N•m

    2. Remove 3 fixing bolts, and take out the powertrain right mount support assembly.

      • Tightening torque: 85±5N•m

  44. Using equipment to take out Integrated Intelligent Front Drive Assembly carefully.

    Caution

    When removing the integrated intelligent front drive assembly using lifting equipment, pay attention to whether there is any interference with the body components and prevent falling.

Installation

  1. Follow the removal steps in reverse order to install.

  2. After the installation, fill in the coolant. See Change of Coolant 

  3. After the installation, fill in the refrigerant. See Recovery and Filling of Refrigerant 

Caution
When replacing the integrated intelligent front drive assembly is required, VDS shall be used for vehicle calibration and matching. Motor Zero Calibration of Integrated Intelligent Front Drive Assembly
