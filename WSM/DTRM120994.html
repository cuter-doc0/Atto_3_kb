Combination Switch - Removal and Installation

Removal

Caution
After removing the combination switch, fix the clock spring with a tie to prevent damage caused by the rotation of the clock spring. If it is rotated, adjust it back to the original position as required.

  1. Remove the steering wheel assembly. See Steering Wheel Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the combination switch lower guard plate. See Combination Switch Lower Guard Plate - Removal and Installation 

  3. Disconnect 2 connectors of combination switch.

  4. Disconnect 1 connector of combination switch.

  5. Remove one fixing screw, and take out the combination switch.

    • Tightening torque: 6N • m

Installation

  1. Follow the removal steps in reverse order to install.
