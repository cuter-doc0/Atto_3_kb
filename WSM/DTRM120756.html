Write of Left Body Control Module

Write Process

Caution
After replacing the left body control module, VDS shall be used to execute write calibration.

  1. Set the vehicle power supply to OK, and apply the parking brake.

  2. Use a VDS to connect the vehicle OBD interface. Refer to the VDS Instructions

    Reminder
    OBD interface is located at right lower corner of right lower guard plate of instrument panel.

  3. Select “Vehicle Diagnostic System”.

  4. Select “Passenger Vehicles”.

  5. Select “EV system”.

  6. Select the corresponding model and variant.

  7. Select “Part Replacement Center”

  8. Click “Part Replacement with body Control Key” in the “Part Replacement Center” interface.

  9. Operate according to the prompt guide in the “Part Replacement with Body Control Key” interface.
