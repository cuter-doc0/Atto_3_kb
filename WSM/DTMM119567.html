High voltage distribution harness sub-assembly

System Function Principle

New energy vehicles high-voltage harness is a key component of high voltage electrical system, which provides a guarantee for the reliable and safe operation of new energy vehicles. It carries connection between the internal and external wiring harness of electric vehicles, distributes power through the power distribution box, transmits electric energy efficiently with high quality, shields external signal interference and other functions. It is the neural network of the high-voltage system of new energy vehicles, connecting all high-voltage electronic components and transmitting power and data, which is extremely important for new energy vehicles.

High current: new energy vehicles high voltage harness as the main energy transmission channel, to withstand high current, DC bus rated current can reach more than 200 A.

Sealing: Due to the high voltage and high current characteristics of the high voltage harness, there are also high requirements for the sealing of the harness, which generally requires water and dust resistance tests and air tightness tests. If the sealing is not good, the moisture or water ingress occurs, resulting in extremely rapid ageing or damage to the wire and connection parts. If the sealing performance of the connector part is poor, the insulation resistance will be reduced and the insulation fault will be reported.

Heat resistance: because the high- voltage harness undergoes the high current for a long time, with high power and Joule effect of a lot of heat, the high voltage harness wire temperature rating generally reaches 125 °C (150 °C) and the terminal temperature generally reaches 140 °C.
