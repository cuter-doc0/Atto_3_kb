Precautions for Inspection and Maintenance of Supplemental Restraint System

  1. When operating airbag components (including indirect operation related to airbags), conduct the following steps:

    1. After disconnecting the negative pole of the low voltage battery, wait for at least 90s.

    2. When removing or installing parts or maintaining sheet metal, never impact or vibrate airbag components, especially the impact sensors.

    3. In order to avoid heating damage during spraying, remove SRS electronic control unit, airbag module, clock spring, impact sensor and seat belt with pre tightening function.

    Caution

    • The supplemental restraint system can supply sufficient voltage to enable the development of the airbag after the negative pole of the low voltage battery is disconnected. When the maintenance of the supplemental restraint system is carried out within 90s after disconnection of the low voltage battery, the supplemental restraint system may be activated and the airbag may be deployed, resulting in serious accidents.

    • Ensure that the specified special tools and test equipment are used.

    • Place the removed airbag components in a clean and dry area. Place the airbag module on a flat surface with its front face upward. Never place any objects on it.

    • Never repair airbag components (SRS electronic control unit, airbag module, clock spring and impact sensor).

    • After replacing airbag components, check whether the warning light works normally.
