Right Curtain Airbag Module - Removal and Installation

Removal

Warning

  • The safety protection system (SRS) can still work for a period of time after the power is disconnected. Wait at least 3 min before disassembling or removing any SRS component.

  • Ensure that the vehicle electrical system is completely powered off and that no other power is connected.

  • The following procedure specifies the critical maintenance steps required for the proper operation of the SRS in the event of a collision. Observe all precautions and steps carefully. Failure to follow the step-by-step instructions may result in incorrect operation of the SRS and increase the risk of serious personal injury or death during a crash.

  1. Remove the right front safety handle bracket. See Front Safety Hand Grab Bracket - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the right rear safety handle bracket. See Front Safety Hand Grab Bracket - Removal and Installation 

  3. Disconnect one connector of the right curtain airbag module.

  4. Remove 11 fixing bolts, and take out the right side curtain airbag module.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

Installation

Warning

  • Failure to follow the following precautions when installing airbags may result in improper airbag deployment and increase the risk of serious injury or death in a crash.

  • Never attempt to disassemble or maintain the airbag. If any abnormality occurs, replace the airbag with a new one.

  1. Follow the removal steps in reverse order to install.
