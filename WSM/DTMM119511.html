System Function Principle

The integrated intelligent front drive assembly is mainly composed of the following components:

  • Drive motor control assembly repair kit

  • Front drive motor and transmission assembly repair kit

  • Differential Oil Seal

The integrated intelligent front drive assembly is connected to its power battery group through a high-voltage wire. It is the main executive mechanism of the vehicle. Its characteristics determine the main indicators of the vehicle and directly affect the power, economy and driving experience of the vehicle.

Eight-in-one integrated intelligent front drive assembly is a newly developed pure electric integrated intelligent front drive assembly, which integrates motor, transmission, motor control module, PDU, DC-DC, Bi-OBC, VCU, BMS. The high integration of the function module system can improve the space utilization, decrease the cost and reduce the weight.

Function introduction of each component inside the integrated intelligent front drive assembly:

  • The function of the drive motor control unit (MCU) is to control the energy transmission between the power battery and the drive motor.

  • The function of two-way on-board charger (OBC) is to convert the AC input from the external charging equipment into DC and adjust the voltage to meet the charging demand of the power battery pack, and convert the high voltage DC of the battery pack into AC for the use of the load equipment.

  • The function of DC-DC is to convert high voltage DC into low voltage DC to supply power to the low voltage load and battery of the vehicle.

  • The function of the high voltage power distribution unit (PDU) is to connect the power grid and electrical loads into a high voltage circuit through copper bars, contactor, fuses and other devices, supply the high voltage DC power of the power battery to the high voltage electrical appliances of the vehicle, and receive the DC power of on-board charger or off board charger to charge the power battery.

  • The function of the drive motor is to convert the electrical energy provided by the drive motor control module into mechanical energy and output it to the transmission, and convert the mechanical energy input by the transmission into electrical energy and output it to the drive motor control module for energy recovery.

  • The function of the single gear transmission is to reduce the speed and increase the torque of the power motor.

  • Power Domain Control Module (VBM) includes: vehicle control unit, and battery manager (some functions).

  1. The vehicle control module has the functions of real-time power calculation and power distribution, real-time information interaction and centralized processing, sensor signal acquisition and processing, and CAN communication, fault processing, online CAN flash, VDS flash, cooperating with other modules to complete the vehicle's work requirements and self-test.

  2. The battery management system has high voltage interlock detection, hardwire collision detection and DC charging connection confirmation, and exchange information with the charging pile through the DC charging subnet, and also have the function of communicating with the BASU.
