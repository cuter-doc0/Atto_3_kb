Integrated AC/DC Charging Vehicle Socket Sub-Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

Danger
For the maintenance of high pressure system, (on the vehicle, wiring harnesses of the high voltage system is in orange). Make sure to wear protective equipment, including insulation gloves, shoes and masks, and make sure to power off the high voltage system before carrying out the maintenance.

  1. Power off the low voltage electrical system. See Power-off and Power-on of Low Voltage Electrical System 

  2. Power off the high voltage electrical system. See Power-off and Power-on of High Voltage Electrical System 

  3. Remove the right front wheel assembly. See Wheel Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  4. Remove the right front wheel mudguard assembly. See Fright Front Wheel Mudguard Assembly - Removal and Installation .

  5. Remove the AC/DC charging port cover assembly I. See AC/DC Charging Port Cover Assembly I - Removal and Installation 

  6. Remove the front drive electric assembly package III. See Front Drive Electric Assembly Package III - Removal and Installation 

  7. Remove drive motor control module access cover.

    1. Remove 5 fixing bolts.

      • Tightening torque: 3.5±0.3N•m

    2. Remove 2 fixing bolts, and take out the drive motor control module access cover.

      • Tightening torque: 3.5±0.3N•m

  8. Remove 4 fixing bolts of the integrated AC/DC charging vehicle socket sub-assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  9. Remove one fixing bolt, and detach the integrated AC/DC charging vehicle socket sub-assembly from the drive motor control module assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  10. Disconnect one high voltage connector of the integrated AC/DC charging vehicle socket sub-assembly.

  11. Detach 2 fixing clips from the integrated AC/DC charging vehicle socket sub-assembly.

  12. Remove 4 fixing nuts from the integrated AC/DC charging vehicle socket sub-assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  13. Remove 2 fixing nuts from the integrated AC/DC charging vehicle socket sub-assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 6±1N•m

  14. Detach the wiring harness between Integrated AC/DC Charging Vehicle Socket Sub-assembly and front compartment wiring harness.

    1. Disconnect one low voltage connector.

    2. Detach one fixing clip,and move away Integrated AC/DC Charging Vehicle Socket Sub-assembly.

  15. Detach the wiring harness between Integrated AC/DC Charging Vehicle Socket Sub-assembly and vehicle body.

    1. Remove 2 AC charging ground bolts.

      • Tightening torque: 22±2N•m

    2. Detach one fixing clip,and move away Integrated AC/DC Charging Vehicle Socket Sub-assembly.

  16. Remove 4 fixing bolts, and take out the integrated AC/DC charging vehicle socket sub-assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

Installation

  1. Follow the removal steps in reverse order to install.
