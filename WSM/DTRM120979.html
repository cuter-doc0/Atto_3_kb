Battery Cooling Plate Heat Exchanger Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

Caution
After removing pipeline connections, properly seal all pipe fittings as soon as possible to prevent dust, impurities, and foreign objects from entering the A/C system and causing system faults.

  1. Recover the refrigerant See Recovery and Filling of Refrigerant 

  2. Power off the low voltage electrical system. See Power-off and Power-on of Low Voltage Electrical System 

  3. Discharge coolant. See Replacement of Coolant 

  4. Detach the wiring harness of battery cooling plate heat exchanger assembly .

    1. Disconnect one connectors.

    2. Detach one wiring harness clips.

  5. Remove 2 fixing bolts, and detach the refrigeration tube assembly 5 from the battery cooling plate heat exchanger assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  6. Remove 2 fixing bolts, and move away Electric three-way water valve.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  7. Loosen 3 fixing clamps, and detach the cooling pipe assembly 5, the plate heat exchanger inlet pipe assembly and the plate heat exchanger outlet pipe assembly from the battery cooling plate heat exchanger assembly.

  8. Remove 3 fixing bolts, and take out the battery cooling plate heat exchanger assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 22±2N•m

Installation

Caution

  • After installation, check the pipeline joints to make sure there is no leakage. If any, the inspection and repair shall be carried out again.

  • For the installation, all “O-rings” of the pipeline must be replaced, and refrigerant oil shall be applied on the surface of the seal rings and the seal rings shall be connected according to the stipulated torque.

  1. Follow the removal steps in reverse order to install. To replace the battery cooling plate heat exchanger assembly, replenish refrigerant oil when filling refrigerant.
