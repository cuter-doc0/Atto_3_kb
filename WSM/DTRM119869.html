Front Brake Pin and Lock Bolt Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

Reminder
The removal and installation of the left and right front brake pins and lock bolt components are the same. The left side is taken as an example.

  1. Remove the left front wheel assembly. See Wheel Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Detach the left front brake caliper body sub-assembly.

    1. Remove 2 fixing bolt of the left front brake caliper sub-assembly.

      • Tightening torque: 35±3N•m

    2. Detach the left front brake caliper body sub-assembly from the brake disc.

      Caution
      After detaching from the brake disc, use a tie to fix the brake caliper body sub-assembly to the vehicle body to prevent damage to the brake hose.

  3. Take out the front brake pin and lock bolt assembly.

Installation

Caution

  • Check whether the front brake pin dust boot is damaged before installation, and replace with a new front brake pin if damaged.

  • Before installing the front brake pin, apply grease to the front brake pin and the front bracket.

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
