Precautions

Caution

  • In the event cracks are identified on the windshield or tailgate glass while the windshield and tailgate remains in one piece, apply protective tapes on the glass diagonally, so as to avoid further damage or personal injury.

  • Wear goggles and gloves when handling any glass with sharp edges or burrs to reduce the risk of injury.

  • When cutting the glass sealant, never scratch the body paint. Damage to the paint will affect the bonding effect.

  • When moving the glass, handle it gently and place it properly to prevent damage to the glass.

  • The gluing shall be uniform and continuous, where the gluing section is preferably be trapezoidal or triangular.

  • The glue shall be applied continuously along the frame to prevent excessive overflow.

  • When removing the glass assembly, use the glass sucker to remove it.

  • After the glass assembly is installed, wait until the glass cement is completely cured before driving.
