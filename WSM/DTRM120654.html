Right Rear Door Lock Body Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Remove the right rear door rear movable guide rail and mud tank assembly. See Right Rear Door Rear Movable Guide Rail and Mud Tank Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Detach one fixing clip, and detach the right rear door lock outward-opening lever from the right rear door lock body assembly.

  3. Remove 3 fixing bolts of the right rear door lock body assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 6±1N•m

  4. Disconnect one connector, and take out the right rear door lock body assembly.

    Caution
    To take out the rear door lock body assembly, the rubber plug connecting the rear door lock body assembly and the door shall be detached first.

Installation

  1. Follow the removal steps in reverse order to install.

    Caution

    After the installation, check the functionality of the rear door lock body assembly.
