System Function Principle

The bumper beam is used to absorb the collision energy in case of collision. It consists of main beam, energy absorption box and mounting plate connecting the vehicle. Both the main beam and the energy absorption box can effectively absorb the collision energy in the event of low-speed collision, thus minimizing the damage to the longitudinal beam of the vehicle body under the impact force and protecting the vehicle. The bumper beam is mainly used to reduce the maintenance cost in case of medium- and low-speed collisions, and can reduce the harm to the driver and passengers to a certain extent.
