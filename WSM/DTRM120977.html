Plate heat exchanger Outlet Pipe Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Discharge coolant. See Replacement of Coolant 

  2. Detach the plate heat exchanger outlet pipe assembly from the three-way water valve body assembly.

    1. Loosen 1 fixing clamp, and detach the plate heat exchanger outlet pipe assembly from the three-way water valve body assembly.

    2. Detach the 1 pipe clamps.

  3. Loosen 1 fixing clamp, detach the plate heat exchanger outlet pipe assembly from the battery cooling plate heat exchanger assembly, and take it out.

Installation

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.

Caution
After installation, check the pipeline joints to make sure there is no leakage. If any, the inspection and repair shall be carried out again.
