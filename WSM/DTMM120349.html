System Function Principle

Vehicle insulation gets the advantages of flame retardant, non-toxic, corrosion resistance, small unit weight, low thermal conductivity, strong chemical stability, low moisture absorption rate, and good water addition. It is currently recognized as the most superior thermal insulation and sound absorbing material, with a wide range of uses.
