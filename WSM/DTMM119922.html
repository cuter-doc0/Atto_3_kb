Precautions

Caution

  • This vehicle is equipped with a supplemental restraint system (SRS), including front double airbags, side airbags and side air curtains. If the operation is not carried out in the correct order, the airbag may be accidentally deployed during the maintenance process, and serious accidents may be caused. Before maintenance (including removal or installation, inspection or replacement of parts), read the precautions for supplemental restraint system.

  • The electric power steering system of this vehicle supports active return control and angle/speed sending out. If any part of the steering system (including electric power steering column assembly, intermediate shaft assembly, mechanical steering gear assembly, EPS electronic control unit assembly, etc.) is replaced, conduct four-wheel alignment, calibrate the steering column angle signal (this vehicle has no SAS angle sensor, so it is not necessary to calibrate the SAS sensor angle signal), and clear the DTCs. If the steering column is replaced, enter the corresponding vehicle interface according to the model, write the corresponding configuration word, calibrate and clear the DTCs. If the vehicle supports remote control driving, this function is prohibited when the angle signal is being calibrated. Serious abnormal situations such as motor burn may occur. When using VDS for calibration, keep your hands off the steering wheel, and the steering wheel shall not be affected by external forces. Serious abnormal situations such as different left and right steering forces and motor burn may occur. For configuration word writing, enter the vehicle interface corresponding to VDS based on the vehicle model, and select the corresponding configuration word. If the configuration word is selected incorrectly, some functions may not match, or the feel may not match.

  • Never hit the steering column assembly, especially the EPS motor and reduction mechanism. If the steering column assembly falls or suffers severe impact, replace it with a new assembly.

  • When moving the steering column assembly, never pull the harness.

  • Never loosen the angle adjustment handle when the steering column assembly is not installed on the vehicle.

  • Before disconnecting the steering column intermediate shaft from the steering gear, keep the wheels dead ahead and fix the steering wheel to prevent rotation. The clock spring on the steering column may deviate from the center position, and even be damaged due to too many turns of steering wheel.

  • Before disconnecting the steering column or intermediate shaft, power off the vehicle. Never move the wheels after disconnecting the above components. Disobey these procedures will result in inaccurate positioning of some components during installation.

  • The duration of turning the steering wheel to the limit position shall not exceed 5s in daily driving. The power assisted motor may be damaged.
