Tyre pressure monitoring module

System overview

  1. A module installed inside the tire to measure tire pressure and transmit information wirelessly.

  2. It is installed at and integrated with the tire valve, which is divided into the monitoring module part and the valve part.

The tyre pressure monitoring system uses the pressure sensor installed in each tyre to measure the tyre pressure, acceleration and other parameters. Through radio frequency transmission, the collected data is sent to the tyre pressure monitoring control module (integrated in the left body control module), which demodulates, analyzes and processes the radio frequency data into CAN signals, and sends the CAN signals to the instrument for display. In such way, the tyre related information is digitally displayed in real time. When the pressure of any tyre exceeds the threshold, the system will make sound, light and vision alarms to remind the driver so as to ensure driving safety.
