Unlocking Webbing of Right Rear Backrest - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Remove the plastic guard of the webbing. See Webbing Plastic Guard - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the rear headrest height adjustment sliding sleeve assembly. See Sliding Sleeve Assembly of Rear Seat Headrest Height Adjustment - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove the rear headrest sliding sleeve assembly. See Rear Headrest Slide Cover Components - Removal and Installation 

  4. Remove the right rear sheet metal bracket. See Right Rear Sheet Metal Bracket - Removal and Installation 

  5. Remove 5 fixing clips, and take out the right rear backrest plate.

  6. Take out the right rear backrest foam.

  7. Remove 1 fixing snap ring from the unlocking webbing of the right rear backrest.

  8. Remove 1 fixing pin from the unlocking webbing of the right rear backrest.

  9. Take out the unlocking webbing of the right rear backrest.

Install

  1. The installation is performed in reverse order of removal.
