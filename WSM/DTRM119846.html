Front Brake Disc - Removal and Installation

Removal

Reminder
The removal and installation of the left and right front brake discs are the same. Take the left front brake disc as an example.

  1. Remove the left front wheel assembly. See Wheel Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Detach the left front brake caliper assembly.

    1. Remove 2 fixing bolts from the left front brake caliper assembly.

      • Tightening torque: 130±10N•m

    2. Detach the left front brake caliper assembly from the brake disc.

      Caution
      After disengaging from the brake disc, fix the brake caliper assembly to the body with a tie to prevent damage to the hose.

  3. Remove 2 fixing bolts, and take out the front brake disc.

    • Tightening torque: 10±1N•m

Installation

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
