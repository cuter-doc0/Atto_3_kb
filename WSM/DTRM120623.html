Front Seat Far-end Right Airbag Module- Removal and Installation

Removal

Reminder
The removal and installation methods of the left and right front airbag module are the same. Take the right front airbag module as an example.
Warning

  • The safety protection system (SRS) can still work for a period of time after the power is disconnected. Wait at least 3 min before disassembling or removing any SRS component.

  • Ensure that the vehicle electrical system is completely powered off and that no other power is connected.

  • Never expose SRS parts to high temperature or open fire. In case of SRS part falling off, cracks, dents or other defects, replace with new parts.

  1. Remove the plastic part 2 at the headrest. See Plastic Part 2 at Headrest - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the driver seat right side guard. See Driver Seat Right Guard - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove the driver seat left side guard. See Driver Seat Left Side Guard - Removal and Installation 

  4. Detach the seat wiring harness from the seat.

    1. Disconnect 1 connector.

    2. Pry up 12 wiring harness clips, and move away the seat wiring harness.

  5. Untie 2 backrest brace webs.

  6. Remove 2 fixing bolts from the left side of the backrest frame and cushion frame.

    • Tightening torque: 45±5N•m

  7. Remove 2 fixing bolts from the right side of the backrest frame and cushion frame, and take out the backrest assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 45±5N•m

  8. Remove 11 fixing clips at the headrest.

  9. Remove 6 metal clips, and take out the seat backrest foaming mask assembly.

    Reminder
    The metal seat buckle is onetime component,it need to be replace with a new one each time.

  10. Remove the front seat far-end right airbag module.

    1. Pry up 2 fixing clips.

    2. Remove 2 fixing nuts, and take out the front seat far-end right airbag module.

      • Tightening torque: 6±1N•m

Installation

  1. Follow the removal steps in reverse order to install.
