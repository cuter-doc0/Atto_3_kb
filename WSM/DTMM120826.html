System Function Principle

When the vehicle is running at a low speed, the engine sound simulator will give a prompt sound to pedestrians nearby.

Forward travel

  • In case of 0km/h ＜ V ≤ 20km/h, the prompt sound increases with the speed.

  • if 20km/h﹤V≤30km/h, the acoustic warning will become weaker with the increase of the speed.

  • The volume of the notification sound decreases as the vehicle speed exceeds 30km/h.

Reverse travel

A continuous uniform acoustic wanning will be issued.
