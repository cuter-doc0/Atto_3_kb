Rear Right Steering Knuckle Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Remove the rear right brake mudguard. Refer to Right Rear Brake Mudguard - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the rear suspension right trailing arm assembly. Refer to Rear Suspension Right Towing Arm Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove 1 fixing bolt, and put aside the floor wiring harness.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1 N•m

  4. Remove 1 fixing nut/bolt from the rear right steering knuckle and the rear right shock absorber assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 200±10 N•m

  5. Remove 1 fixing nut/bolt from the rear right steering knuckle and the upper right arm assembly of the rear suspension.

    • Tightening torque: 200±10 N•m

  6. Remove 1 fixing nut/bolt between the rear right suspension lower arm assembly and the rear right steering knuckle.

    • Tightening torque: 200±10 N•m

  7. Remove the rear right steering knuckle.

    1. Remove 1 fixing nut.

      • Tightening torque: 90±5 N•m

    2. Detach the rear right suspension control arm assembly, and take out the rear right knuckle.

Installation

  1. Install it in the reverse order of removal.

  2. Implement four-wheel alignment after installing the rear steering knuckle. Refer to Four-Wheel Alignment
