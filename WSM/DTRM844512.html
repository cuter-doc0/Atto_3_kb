Inspection of Tire Pressure

Caution

For the sake of driving safety, all wheels must be equipped with tires with the same model, size (rolling circumference) and tread pattern as those in the factory status.

  • The tire pressure shall never be lower than the specified value.

  • If it is lower than the specified value, the tire deformation will be multiplied, which will accelerate crack and layer fracture of the tire and increase the risk of accidents.

Reminder

  • The tire pressure label is pasted under the B-pillar lock ring on the driver's side.

  • Note that the tire inflation pressure listed on the label is applicable to cold tires. Do not reduce the increased tire pressure when the tire is hot.

Repair tools and equipment required

  • Tire pressure gauge

  1. Inquire the corresponding tire pressure in the tire pressure label according to the tire model.

  2. Check the tire pressure with a tire pressure gauge and adjust it if necessary.

  3. Check the pressure of spare tire (if equipped) and adjust it if necessary.

    Caution

    • For non-full size spare tire, please adjust the pressure according to that on the spare tire label.

  4. Obtain a new work order for repair or replacement.
