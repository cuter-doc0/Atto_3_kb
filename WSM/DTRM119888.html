Rear Brake Pad Assembly Aftersales Package - Removal and Installation

Reminder
The removal and installation of the left and right rear brake pad assemblies are the same. Take the left as an example.

Removal

  1. Connect the vehicle with VDS and enter the rear brake "Maintenance Mode Release".

  2. Remove the left rear wheel assembly. See Wheel Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove 2 fixing bolts, take out the left rear brake caliper sub-assembly aftersales package.

    • Tightening torque: 30±2N•m

    Caution
    After taking out the rear brake caliper sub-assembly aftersales package, in case the brake pipeline is pulled and damaged, fix it on the vehicle body with a tie.

  4. Take out the rear brake pad assembly aftersales package.

    Caution
    After taking out the rear brake pad assembly aftersales package, replace the rear brake pad return bayonet pin on the brake caliper bracket as well.

Installation

  1. Caution
    After replacing the rear brake pad assembly aftersales package, reset the piston of the rear brake caliper sub-assembly with a reset tool.

    The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
