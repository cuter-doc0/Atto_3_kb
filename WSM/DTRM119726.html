Rear Subframe Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Remove the left rear steering knuckle. See Left Rear Steering Knuckle - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the right rear steering knuckle. See Rear Right Steering Knuckle Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove the left/right rear stabilizer bar tie rod and ball joint assemblies. See Rear Stabilizer Bar Tie Rod and Ball Joint Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  4. Remove the rear stabilizer bar fitting assembly. See Rear Stabilizer Bar Fitting Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  5. Remove the left/right rear shock absorber assembly. See Rear Shock Absorber Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  6. Remove the left/right rear suspension lower arm assembly. See Rear Suspension Lower Arm Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  7. Remove the rear suspension left/right upper arm assembly. See Rear Suspension Upper Left Arm Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  8. Remove the left/right rear suspension control arm assembly. See Rear Suspension Control Arm Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  9. Remove the rear suspension left towing arm assembly. See Rear Suspension Left Towing Arm Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  10. Remove the rear suspension right towing arm assembly. See Rear Suspension Right Towing Arm Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  11. Remove the rear floor mudguard. See Rear Floor Mudguard - Removal and Installation 

  12. Remove the left/right lower mudguard bracket assembly. See Lower Mudguard Bracket Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  13. Remove the left/right rear floor mudguard bracket assembly. See Rear Floor Mudguard Bracket Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  14. Remove 4 fixing nuts, and take out the rear subframe assembly with the equipment.

    • Tightening torque: 200±10N•m

    Caution

    • Before removing the rear subframe assembly, use lifting device to support the lower part of the rear subframe assembly.

    • When removing the rear sub-frame assembly, multiple technicians are required to cooperate with each other to adjust the lifting device, slowly lower the rear sub-frame assembly, and check whether the rear sub-frame assembly interferes with other parts of the vehicle body.

Installation

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
