Cooling Pipe Assembly 4 - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Discharge coolant. See Replacement of Coolant 

  2. Remove the cooling pipe assembly 4.

    1. Loosen 1 fixing clamp, and detach the cooling pipe assembly 4 from the expansion tank assembly I.

    2. Pry up a hold-open catch, and take out the cooling pipe assembly 4.

Installation

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.

Caution
After installation, check the pipeline joints to make sure there is no leakage. If any, the inspection and repair shall be carried out again.
