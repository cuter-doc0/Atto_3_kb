Carpet Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Remove the accelerator pedal bracket assembly. See Accelerator Pedal Bracket Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the console rear mounting bracket assembly. See Console Rear Mounting Bracket Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove the driver seat assembly. See Driver Seat Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  4. Remove the front passenger seat assembly. See Front Passenger Seat Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  5. Remove the left B-pillar lower guard assembly. See Left B-Pillar Lower Guard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  6. Remove the right B-pillar lower guard assembly. See Right B-Pillar Lower Guard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  7. Remove the left rear side wall guard assembly. See Left Rear Side Wall Guard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  8. Remove the right rear side wall guard assembly. See Right Rear Side Wall Guard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  9. Remove 11 fixing clips from the carpet assembly.

  10. Remove 2 hook and loop fasteners from the front of the carpet assembly.

  11. Put aside the floor wiring harness, and take out the carpet assembly.

Installation

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
