Regular Maintenance Information

Regular Maintenance Information (Every 20,000 km or 12 Months After the First Maintenance)

Vehicle maintenance shall be based on the mileage and months, whichever comes first.

Reminder

If a fault is found during maintenance, it must be eliminated and the customer informed.

Ask whether the customer requires:

  • new wiper blade

  • filling of windshield washer fluid
