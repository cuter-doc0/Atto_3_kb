Ceiling Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Remove the left A-pillar upper guard assembly. See Left A-Pillar Upper Guard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the right A-pillar upper guard assembly. See Right A-Pillar Upper Guard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove the left B-pillar upper guard assembly. See Left B-pillar Upper Guard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  4. Remove the right B-pillar upper guard assembly. See Right B-pillar Lower Guard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  5. Remove the left C-pillar upper guard assembly. See Left C-pillar Upper Guard Assembly - Removal and Installation .

  6. Remove the right C-pillar upper guard assembly. See Right C-Pillar Upper Guard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  7. Remove the left sun visor assembly. see Left Sun Visor Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  8. Remove the right sun visor assembly. see Right Sun Visor Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  9. Remove the sun visor fixing bracket. see Sun Visor Fixing Bracket - Removal and Installation 

  10. Remove the roof handle assembly. see Roof Handle Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  11. Remove the left rear side interior light assembly. see Left Rear Side Interior Light Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  12. Remove the right rear side interior light assembly. see Left Rear Side Interior Light Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  13. Remove the left door light (vanity mirror). see Left Door Light (Vanity Mirror Light) - Removal and Installation 

  14. Remove the front interior light assembly. see Front Interior Dome Light Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  15. Remove 2 fixing bolts from the front of the ceiling assembly

    • Tightening torque: 6±0.5N•m

  16. Pry down the ceiling assembly, and take it out from the tailgate.

    Caution
    When taking out the ceiling assembly, never bend the assembly, and place the rear ceiling assembly properly at an appropriate place.

Installation

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
