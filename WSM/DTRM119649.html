Front Subframe Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

Caution
Before removing the front subframe assembly, hoist the powertrain with the equipment. Taking out the front subframe assembly requires cooperations of several maintenance technicians.

  1. Remove the front subframe and power assembly. See Front Subframe and Power Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the front stabilizer bar fitting assembly. See Front Stabilizer Bar Fitting Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove the mechanical steering gear with tie rod assembly. See Mechanical Steering Gear with Tie Rod Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  4. Remove one fixing nut/bolt from the front subframe assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 85±5N•m

  5. Remove the front subframe assembly.

    1. Remove 2 fixing nuts of the front subframe assembly.

      • Tightening torque: 55±5N•m

    2. Remove 6 fixing bolts and take out the front subframe assembly.

      • Tightening torque: 55±5N•m

Installation

  1. Follow the removal steps in reverse order to install.
