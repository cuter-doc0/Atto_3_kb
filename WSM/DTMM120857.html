System Function Principle

The lighting system provides lighting for the vehicle to drive at night. The exterior lighting mainly include headlights, reversing lights, license plate lights, fog lights, etc. The lights are installed in the required positions, equipped with control switches, circuits and fuses to form the interior lighting system. The lighting system also gets a signal prompt function, which generates light signals to warn other drivers and pedestrians and ensure the safety of the vehicle. This includes turn signals, brake signals, hazard warning signals, outline signals, and reversing signals. In addition to the traditional lighting functions, the vehicle is also equipped with automatic lighting and headlight delay off, which makes the lighting more convenient and humanized.

  • Automatic light: turn the light switch group to "AUTO". The left body control module will determine according to the external light intensity collected by the light intensity sensor, automatically control the light (on and off), and turn on the clearance light or headlight according to the light intensity.

  • Headlight delay off: when the headlights are turned on and the vehicle power is switched from OK to OFF, the headlights will not go out immediately. The light switch group will automatically count the time, disconnect the light relay after the headlight stays on for 10s, and then turn off the headlights.
