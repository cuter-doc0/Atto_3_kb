Integrated Thermal Management Module - Removal and Installation

Removal

Caution
After removing pipeline connections, properly seal all pipe fittings as soon as possible to prevent dust, impurities, and foreign objects from entering the A/C system and causing system faults.

  1. Recover the refrigerant See Recovery and Filling of Refrigerant 

  2. Power off the low voltage electrical system. See Power-off and Power-on of Low Voltage Electrical System 

  3. Power off the high voltage electrical system. See Power-off and power-on of high voltage electrical system 

  4. Remove the front motor lower mudguard assembly. See Front Motor Lower Mudguard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  5. Remove the refrigeration tube assembly 4. See Refrigeration Tube Assembly 4 - Removal and Installation 

  6. Remove compressor discharge pipe assembly 2. See Compressor Discharge Pipe Assembly 2 - Removal and Installation 

  7. Remove the refrigeration tube assembly 3. See Refrigeration Tube Assembly 3 - Removal and Installation 

  8. Remove compressor discharge pipe assembly 5. See Compressor Discharge Pipe Assembly 5 - Removal and Installation 

  9. Remove compressor suction pipe assembly 2. See Compressor Suction Pipe Assembly 2 - Removal and Installation 

  10. Detach the compressor discharge pipe assembly 3 and the refrigeration tube assembly 7 from the integrated thermal management module.

    1. Remove 1 fixing bolt, and detach the refrigeration tube assembly 7 from the integrated thermal management module.

      • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

    2. Remove 2 fixing bolts, and detach the compressor discharge pipe assembly 3 from the integrated thermal management module.

      • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  11. Detach the compressor suction pipe assembly 3 and the refrigeration tube assembly 6 from the integrated thermal management module.

    1. Remove 1 fixing bolt, and detach the compressor suction pipe assembly 3 from the integrated thermal management module.

      • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

    2. Remove 1 fixing bolt, and detach the refrigeration tube assembly 6 from the integrated thermal management module.

      • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  12. Disconnect 1 connector of Integrated Thermal Management Module

  13. Detach the wiring harness connection of the integrated thermal management module.

  14. Remove 3 fixing bolts, and take out the thermal management integrated module.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

Installation

Caution
For the installation, all “O-rings” of the pipeline must be replaced, and refrigerant oil shall be applied on the surface of the seal rings and the seal rings shall be connected according to the stipulated torque.

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps. To replace the thermal management integrated module, replenish refrigerant oil when filling refrigerant.
