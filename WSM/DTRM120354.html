Front Wall Thermal Insulation Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Remove the heat pump HVAC assembly. See Heat Pump HVAC Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the brake pedal assembly. See Brake Pedal Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove the brake pedal upper bracket. See Bracket On Brake Pedal - Removal and Installation 

  4. Remove the electric power steering column and universal joint assembly. See Electric Power Steering Column and Universal Joint Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  5. Remove the left body control module bracket. See Left Body Control Module Bracket - Removal and Installation 

  6. Remove the right body control module bracket. See Right Body Control Module Bracket - Removal and Installation 

  7. Disconnect 3 connectors of the floor wiring harness, instrument panel wiring harness and front compartment wiring harness (left side).

  8. Disconnect one connector between the front compartment wiring harness and the instrument fuse box.

  9. Remove 3 ground bolts from the front compartment wiring harness.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  10. Detach 7 fixing clips, and move away the floor wiring harness.

  11. Detach 7 fixing clips, and move away the front compartment wiring harness.

  12. Remove 10 plastic nuts and take out the front wall insulation assembly.

Installation

  1. Follow the removal steps in reverse order to install.
