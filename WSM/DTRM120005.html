Instrument Panel Upper Body Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

Warning

  • Carefully read the precautions related to the airbag module before repairing.

  • Before maintenance, turn off the ignition switch, disconnect the negative pole of the battery for more than 3min, and prevent static electricity.

  • Do not operate on the front of the airbag, but on the side of the airbag.

  1. Remove the left A-pillar upper guard assembly. See Left A-Pillar Upper Guard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the right A-pillar upper guard assembly. See Right A-Pillar Upper Guard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove the glove box assembly. See Glove Box Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  4. Remove the PAD guard upper support plate assembly. See PAD Guard Upper Support Plate Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  5. Remove the instrument panel right lower guard assembly. See Instrument Panel Right Lower Guard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  6. Remove the instrument panel right trim panel assembly. See Instrument Panel Right Trim Panel Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  7. Remove the instrument panel left trim panel assembly. See Instrument Panel Left Trim Panel Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  8. Remove the combination switch. See Combination Switch - Removal and Installation 

  9. Remove the left fastener of the instrument panel upper body assembly.

    1. Remove 1 fixing screws.

    2. Remove 1 fixing bolt.

      • Tightening torque: 4±0.5N•m

  10. Remove 6 fixing screws in the middle of the instrument panel upper body assembly.

  11. Remove the right fastener from the instrument panel upper body assembly.

    1. Remove 1 fixing screws.

    2. Remove 1 fixing bolt.

      • Tightening torque: 4±0.5N•m

  12. Remove 2 fixing bolts from the front passenger airbag module.

    • Tightening torque: 18~20N•m

  13. Disconnect the front passenger airbag module connector.

  14. Disconnect 1 connector between instrument panel wiring harness I and instrument panel wiring harness.

  15. Pry up the instrument panel upper body assembly.

  16. Remove three-in-one module. See Three-in-one Module - Removal and Installation 

  17. Remove instrument panel wiring harness I. See Instrument Panel Wiring Harness I - Removal and Installation 

  18. Remove the channel temperature sensor (face). See Channel Temperature Sensor (Face) - Removal and Installation 

Installation

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
