System Function Principle

The wiper and washing system is mainly composed of wiper arm assembly, wiper link, wiper brush, wiper motor, washing pump, wiper liquid pot, wash line and nozzle. It gets a low and a high speed, as well as an intermittent setting. The front windshield wiper and washing system is intended to provide a convenient, safe and reliable means to ensure the windshield view.

Principle of wiper system: the left body control module receives the combination switch signal, and controls the wiper motor to operate the wiper arm.

Principle of washing system: the right body control module receives the combination switch signal, controls the washer motor to spray water, which is emitted through the wash line and the nozzle.

Wiper system consists of:

  • Wiper blade assembly

  • Wiper arm assembly

  • Wiper drive system assembly

Washing system composition:

  • Washer reservoir and motor assembly

  • Wash line assembly

  • Nozzle assembly
