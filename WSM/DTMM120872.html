Precautions

Caution

  • Maintenance of air conditioning system must be carried out by professional technicians.

  • Wear goggles before maintenance and keep them until maintenance is completed.

  • Do not open the high pressure valve of the pressure gauge when the compressor is running.

  • Dedicated refrigerant oil must be used, using refrigerant oil of other brands or mixed using refrigerant oils of different grades is prohibited.

  • The refrigerant filling volume of the air conditioning system is 1100g± 10g. The total amount of refrigerant oil is 260mL. When the total amount of refrigerant oil is less than 200mL due to leakage, it may cause excessive wear of the compressor. The maintenance station shall add refrigerant oil as appropriate.

  • For the pressure protection for the air conditioner, pressure sensors are used to collect pressure information of the high pressure pipelines and of the low pressure pipelines. When the pressure at the high pressure side exceeds 3.15MPa or the pressure at the low pressure side is lower than 0.296MPa, the compressor relay will be disconnected for the protection. Temperature protections adopted include the evaporator temperature protection (low temperature protection 0--3°C); and the compressor overtemperature protection (high temperature protection 130±5°C).

  • During maintenance, replace the "O" ring of the opened pipeline, apply refrigerant oil on the seal before assembly, and then connect it to according to the required torque.

  • Follow the technical requirements (filling amount, model of refrigerant oil, torque requirements, etc.). Overhaul the air conditioner as required to ensure the normal operation and service life of the air conditioning system.

  • Since the refrigerant oil has strong water absorption, when removing the pipeline, the pipe openings shall be immediately sealed with a plug or a cap, so as to prevent the moisture or dust from entering the refrigeration system.

  • When discharging excessive refrigerant from the system, the excessive refrigerant shall be discharged slowly, so as to prevent the compressor oil from being discharged together.

  • Air filter shall be cleaned regularly to maintain good air conditioning quality.

  • Check whether the surface of the condenser radiator is dirty. Never wash it with steam or high pressure water. Brush it with a soft brush.

  • Avoid excessive refrigerant. Excessive refrigerant will lead to poor refrigeration.

Warning

  • Before the maintenance, make sure that the maintenance site is well ventilated. Carrying out the maintenance in a closed space or near an open flame is prohibited.

  • Avoid contact of liquid refrigerant with eyes and skin. If the refrigerant comes into contact with eyes and skin, rinse with cold water immediately, and please noted that never rub eyes or skin with your hands. Apply Vaseline jelly on the affected skin. For serious cases, seek medical advice immediately.

  • Never operate the electric compressor assembly if there is not enough refrigerant in the refrigeration system. Avoid burnout of the electric compressor assembly due to insufficient refrigerant and insufficient oil lubrication in the system.

  • Before maintaining high voltage components, power off the high voltage electrical system.
