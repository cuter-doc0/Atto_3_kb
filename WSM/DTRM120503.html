Right Front Door Glass Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Remove the right front door sealing plug I. See Right Front Door Sealing Plug I - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the right front door inner waist belt assembly. See Right Front Door Inner Waist Belt - Removal and Installation 

  3. Take out the patch.

  4. Remove the right front door glass assembly.

    1. Remove 2 fixing bolt.

      • Tightening torque: 8~10N•m

    2. Take out the assembly of right front door window.

    Caution

    Before removing the two fixing bolts, connect the negative electrode of the battery and connect relevant electrical components, and then adjust the fixing bolts of the front door glass to the removal position.

    In the event the removal process cannot be proceeded due to window regulator motor fault, remove the fixing bolts of the front window regulator, so as to adjust the front door glass to the removal position.

Installation

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
