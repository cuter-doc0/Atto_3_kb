Precautions

Caution

  • When removing and installing wheels, wear personal protective equipment, so as to avoid injury.

  • During the maintenance, the tool shall not directly contact the surface of the aluminum alloy wheels, so as to avoid scratching.

  • Cleaning the wheels with wire brush and strong detergent is prohibited. It may result in wheel damage.

  • The tyre pressure shall be maintained at the standard value, so as to avoid tyre burst during the driving process due to low or high tyre pressure.

  • Radial tires with rubber service life expired shall be replaced in time, so as to prevent tyre burst.

  • The front and rear wheels bear different loads when running, and the wear condition will be very different. To prevent the tires from being worn in a single direction, regular and timely rotation will make the tires wear evenly, thus prolong the service life of the tires.
