System Function Principle

System overview

This vehicle is equipped with a self-regulating A/C system designed with the single evaporator and applied to Deluxe and Premium SUVs. The system is mainly composed of compressor, condenser, HVAC assembly, cooling pipe, air duct and A/C controller, with the cooling, heating, defrosting & defogging and ventilation functions. The system uses a low voltage PTC heater for heating, and a vapor compression refrigeration cycle for refrigeration.The refrigerant is R1234yf/ R134a. And the control mode is touch operation. The mode damper, cooling and heating mixing damper and internal and external circulation damper of air handling unit (AHU) are all motor controlled.

Principle of Refrigeration System

The scroll compressor used for this model extracts the gaseous refrigerant from the evaporator and presses it into the condenser. The gaseous refrigerant is liquefied through the condenser for heat exchange (releasing heat), and the heat is taken away by the air outside the vehicle. The high pressure liquid refrigerant is depressurized by the throttling action of the expansion valve. The low pressure liquid refrigerant is vaporized in the evaporator for heat exchange (absorbing heat). The cooled air near the evaporator is blown into the compartment through the blower. The gaseous refrigerant is pumped out by the compressor and then pumped into the condenser, so that the refrigerant circulates in a closed way, discharging the heat in the compartment to the outside, and reducing the temperature in the compartment.

  1. Gas expands and temperature decreases

  2. Absorption of heat from the environment

Principle of Heating System

The heating system adopts heat pump or air PTC for heating. The heating system is mainly composed of wind PTC, compressor, gas-liquid separator, integrated thermal management module, heat exchanger, heating tube, and blower, duct and control mechanism. The heating unit shell is integrated with the evaporator shell, and the blower and duct are shared with the refrigeration system.

When the compressor is working, the high temperature and high pressure gaseous refrigerant discharged from the compressor discharge pipe enters the indoor condenser through the heating hard pipe. And the air blown by the blower distributes the heat flow of the high temperature and high pressure refrigerant to the passenger compartment or the front glass to raise the cabin temperature and defrost. The low temperature and low pressure liquid refrigerant dissipated in the indoor condenser becomes gaseous after the heat is absorbed from the environment by the integrated thermal management module (or the electric coolant of the motor) . The refrigerant then passes through the gas-liquid separator and enters the compressor, and so on, to achieve warm air heating.

  1. Compressed gas and temperature rise

  2. Heat dissipation into the cabin

Battery thermal management function

Battery thermal management function includes battery cooling, battery heating and battery recirculation functions. It is the main function of heat pump air conditioning system.

The characteristics of the heat pump A/C system include direct cooling and direct heating of the battery; Non-extremely low temperature, reducing A/C energy consumption; Extremely low temperature environment, supplemented by motor reactive power and low voltage PTC low efficient heat generation. Common functions include: heat pump heating, A/C cooling, battery cooling, battery heating, synchronous start-up of cooling and battery cooling synchronous start-up of heating and battery heating, dehumidification and other working modes.

  1. A/C heater

  2. Battery heating

  3. A/C heating and battery heating

  4. Battery cooling

  5. Air conditioner cooling and battery cooling

  6. Air conditioner heating and battery cooling
