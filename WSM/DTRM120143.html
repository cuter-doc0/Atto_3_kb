Left Front Door Guard Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Power off the low voltage electrical system. See Power-off and Power-on of Low Voltage Electrical System 

  2. Remove the left front window control panel assembly. See Front Left Window Control Panel Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  3. Pry up the left front door handle box cover.

  4. Remove 1 fixing bolt from the left front door guard assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 5±0.5N•m

  5. Turn 45 ° clockwise, and take out the left front door snap handle ambient light.

  6. Remove 2 fixing bolt from the left front door guard assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 5±0.5N•m

  7. Pry up the left front door guard assembly.

  8. Disconnect 1 connector of the left front door tweeter horn.

  9. Disconnect 1 connector of the left front door ambient light assembly.

  10. Disconnect 1 connector of the left front door inner snap handle ambient light assembly.

  11. Disconnect 2 connections of the door lock cable, and take out the left front door guard assembly.

  12. Disconnect 1 connector of the left door light.

  13. Take out the left front door guard assembly.

  14. Remove the left front door snap handle ambient light assembly. See Right Front Door Snap Handle Ambient Light Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  15. Remove the left door light (door). See Left Door Light (Door) - Removal and Installation 

Installation

Caution

  • Before installation, it is necessary to check whether all the clips are missing or damaged, and if so, install new clips, and then install the trim panel;

  • After installation, check whether all clips are installed in place. The installation position of the trim panel may deviate, resulting in warping or abnormal gaps.

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
