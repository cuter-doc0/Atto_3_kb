Front Drive Motor + Transmission Assembly Maintenance Kit - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Remove the integrated intelligent front drive assembly. See Integrated Intelligent Front Drive Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Loosen 2 fixing clamps,and take out the cooling pipe assembly.

  3. Remove left cover of the integrated intelligent front drive assembly.

    1. Remove 9 fixed bolts.

      • Tightening torque: 6N • m

    2. Remove one stud bolt and take out the left cover of the integrated intelligent front drive assembly.

      • Tightening torque: 6N • m

    Caution

    • Uncover the front drive electric assembly package II side.

    • Each time the left cover of the integrated intelligent front drive assembly is removed, the operation shall be standardized. The bolts should be removed from the outside to the inside.

    • To remove the motor rear cover, you may use a scraper tilted at a 25° angle from top to bottom. Press it down gently to avoid damaging the sealing surface.

  4. To disconnect the rotary transformer connector of the drive motor control module assembly maintenance kit, you may use a small slotted screwdriver.

    Caution

    Never touch the rotary transformer components and temperature sensing plug-ins to avoid damaging them.

  5. Remove 3 fixing bolts of the three-phase high voltage wiring harness of integrated intelligent front drive assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 11.5N • m

  6. Remove 6 fixing bolts and take out the drive motor control module assembly maintenance kit.

    • Tightening torque: 35N • m

  7. Take out the front drive motor and transmission assembly maintenance kit.

Installation

  1. Follow the removal steps in reverse order to install.

  2. Replace the gasket after each removal.
