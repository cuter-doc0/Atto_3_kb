Pipe Beam Welding Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

Caution
Never scratch the front door interior trim panel or touch the front windshield assembly when taking out the pipe beam welding assembly.

  1. Remove the left strut assembly. See Left Strut Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the right strut assembly. See Right Strut Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove the instrument panel lower body assembly. See Instrument Panel Lower Body Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  4. Remove the ventilation cover assembly. References Ventilation Cover Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  5. Remove the PM2.5 quick tester assembly. See PM2.5 Quick Tester Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  6. Remove the ECallBOX assembly. See ECallBOX Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  7. ECall4GGPS antenna. See ECall4GGPS Antenna - Removal and Installation 

  8. Remove 3 fixing bolts of pipe beam welding assembly exterior.

    • Tightening torque: 25±2N•m

  9. Remove 4 fixing bolts of pipe beam welding assembly middle.

    • Tightening torque: 6N • m

  10. Remove one fixing bolt of pipe beam welding assembly rear.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  11. Remove one fixing bolt of Instrument fuse.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  12. Detach 24 wiring harness clips of pipe beam welding assembly,and move away the wiring harness of Instrument Panel.

  13. Remove 10 fixing bolts of pipe beam welding assembly rear.

    • Tightening torque: 25±2N•m

  14. Take out the pipe beam welding assembly.

Installation

  1. Follow the removal steps in reverse order to install.
