Inspection of charging port

Caution

  • Before operation, high-voltage components in the operating area shall be visually checked.

  • Any work on a vehicle equipped with a high-voltage system shall be only carried out by qualified personnel.

  • In case of any abnormality or doubt, handling shall be only carried out by qualified personnel in HV or electrical aspect.

  1. Check the charging port.

    1. Open the charging port cover and check the charging port for foreign matters or aging.

    2. Check the charging port terminal reed for wear, aging, deformation, fracture and ablation.

      Caution

      Operate carefully to avoid the risk of electric shock.

    Reminder

    In case of any defect, it must be handled immediately by qualified personnel.

  2. Obtain a new work order for repair or replacement.
