Precautions

Caution

  • Carefully read the precautions related to the airbag module before repairing.

  • Before maintenance, turn off the ignition switch, disconnect the negative pole of the battery for more than 3 min, and discharge the static electricity.

  • Do not operate on the front of the airbag, but on the side of the airbag.

  • All seat belt assembly shall be inspected after any collision. Replace the old assembly with a new seat belt assembly unless it is confirmed by a qualified professional technician that the old assembly is not damaged and functions normally. The seat belt assembly not used in the collision shall also be checked, and in case of damage, replace it with a new one. Installing a new seat belt assembly according to the improper installation procedure may increase the risk of personal injury during a crash.

  • The seat belt pretensioner is a pyrotechnic device. Always wear goggles when servicing seat belt equipped with pretensioner. Do not explore the electrical terminal of the pretensioner, as this may trigger the pretensioner, cause the deployment of airbag, and result in personal injury.

  • Failure to follow the correct operation may result in the accidental deployment of the airbag and the failure of the safety protection device when required.

Caution

  • The seat belt shall be properly connected with the retractor and the buckle.

  • Keep sharp and destructive objects away from the seat belt.

  • Do not bleach or dye the belt (wash it with neutral soap and warm water only).

  • Pre-tighten the screw by hand during installation of the seat belt to avoid thread disorder.

  • Do not attempt to repair the retractor or its cover, and replace the failed assembly with a new one.

  • Be sure to keep the belt dry and clean.

  • Replace the cut or damaged seat belt.

  • When disconnecting the seat belt connector, insert a small screwdriver wrapped with adhesive tape into the connector slot to pry up the striker, and then pull out the connector.

  • After installing the seat belt connector, check whether the striker is fully pressed in.

  • The seat belt and its accessories can affect major components and systems of a vehicle. Therefore, check it carefully and original parts shall be used for the replacement.

  • Triggered pretensioner (retractor) cannot be reused, the whole pretensioner assembly shall be replaced.
