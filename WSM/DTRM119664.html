Removal of Front Shock Absorber Strut Assembly

Removal

Reminder
The removal and installation methods of the left and right front shock absorber strut assemblies are the same. The left front shock absorber strut assembly is taken as an example.

  1. Remove the left front shock absorber strut assembly. See Left Front Shock Absorber Strut Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Install the spring compressor.

  3. Compress the front shock absorber coil spring until it is separated from the front shock absorber bearing combination.

  4. Take out the front shock absorber dust cover.

  5. Fix the left front shock absorber damper assembly with a tool and remove 1 lock nut.

    • Tightening torque: 85±5N•m

  6. Take out the front shock absorber upper support combination.

  7. Take out the front shock absorber bearing combination.

  8. Take out the front shock absorber spring upper seat combination.

  9. Take out the front shock absorber coil spring.

  10. Take out the front shock absorber spring lower cushion.

  11. Take out the front shock absorber dust cover.

  12. Take out the front shock absorber buffer.

  13. Take out the left front shock absorber damper assembly.

Install

Caution

  • Fit the lower end of the coil spring into the groove of the front shock absorber spring lower cushion.

  • Turn and align the front shock absorber upper support combination.

  1. The installation is performed in reverse order of removal.
