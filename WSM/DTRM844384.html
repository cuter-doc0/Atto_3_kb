Inspection and setting of power window function

Warning

  • Do not pinch someone's hand or head when operating the window or sunroof; otherwise, extremely serious injury may be caused.

Inspection of power window lifting function

  1. Power on the vehicle (OK mode).

  2. Check that the front and rear door windows can open and close normally without any abnormal sound or jamming, and remove the dirt or dust in the glass-run if necessary.

  3. Obtain a new work order for repair or replacement.

Reminder

After disconnection and connection of the 12 V battery, the automatic raising function of the power window regulator may not work. Therefore, the window regulator must be reset before delivery of a new vehicle, and it is not allowed to disconnect the 12 V battery after setting.

Caution

After disconnection and connection of the 12 V battery, the anti-pinch function of the power window regulator will not work. Therefore, be careful to avoid serious crush injury!

Perform the following steps to initialize the power window regulator:

Reminder

The following operation description takes the front left window regulator as an example. The setting of other window regulators is also realized by operating the switch on the front left door.

  1. Power on the vehicle (OK mode).

  2. Fully close all windows and doors.

  3. After closing the window glass, release the switch, pull up the window regulator switch again and hold it for more than 3s.

At this time, the automatic regulation and anti-pinch functions are activated again.

Caution

Operating the anti-pinch function initialization too frequently will cause the thermal protection of the window regulator motor.
