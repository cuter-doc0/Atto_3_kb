Inspection of 12 V Battery Wiring Harness

Repair tools and equipment required

  • Torque wrench (5-50 N•m)

Reminder

  • The 12 V battery wiring harness is firmly connected and installed to ensure normal function and durability of the battery.

  • When fixing the 12 V battery wiring harness, be sure to place the connector completely flat on the 12 V battery terminal.

Caution

If the wiring harness on the 12 V battery positive terminal is not connected firmly, disconnect the GND wire on the 12 V battery negative terminal first to eliminate the risk of accident.

  1. Turn off all electrical appliances of the vehicle and power off the vehicle.

  2. Open the 12 V battery heat shield.

  3. Open the positive protective cover of 12 V battery assembly.

  4. Check that positive and negative wiring harnesses of the 12 V battery are firmly installed and fixed, and tighten the fixing nuts to the specified torque if necessary.

    Caution

    Do not check the fixation by lifting the connector upward! Check whether the wiring harness is firmly fixed by turning it slightly in the horizontal direction.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1 N•m

  5. Check that the positive and negative terminals of the 12 V battery are free of corrosion, and carry out anti-corrosion treatment or replace the 12 V battery assembly if necessary. Refer to Battery Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  6. Obtain a new work order for repair or replacement.
