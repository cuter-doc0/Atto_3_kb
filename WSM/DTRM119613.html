Expansion Tank Testing

Testing Procedure

  1. Wait patiently until the coolant is cooled down to the room temperature, screw off the expansion tank cover, and check whether the coolant level falls at the MAX mark of the expansion tank, if not, add coolant of the same specification until reaching the MAX mark on the expansion tank.

  2. Connect a small fitting to the pressure meter, and then install the pressure meter onto the expansion tank.

  3. Apply a pressure between 15kPa to 45kPa.

  4. Hold the pressure for 5 minutes, and check the liquid level and check for pressure drop.

  5. Remove the pressure meter, and install the expansion tank cover onto the expansion tank.
