Adjustment of Front Compartment Cover Assembly

Adjust

  1. Open the front compartment cover assembly.

  2. Loosen the fixing nuts, and adjust the front compartment cover assembly.

    1. Loosen 4 fixing nut.

      • Tightening torque: 28±2N•m

    2. Adjust the front compartment cover assembly horizontally and vertically.

  3. Adjust the front compartment cover assembly to the satisfactory range, and tighten the fixing nuts.

  4. Adjust the front end of the front compartment cover through the buffer block, so that the front compartment cover is aligned with the fender.
