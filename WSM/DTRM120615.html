Driver Airbag Module - Removal and Installation

Removal

Warning

  • The safety protection system (SRS) can still work for a period of time after the power is disconnected. Wait at least 3 min before disassembling or removing any SRS component.

  • Ensure that the vehicle electrical system is completely powered off and that no other power source is connected.

  • Do not expose any SRS parts to high temperatures or open flames directly. If any SRS part falls, or if any cracks, dents or other defects are found, replace it with a new one.

  1. Power off the low voltage electrical system. See Power-off and Power-on of Low Voltage Electrical System 

  2. Insert a slotted screwdriver into the 2 service holes of the driver airbag module and press the spring clip in the direction of the arrow.

  3. Detach the driver airbag module from the steering wheel.

  4. Disconnect 2 connector, and take out the driver airbag module.

Installation

Warning

  • Failure to follow the following precautions when installing airbags may result in improper airbag deployment and increase the risk of serious injury or death in a crash.

  • Before installing the airbag, check whether the installation position of the airbag and the steering wheel is damaged. If there is any damage, please install new parts.

  • Before installing the airbag, check for foreign objects between the airbag and the steering wheel to ensure that the airbag can deploy properly.

  • Do not attempt to disassemble or repair the airbag. If any abnormality occurs, replace the airbag with a new one.

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
