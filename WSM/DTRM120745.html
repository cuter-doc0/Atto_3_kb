Left Body Control Module - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Power off the low voltage electrical system. See Power-off and Power-on of Low Voltage Electrical System 

  2. Remove the glove box assembly. See Glove Box Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  3. Detach the wiring harness from the left body control module.

    1. Disconnect 7 connectors.

    2. Remove 1 fixing nut, and put aside the wiring harness.

    • Tightening torque: 6±1N•m

  4. Remove 2 fixing bolts, and take out the left body control module.

    Caution
    During removal, handle the left body control module with care, never touch the plug pins of the left body control module, or disassemble the left body control module, so as to prevent damage to the left body control module.

    • Tightening torque: 8±1N•m

Installation

Caution
If the left body controller is replaced, VDS is required to write the left body controller to the vehicle. See Write of Left Body Control Module 

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
