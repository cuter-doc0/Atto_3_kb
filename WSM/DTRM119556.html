Camber Calibration of Drive Motor Control Module Assembly

Camber Calibration Procedure

Caution
On completion of the replacement of drive motor control module assembly, use VDS to carry out tilt angle calibration. Set the vehicle power supply to OK and pull up the vehicle on a level ground.

  1. Use a VDS to connect the vehicle OBD interface. Refer to the VDS Instructions

    Reminder
    OBD interface is located at right lower corner of right lower guard plate of instrument panel.

  2. Set the vehicle power supply to OK, enter “Vehicle Diagnostic System”, and select the vehicle model.

  3. Select “ECU Module”, execute vehicle ECU module scanning.

  4. After the scanning is complete, select "Dynamic Body Control Module TI" to enter the dynamic body control module diagnosis interface.

  5. Select “Camber Calibration”, VDS displays “Calibration Result: Calibration Seceded”, it indicates that the camber calibration is completed.

  6. Execute “Data Flow” to read the camber calibration state, is VDS displays “Calibrated”, it indicates that the camber calibration of drive motor control module assembly has been completed successfully.
