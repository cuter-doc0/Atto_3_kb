Precautions

Warning

  • Do not contact the negative harness with the positive pole of the battery cable.

  • Keep the battery away from open fire or high temperature.

  • Do not disassemble or modify batteries without permission.

  • Do not charge the battery with chargers that are not approved by the manufacturer.

  • Collision, falling off and knocking shall be prevented.

  • Keep batteries away from flammable and explosive substances.

  • Keep batteries out of reach of children.

  • Do not place batteries upside down during transportation or storage.
