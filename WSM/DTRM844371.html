Inspection of on-board items

Reminder

  • On-board items may differ according to vehicle models and are subject to actual allocation and official instruction. Following are just a few tools for common use and emergency.

  • The on-board tools are stored in the toolkit under the trunk cover board.

On-board items

Quantity

Inspection contents

Warning triangle

1

Check whether the warning triangle is in good condition.

Reflective vest

1

Check whether the reflective vest is free of damage.

Removal clamp of wheel nut cap

1

Check whether the removal clamp of wheel nut cap is in good condition.

Tire repair device

1

Check the expiration date of the tire repair liquid (refer to the date on the label of the tire repair liquid container) and remind the customer to replace the liquid when it is about to expire.

Towing hook

1

Check whether the thread of towing hook is in good condition.

AC charging equipment/discharging equipment (if any)

1

Check the status of AC charging equipment/discharging equipment in the on-board tools.

  • Check whether all components are free from external damage.

  • Ensure that the insulator of charging wire is in good condition and not damaged.

  • Check the charging wire for abnormal deformation.

Microphone (if any)

1

Check whether the microphone is in good condition.

If any defect is found, the customer must be informed and relevant repairs shall be carried out.

If the on-board items are lost or damaged, the customer must be informed and corresponding measures shall be taken after negotiation.
