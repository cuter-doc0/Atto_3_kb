System Function Principle

The central control door lock system is a system where the left/right body control module controls the unlocking/locking of the electric door lock. Its operation mode is divided into two types:

  1. Press the master door lock switch on the right front window regulator switch group to send the unlocking/locking request signal to the left/right body control module. The control module receives and processes the switch signal and drives the corresponding door lock motor to unlock/lock.

  2. When unlocking a door with a mechanical key, the key cylinder switch sends an unlocking/locking request signal to the left body control module, which receives and processes the switch signal and drive the corresponding door lock motor to unlock/lock.
