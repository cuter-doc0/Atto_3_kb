Right Front Door Snap Handle Ambient Light Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

Reminder
The removal and installation methods of the left front and the right front door snap handle ambient light assemblies are the same. The right front door snap handle ambient light assembly is taken as an example.

  1. Remove the right front door guard assembly. See Right Front Door Guard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the right front door snap handle ambient light control module.

    1. Disconnect 1 connectors.

    2. Remove 2 fixing screws, and take out the right front door snap handle ambient light control module.

  3. Remove 3 fixing screws, and take out the right front door inner snap handle.

  4. Remove 2 fixing screws, and put aside the right front tweeter horn.

  5. Remove 1 fixing screws.

  6. Pry up the right front door inner snap handle seat and the handle.

  7. Remove 2 fixing screws, and take out the right door inner snap handle inner plate.

  8. Take out the right front door inner snap handle ambient light and the tweeter horn.

Install

  1. The installation is performed in reverse order of removal.
