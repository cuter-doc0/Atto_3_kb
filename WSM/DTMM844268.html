Maintenance cycle reminder and display

Reminder

  • The menus displayed on the infotainment display screen depend on the vehicle electrical system and vehicle equipment.

Calculation of maintenance cycle:

  • Vehicles maintained by time or mileage have a fixed maintenance cycle, that is, the mileage or interval is determined and specified in advance.

  • From a technical point of view, be sure to observe this maintenance cycle under normal operating conditions.

Display of due maintenance items:

  • When the remaining service life of fluid is short, the PAD will display information such as "Please replace the fluid ASAP" after each power-on.

The maintenance prompt disappears after the vehicle runs or after a few seconds.

Maintenance warning:

If a maintenance item is about to expire, the infotainment display screen will display "Maintenance prompt" when the vehicle is powered on.

  • Display the time to replace transmission fluid, brake fluid and coolant.

The maintenance prompt disappears after the vehicle runs or after a few seconds.

  • The first maintenance warning will be displayed before the expiration of maintenance.

  • The remaining mileage displayed is usually rounded up by 100 km, and the remaining driving time is rounded up by days.

Maintenance information inquiry in the infotainment system:

The current maintenance information can be inquired at any time when the vehicle exits the driving status and is stationary.

  • Power on the vehicle (OK mode).

  • Turn on the infotainment system.

  • Tap "Vehicle".

  • Tap "Vehicle health".

  • Tap "Intelligent maintenance" or "Maintenance".

At this time, the "Fluid service life" or "Maintenance time and mileage" is displayed on the current interface of the infotainment system.

Reminder

The intelligent vehicle maintenance system must be reset after each maintenance and fluid replacement for its normal operation.
