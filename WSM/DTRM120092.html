Rear Headrest Slide Cover Components - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Remove the rear seat headrest assembly. See Rear Headrest Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Open the zipper, and lift the left rear backrest skin.

  3. Remove 5 fixing clips, and take out the left rear backrest plate.

  4. Remove the rear headrest sliding sleeve assembly.

    1. Press both sides of the headrest sliding sleeve component.

    2. Take out the headrest sliding sleeve component.

Removal

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
