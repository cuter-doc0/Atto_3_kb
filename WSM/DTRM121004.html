Floor Wiring Harness - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Remove the rear floor mudguard. See Rear Floor Mudguard - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the left rear wheel mudguard assembly. See Left Rear Wheel Mudguard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove the right rear wheel mudguard assembly. See Right Rear Wheel Mudguard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  4. Remove the carpet assembly. See Carpet Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  5. Remove the carpet insulation assembly. See Carpet Insulation Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  6. Remove the ceiling assembly. See Ceiling Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  7. Disconnect 2 connectors from the left rear wheel side.

  8. Disconnect 2 connectors from the right rear wheel side.

  9. Remove 7 clips from the left rear wheel side.

  10. Remove 7 clips from the right rear wheel side.

  11. Detach the floor wiring harness from the rear bumper.

    1. Disconnect 1 connectors.

    2. Detach the floor wiring harness from the chassis.

  12. Disconnect 1 connector between the floor wiring harness and the left rear door wiring harness.

  13. Disconnect 1 connector between the floor wiring harness and the right rear door wiring harness.

  14. Disconnect 4 connectors from the left side of the trunk and the roof.

  15. Disconnect 11 connectors from the right side of the trunk and the roof.

  16. Disconnect 10 connectors from the front passenger's side.

  17. Disconnect 4 connectors from the driver's side.

  18. Disconnect 2 connectors from the rear seat side.

  19. Remove 3 ground bolts from the left side of the trunk.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  20. Remove 3 ground bolts from the right side of the trunk.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  21. Remove 3 ground bolts from the front passenger's side.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  22. Remove 2 ground bolts from the driver's side.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  23. Remove 11 fixing clips from the left rear ceiling side.

  24. Remove 15 fixing clips from the right rear ceiling side.

  25. Remove 12 fixing clips from the left side of the trunk.

  26. Remove 18 fixing clips from the right side of the trunk.

  27. Remove 51 fixing clips, and take out the floor wiring harness.

Installation

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
