Manual Reset of Maintenance Cycle

Setting of maintenance reminder cycle

Caution

Maintenance cycle prompt shall be set after each regular maintenance.

  1. Power on the vehicle.

  2. Go to the Infotainment system → Vehicle → Vehicle health → "Maintenance" page.

  3. Tap the "Settings" option beside "Maintenance time: XXX days", select the interval days between this maintenance and the next maintenance (refer to maintenance cycle), and then tap "OK".

  4. Tap the "Settings" option beside "Maintenance mileage: XXX km", select the interval mileage between this maintenance and the next maintenance (refer to maintenance cycle), and then tap "OK".

Reset of intelligent vehicle maintenance system

Reminder

The intelligent vehicle maintenance system must be reset after each fluid replacement for its normal operation. Whether the fluid service life reset function is equipped depends on the actual vehicle.

  1. Power on the vehicle (OK mode) and keep the gearshift lever in P position.

  2. In Infotainment system → Vehicle → Vehicle health → "Intelligent maintenance" page, the remaining service life of fluid is displayed.

  3. Tap "Reset" beside the "Fluid service life" to reset the fluid service life, and "XXX service life reset succeeded" will be displayed on the PAD.
