Wheel changing

Because the load borne by the front and rear wheels of the vehicle is different during driving, the wear situation will be different. Therefore, in order to avoid tyre wear in a single direction, regular and timely transposition can make the tyre wear even, thus extending the tyre life. It is recommended to change positions every 5000-8000km.The main purpose of tyre transposition is :

  • Ensure uniform tyre wear and average fatigue, thus ensuring stability and economy.

  • Check the condition of tires during transposition .It can find out the damage in time and prevent accidents.

  • When replacing tires, it is necessary to ensure that the steering tires are in the best condition.It can improve the vehicle's stability and safety.

Exchange Places Method for Wheels

  1. Remove 4 wheel assemblies.

  2. Install the 1 # wheel to the original mounting position of the 4 # wheel.

  3. Install the 4 # wheel to the original mounting position of the 2 # wheel.

  4. Install the 2 # wheel to the original mounting position of the 3 # wheel.

  5. Install the 3 # wheel to the original mounting position of the 1 # wheel.
