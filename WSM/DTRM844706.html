Inspection of Test Drive Items

Inspection of Foot and Hand Brakes, Steering, Driving Performance, Noise, A/C, Instrument Panel Cluster Display, etc. Through Test Drive

Reminder

  • The following ranges are related to vehicle equipment and the environment (urban/rural) in which it is used.

  • The road test is not required for each maintenance, but shall be carried out according to the actual needs.

The following items shall be checked and evaluated during test drive:

  • Performance and acceleration of motor.

  • Gear shifting: flexibility, gearshift switch position, gearshift status and instrument panel cluster display.

  • Foot brake and parking brake: function, free stroke and application, deviation, shaking and braking noise.

  • ABS function: a pulsation on the brake pedal must be felt during ABS modulating braking.

  • Steering mechanism: function, steering clearance, steering wheel in the middle position during straight driving.

  • Panoramic sunroof: functions.

  • Infotainment system: function, reception, automatic matching of volume and vehicle speed, interference and noise.

  • A/C: function test (when the temperature is low, the A/C function must be tested in workshop).

  • Vehicle: deviation or not during driving straight (flat lane).

  • Unbalance (vibration): wheel, drive shaft and universal joint shaft.

  • Wheel bearing: abnormal sound.

  • Other noise or abnormal sound.

If any abnormality is determined, the customer must be informed and relevant repair measures shall be taken.
