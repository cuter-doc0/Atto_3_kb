Left Front Lower Swing Arm Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Remove the left front wheel assembly. See Wheel Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the front motor lower mudguard assembly. See Front Motor Lower Mudguard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove the left front lower swing arm assembly and the fastener of the ball stud press assembly.

    1. Remove 2 fixing nuts.

      • Tightening torque: 90±5N•m

    2. Remove 1 fixing bolt.

      • Tightening torque: 90±5N•m

  4. Remove the left front lower swing arm assembly.

    1. Remove 1 fixing nut/bolt.

      • Tightening torque: 200±10N•m

    2. Remove 1 fixing nut/bolt, and take out the left front lower swing arm assembly.

      • Tightening torque: 200±10N•m

    Reminder
    Take out the left front lower arm assembly with the assistance of another maintenance person.

Installation

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
