BuildYourDreams Word Mark Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

Reminder
The removal and installation methods of BuildYourDreams word mark assembly and BYD word mark assembly; EV word mark assembly; and PLUS word mark assembly are the same, The BuildYourDreams word mark assembly is taken as an example.
Caution

  • If the coating surface is scratched and damaged, repair it or replace it with a new one, otherwise the damaged part will gradually expand.

  • Cover the body around the word mark with adhesive tapes, so as to prevent the body and the paint from being damaged.

  1. Mark the position before removing the word mark.

  2. Pull the wire along the word mark, cut the double-sided tape of the word mark carefully, and take out the BuildYourDreams word mark assembly.

    Caution
    Heat the body around the word mark with a dryer, to facilitate word mark removing.

Installation

  1. Clear residual sealant on the contact face of the body and the word mark, and make sure the surface of the body meets relevant requirements.

    Caution

    • Be careful not to damage the body on its paint.

    • After cleaning, do not touch oil and water.

  2. Install the BuildYourDreams word mark assembly according to the mark.
