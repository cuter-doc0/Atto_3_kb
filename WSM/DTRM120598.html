Right Rear Water Pipe Components - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Remove the ceiling assembly. See Ceiling Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Loosen the hoop, and detach the right rear water pipe assembly from the sunroof.

  3. Detach the 3 pipe clamps.

  4. Lift part of the carpet.

  5. Detach the right rear water pipe assembly from the vehicle body, and take it out.

Installation

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
