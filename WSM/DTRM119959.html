Front Bumper Body - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Remove the front middle position light. See Front Middle Position Light - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the front bumper wire. See Front Bumper Line - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove the left trim cover of front bumper. See Left Trim Cover of Front Bumper - Removal and Installation 

  4. Remove the right trim cover of front bumper. See Right Trim Cover of Front Bumper - Removal and Installation 

  5. Remove the front small probe. See Front Small Probe - Removal and Installation 

  6. Remove the front-view camera assembly. Attendance Front-View Camera Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  7. Remove 3 fixing screws, and take out the camera cover.

  8. Remove 7 fixing screws from the front bumper body.

  9. Pry up the front bumper lower body.

Installation

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
