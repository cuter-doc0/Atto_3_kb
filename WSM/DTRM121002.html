Ceiling Wiring Harness - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Remove the instrument panel upper body assembly. See Instrument Panel Upper Body Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove LDW module cover I. See LDW Module Cover I - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove the ceiling assembly. See Ceiling Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  4. Disconnect the front connection of the ceiling wiring harness.

    1. Disconnect 3 connectors.

    2. Detach 2 fixing clip.

  5. Detach 3 fixing clips between the ceiling wiring harness and the right A-pillar.

  6. Disconnect 1 connector of sunroof blinder motor.

  7. Disconnect 1 connector of the rear blinder motor.

  8. Disconnect 1 connector of the digital video recorder camera.

  9. Disconnect 1 connector of air humidity sensor.

  10. Disconnect 1 connector of the multifunction video controller assembly.

  11. Remove the fixing clip and fixing bolt from the top of the ceiling wiring harness.

    1. Remove 1 fixing bolt.

      • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

    2. Detach 25 fixing clip.

  12. Take out the ceiling wiring harness.

Installation

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
