Radial Tire - Removal and Installation

Removal

Caution
The removal and installation methods of other radial tires are the same. The left front radial tyre is taken as an example.

  1. Remove the left front wheel assembly. See Wheel Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Radial tire exhaust.

    1. Screw off the valve cap.

    2. Screw off the valve element with the air discharge tool for air discharging.

  3. Remove the radial tire from the rim with a tire changer.

    Caution

    • When using the tyre changer to remove radial tires, press the four edges of the radial tires so as to facilitate removing.

    • Do not contact the rim when using the tyre changer, for it may cause rim damage.

    • When using the tyre changer, the valve nozzle shall be avoided, so as to prevent damage to the tyre pressure sensor.

  4. Turn the radial tire over, and remove the other side of the radial tire from the rim with the tire changer.

    Caution

    • When using the tyre changer to remove radial tires, press the four edges of the radial tires so as to facilitate removing.

    • Do not contact the rim when using the tyre changer, for it may cause rim damage.

    • When using the tyre changer, the valve nozzle shall be avoided, so as to prevent damage to the tyre pressure sensor.

  5. Pry up the upper edge of the radial tire with a tire lever, and detach the upper edge of the radial tire with a tire changer.

    Caution

    • When removing radial tires, note the distance between the changer head of tyre changer and the rim, so as to prevent the rim surface from being damaged.

    • When prying up the tyre, clamp the rim firmly and carefully check the condition before prying. Never separate the radial tyre by hand.

    • When removing the radial tyre, never damage tyre nozzle, so as to avoid leakage.

  6. Pry up the lower edge of the radial tire with a tire lever, and remove the radial tire with a tire changer.

    Caution

    • When removing radial tires, note the distance between the changer head of tyre changer and the rim, so as to prevent the rim surface from being damaged.

    • When prying up the tyre, clamp the rim firmly and carefully check the condition before prying. Never separate the radial tyre by hand.

    • When removing the radial tyre, never damage tyre nozzle, so as to avoid leakage.

Installation

  1. Load the lower edge of the radial tire onto the head of the tire changer, and press the lower edge of the tire into the rim with the tire changer.

    Caution

    • When installing radial tires, apply soapy water on the contact surface between the tyre bead and the rim along the peripheral direction.

    • When installing radial tires, the side marked with “OUTSIDE” or “Production Cycle” shall face outwards.

    • When installing radial tires, note the distance between the changer head of tyre changer and the rim, so as to prevent the rim surface from being damaged.

    • When installing radial tires, clamp the rim firmly and carefully check the condition before the installation.

  2. Load the upper edge of the radial tire onto the head of the tire changer, and press the upper edge of the tire into the rim with the tire changer.

    Caution

    • When installing radial tires, note the distance between the changer head of tyre changer and the rim, so as to prevent the rim surface from being damaged.

    • Remove the wheel assembly from the tyre changer, and inflate the tyre to a pressure between 100kPa to 200kPa. The sound of the tyre bead sliding across the rim dome must be heard, then tighten the valve.

      1. Adjust the inflation pressure to the specified value.

      2. Carry out wheel dynamic balancing.

      3. On completion of wheel assembly installation, the tyre pressure sensor shall be fitted.
