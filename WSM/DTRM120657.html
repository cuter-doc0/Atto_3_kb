Tailgate Lock Body Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Power off the low voltage electrical system. See Power-off and Power-on of Low Voltage Electrical System 

  2. Remove the tailgate lower guard assembly. See Tailgate Lower Guard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove the tailgate lock cover. See Tailgate Lock Cover - Removal and Installation 

  4. Disconnect one connector of the tailgate lock body assembly.

  5. Remove 2 fixing bolts of the tailgate lock body assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 20±2N•m

  6. Take out the tailgate lock body assembly.

Installation

  1. Follow the removal steps in reverse order to install.

    Caution

    After the installation, check the functionality of the tailgate lock body assembly.
