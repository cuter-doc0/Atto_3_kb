Left Front Door Upper Hinge Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Remove the left fender assembly. See Left Fender Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the left front door upper hinge assembly.

    1. Remove 2 fixing bolts between the left front door upper hinge assembly and the door.

      • Tightening torque: 28±2N•m

    2. Remove 2 fixing bolts between the left front door upper hinge assembly and the vehicle body.

      • Tightening torque: 28±2N•m

    3. Take out the left front door upper hinge assembly.

Installation

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.

  2. After the installation, adjust the front door assembly. See Adjustment of Front Door Assembly
