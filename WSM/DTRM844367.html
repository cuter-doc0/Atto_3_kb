Inspection and Adjustment of Headlight (Headlight Adjustment Tool)

Repair tools and equipment required

  • VDS

  • Headlight adjustment tool

Reminder

It is essential to adjust the headlight cluster assembly in case of lighting deviation, or removal/installation and replacement.

Caution

Before testing and adjusting the headlight, read the instructions of the headlight adjustment tool carefully and operate strictly as required.

Conditions of inspection and adjustment

  • Check and make sure that there is no damage or deformation of the vehicle and around the headlights.

  • Ensure the headlight glass is not damaged or dirty, and the reflector and lights are normal.

  • Make sure that the oil level and the coolant level meet the design requirements.

  • Make sure that the tire pressure meets the standard inflation pressure requirement.

  • Never place other articles in the vehicle, but be sure to place the tools delivered with the vehicle at the specified positions.

  • Turning the steering device is not allowed.

  • The vehicle and headlight adjustment tool must be on the same plane.

  • The vehicle or headlight adjustment tool must be calibrated.

  • The inclination of headlights must be adjusted.

  • The fault record must be cleared.

  • Dynamic headlight follow-up function (if any) must be turned off.

  • The lighting distance adjustment initialization step must have been completed.

  • The headlamp tester must be in the correct adjustment mode.

Reminder

After adjusting the headlights, the dynamic headlight follow-up function (if any) must be reactivated.

Preparation of vehicle under test

  1. The driver seat shall be occupied by a 75kg person (if the weight is insufficient, the counterweight method can be used to reach the standard 75kg).

  2. The vehicle must be moved several meters and shake the vehicle body from side to side, so that the front and rear parts of the vehicle bounce many times, so that the coil spring sinks and the chassis suspension of the vehicle is in a stable state.

  3. Light adjustment settings and related operations.

    • For vehicles with dimming switch, the headlight adjusting switch should be placed in "0" gear before dimming;

    • For vehicles without dimming switch (i.e. automatic dimming), check that the light fault indicator on the instrument should not be lit before dimming. If it is lit or flashing, it needs to be repaired and the feedback should be recorded;

    • If the fault indicator light is not lit or flashes, initialize it with a diagnostic instrument.

  4. Connect VDS, enter the diagnostic system, power on the vehicle, and switch the vehicle power supply to OK gear.

  5. Enter the following menu: Model diagnosis/(corresponding model code)/Body module/Headlight adjustment unit/Headlight initialization

Check the headlight lights

Caution

  • There are two shapes of low beam light.They are and . Lower inflection point of cut-off line is optical center of low beam. The optical center of high beam is brightest point.

  • If low beams need to be shielded for adjusting high beams, headlight surfaces cannot be directly shielded or the shielding time cannot exceed 2 minutes as headlight covers are made of plastic, so as to avoid gathering of heat that may cause burn-out of headlight covers.

  1. Position and adjust headlight adjustment tool.

    Reminder

    Refer to headlight adjustment tool User Manual.

    1. Position the headlight adjustment tool at the specified position in front of the headlights of the vehicle (on the same horizontal ground).

    2. Adjust the headlight adjustment tool to be parallel to the longitudinal axis of the vehicle.

    3. Align the coordinate center of the headlight adjustment tool projection screen with the center of the low beam of the vehicle's headlights (at the same height).

  2. Power on the vehicle (OK mode), and turn on the low beam light.

  3. Check that the low beam light should meet the requirements.

    Reminder

    The inspection methods of left and right combined headlights are similar.

    • The horizontal dimming line to the left of the dimming line of the low beam lamp shall coincide with the horizontal line "3" of the detection surface in the headlight adjustment tool.

    • The inflection point between the left horizontal part and the rising part at the light-dark boundary of the low beam lamp shall coincide with the intersection point "1" of the horizontal line and the vertical line of the detection surface in the headlight adjustment tool.

    Reminder

    • To simplify the determination of the inflection point "1", cover the left half of the headlight briefly (viewed along the driving direction), and then expose it again, alternately for many times.

    • If the low beam lights meet the requirements, the beam center of the high beam must be located at "2" above the intersection of horizontal and vertical lines of the detection surface.

    • Before testing and adjusting the headlight, read the instructions of the headlight adjustment tool carefully and operate strictly as required.

Caution

If the headlight lighting does not meet the requirements, the steps of adjusting the headlight lighting must be performed!

Reminder

A new work order is required to adjust the headlight lighting.

Adjust the headlight lighting

Caution

  • For a model with joint adjustment of high and low beams, only low beams need to be adjusted.

  • The shape of low beam light has 2.They are and .Lower inflection point of cut-off line is optical center of low beam.The optical center of high beam is brightest point.

  • If low beams need to be shielded for adjusting high beams, headlight surfaces cannot be directly shielded or the shielding time cannot exceed 2 minutes as headlight covers are made of plastic, so as to avoid gathering of heat that may cause burn-out of headlight covers.

  • During light adjustment, observe beam positions while rotating the adjusting bolt. Make proper adjustment at one time whenever possible. Refrain from continuous and prolonged rotation of the adjusting bolt with an electric screwdriver to prevent need for subsequent readjustment caused by excessive adjustment.

  1. Position and adjust headlight adjustment tool.

    Reminder

    Refer to headlight adjustment tool User Manual.

    1. Position the headlight adjustment tool at the specified position in front of the headlights of the vehicle (on the same horizontal ground).

    2. Adjust the headlight adjustment tool to be parallel to the longitudinal axis of the vehicle.

    3. Align the coordinate center of the headlight adjustment tool projection screen with the center of the low beam of the vehicle's headlights (at the same height).

  2. Power on the vehicle (OK mode), and turn on the low beam light.

  3. Adjust the low beam light to meet the requirements.

    Reminder

    The adjustment methods of the combined headlights on the left and right sides are similar, and the left side is taken as an example here.

    • Before adjustment, remove the left trim panel assembly of front compartment.

    1. Rotate the bolt "b" and adjust the horizontal line to the left of the dimming line of the low beam light to coincide with the horizontal line of the detection surface in the headlight adjustment tool.

    2. Rotate the bolt "a" to adjust the inflection point between the left horizontal part and the rising part at the light-dark boundary of the low beam light to coincide with the intersection point of the horizontal line and the vertical line of the detection surface in the headlight adjustment tool.

    3. Turn the bolt "c" to adjust the beam center of the high beam above the intersection of horizontal and vertical lines of the detection surface in the headlight adjustment tool.

    Caution

    Before testing and adjusting the headlight, read the instructions of the headlight adjustment tool carefully and operate strictly as required.

    Reminder

    • To simplify the determination of the inflection point, cover the left half of the headlight briefly (viewed along the driving direction), and then expose it again, alternately for many times.

    Reminder

    Adjustment of light sources in horizontal and vertical directions refers to adjusting the irradiation range and distance of light sources of the headlight cluster assembly.

    R Right D Down

  4. After headlight adjustment, turn OFF the headlight, switch the vehicle power to OFF gear, then switch the vehicle power to OK gear, and check the headlight lighting again until the light meets the requirements.
