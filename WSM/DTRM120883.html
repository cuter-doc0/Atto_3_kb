Floor Duct Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Remove the rear face duct assembly III. See Rear Face Duct Assembly III - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the left fasteners of the floor duct assembly.

    1. Detach the wiring harness clips.

    2. Remove 2 fixing bolt.

      • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  3. Remove 2 fixing bolts on the right side of the floor duct assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  4. Take out the floor duct assembly.

Installation

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
