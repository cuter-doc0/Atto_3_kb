Front Drive Electric Powertrain Package I - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Power off the high-voltage electrical system. Refer to Power-off and power-on of high voltage electrical system 

  2. Remove the front motor lower mudguard assembly. Refer to Front Motor Lower Mudguard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove the front left half shaft assembly. Refer to Left Front Drive Half Shaft Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  4. Remove the electric compressor assembly. Refer to Electric Compressor Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  5. Remove 2 fixing bolts, and move aside the high-voltage power distribution wiring harness sub-assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1 N•m

  6. Remove 3 fixing bolts of the front drive electric powertrain package I.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1 N•m

  7. Remove 4 fixing bolts and take out the front drive electric powertrain package I.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1 N•m

Installation

  1. Install it in the reverse order of removal.
