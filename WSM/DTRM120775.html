Left Front Window Regulator Bracket Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Remove the left front door glass assembly. See Left Front Door Glass Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the left front door sealing plug II. SeeLeft Front Door Sealing Plug II - Removal and Installation 

  3. Disconnect one connector of the left front window regulator bracket assembly.

  4. Remove the left front window regulator bracket assembly.

    1. Remove one fixing bolt.

      • Tightening torque: 8~10N•m

    2. Remove 4 fixing nuts, and take out the left front window regulator bracket assembly.

      • Tightening torque: 8~10N•m

  5. Where replacing the left front window regulator bracket assembly is required, remove the following accessories of the left front window regulator bracket assembly:

    • Remove the left front window regulator motor. See Left Front Window Regulator Motor - Removal and Installation 

Installation

  1. Follow the removal steps in reverse order to install.

    Caution
    After the installation, test the front window regulator bracket assembly for normal function.
