Tailgate Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Power off the low voltage electrical system. See Power-off and Power-on of Low Voltage Electrical System 

  2. Remove the trunk lid switch. See Trunk Lid Switch - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove the license plate light. See Left License Plate Light - Removal and Installation 

  4. Remove the middle tail light. See Middle Tail Light - Removal and Installation 

  5. Remove the rear wash line sub-assembly IV. See Rear Washing Line Sub-assembly IV - Removal and Installation 

  6. Remove the rear wiper motor and bracket assembly. See Rear Wiper Motor and Bracket Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  7. Remove the tailgate lock body assembly. See Tailgate Lock Body Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  8. Remove the tailgate limit block. See Tailgate Limit Block - Removal and Installation 

  9. Remove the tailgate air spring door bracket. See Tailgate Left Gas Spring Door Bracket - Removal and Installation 

  10. Remove tailgate wiring harness. See Tailgate Wiring Harness - Removal and Installation 

  11. Remove the tailgate glass assembly. See Tailgate Glass Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  12. Remove 4 fixing bolts, and take out the tailgate assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 25±3N•m

    Caution

    • When removing and installing the tailgate assembly, the help of another technician is required.

    • When removing and installing the tailgate assembly, be careful not to scratch the surface material.

Installation

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
