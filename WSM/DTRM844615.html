Inspection of High-Voltage Battery

Warning

  • In case of smoke, heating or fire from the high-voltage battery, immediately call the corresponding regional and national emergency numbers. Do not touch the battery, but evacuate the dangerous area.

  • Prevent skin from directly contacting the leaking electrolyte. Evacuate the dangerous area and keep a safe distance, and ensure sound ventilation in the operating area.

Caution

  • Risk of electric shock! Before operation, high-voltage components in the operating area shall be visually checked.

  • Any work on a vehicle equipped with a high-voltage system shall only be carried out by qualified personnel.

  • In case of any abnormality or doubt, handling shall only be carried out by qualified personnel in HV or electrical aspect.

Repair tools and equipment required

  • Torque wrench (60-340 N•m)

  • Torque wrench (5-50 N•m)

Inspection

  1. Lift the vehicle. Refer to Lifting Vehicle 

  2. Check that the fasteners of the high-voltage battery shield are not lost, and the high-voltage battery shield is free of abnormal deformation, dents or damage.

    Reminder

    • In case of any abnormality, please inform the customer before detailed inspection, and obtain a new work order.

  3. Check the high-voltage battery.

    Reminder

    • In case of any abnormality, please inform the customer before detailed inspection, and obtain a new work order.

    1. Check the appearance of the high-voltage battery for bumps, bulges and damage.

      Caution

      Any dent over 5 mm is a abnormality.

    2. Check that there is no peculiar smell such as irritation and burning around the high-voltage battery.

    3. Check the high-voltage battery for water seepage marks. If any, remove the dirts and dry it.

    4. Check that the fixing bolts of the high-voltage battery are not loose. In case of any abnormality, find out the cause and re-tighten the fixing bolts.

      • Tightening torque: 135 N•m

  4. Check that the fixing bolts of high-voltage battery GND wiring harness are free of looseness and rust, and carry out relevant treatment if necessary.

    • Tightening torque of fixing bolts between the GND wiring harness and high-voltage battery: 18±2 N•m

    • Tightening torque of fixing bolts between the GND wiring harness and vehicle body: 22±2 N•m

    Reminder

    The high-voltage battery GND wiring harness is located on the right side of the high-voltage battery, approximately in the middle.

  5. Obtain a new work order for repair or replacement.
