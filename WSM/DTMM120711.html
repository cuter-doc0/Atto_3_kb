System Function Principle

The full speed adaptive cruise system, with a setting range of 30~150km/h and an operating range of 0~1km/h, can be activated from standstill, and realize adaptive endurance, follow-up and vehicle parking control.

Intelligent Cruise Control (ICC), combining Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) with Lane Keeping System (LKS), provides both vertical and horizontal auxiliary control over a full range of speeds from 0-150 km/h (0-95 mph), eases the driver’s burden, and provides a safe and comfortable driving environment.
