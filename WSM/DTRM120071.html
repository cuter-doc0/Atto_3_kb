Front Passenger Seat Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Power off the low voltage electrical system. See Power-off and Power-on of Low Voltage Electrical System 

  2. Insert the front removal hole with an appropriate tool.

  3. Insert the rear removal hole with an appropriate tool,and detach the connection between seat belt and buckle.

  4. Remove 2 fixing bolts from the front side of the front passenger seat assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 45±5N•m

  5. Remove 2 fixing bolts from the rear side of the front passenger seat assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 45±5N•m

  6. Disconnect the wiring harness from the bottom of the front passenger seat assembly.

    1. Disconnect 5 connectors.

    2. Detach 1 wiring harness clips.

  7. Take out the front passenger seat assembly.

    Caution
    Carefully take out the driver seat assembly and avoid damage to the interior trim. Make two people work together when necessary.

Installation

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
