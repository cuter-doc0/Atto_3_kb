Left Rear Brake Tube II Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

Caution

  • The brake fluid has a strong corrosive effect on the paint. If it is accidentally splashed on the paint of the vehicle body, it must be cleaned with a rag as soon as possible.

  • Avoid oil contact with skin. If it is accidentally splashed into the eyes, immediately rinse with plenty of water and seek medical attention as soon as possible.

  • Do not discharge the oil at will, and recycle it according to local regulations.

  1. Set the intelligent power brake system assembly to "installation mode". See Installation Mode of Intelligent Power Braking System 

  2. Remove the front subframe rear crash bar. See Front Subframe Rear Crash Bar - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove the power battery assembly. See Power Battery Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  4. Discharge brake fluid. See Replacement of Brake Fluid 

  5. Remove 1 tube connector, and detach the tube connection.

    • Tightening torque: 18±2N•m

  6. Remove the pipe clamp from the left rear brake tube II assembly.

    1. Remove 1 single-hole pipe clamp.

    2. Remove 8 two-hole pipe clamps.

  7. Remove 1 tube connector, and take out the left rear brake tube II assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 18±2N•m

Installation

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.

  2. Fill the brake fluid, and drain the brake system. See Air Discharge of Braking System 

Caution

  • After the installation, press the brake pedal several times to ensure that the brake system works normally.

  • Perform road test for inspection.
