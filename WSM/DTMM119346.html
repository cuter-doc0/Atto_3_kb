Safety Instructions

This manual cannot anticipate all possible changes and give all advice or warnings. If you do not follow the instructions in this manual, you must ensure that the selected methods, tools or spare parts will not endanger personal safety, vehicle/equipment performance and environment.

This manual gets the following four prompt symbols according to severity levels and use cases, and each prompt symbol represents a specific meaning.

Reminder
This type of content provides supplementary information that helps to complete a specific procedure, or better results can be achieved by following the prompts.

Caution
This type of content indicates that ignoring this warning may result in an accident or damage to the vehicle/part.

Warning
Such content indicates that ignoring the warning may result in general personal injury, damage to vehicles/parts, or environmental damage.
Danger
Such content indicates that neglect of this warning may result in serious personal injury or death.

For the purpose of this manual, there are many of the said prompt symbols. If a prompt is applicable to many steps, it will appear before such steps. If a prompts is applicable to only one step, it will appear after the step.
