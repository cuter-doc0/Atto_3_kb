EPB Applied and Released for Repair

EPB Released for Repair

Reminder
Before removing and installing the rear brake calipers and the rear brake pad, execute EPB maintenance release first. It may increase the assemble difficulty and result in hardware damage due to accidental EPB activation After the EPB maintenance release, conduct EPB maintenance pull-up to exit the maintenance release status. After exiting the maintenance release state, the EPB system will resume to the normal state, and will be able to respond to all functions.

Method I

  1. Connect VDS to OBD interface.

  2. Set the vehicle power supply to OK, self-checking completed.

  3. Use VDS to set EPB to the “Released for Repair” state.

    1. Connect VDS to the vehicle for communication.

    2. Click “Vehicle Diagnosis System”.

    3. Select “Passenger Car”.

    4. Select the corresponding vehicle series.

    5. Select the corresponding vehicle model.

    6. Select “Scan”.

    7. Select “EPB” module.

    8. Select motion testing.

    9. Select “Repair Release”

    Caution
    After maintenance release, all functions of EPB will be invalid, so as to avoid accidental action during the maintenance process and result in EPB drive assembly damage. After the maintenance is completed and the vehicle is restored to the normal state, execute "maintenance apply" to restore the EPB system to the normal state.

Method II

  1. Press and hold the brake pedal, and the effect shall be “brake light keeps on steadily”.

  2. Press and hold EPB switch for 10 seconds and above, the alarm indicator on the instrument panel will start flashing.

  3. Release EPB switch within 2 seconds after the alarm indicator start flashing.

  4. Start timing after releasing the EPB switch, and press and release EPB switch within 3~5 seconds.

  5. EPB will be fully released, and the alarm indicator keeps on steadily.

  6. Release the brake pedal, operation completed.

    Reminder

    1. Press the brake pedal throughout the whole process.

    2. Press and hold the switch for less than 10 seconds or more than 12 seconds for the first time, the system will automatically restore to the original state, and repeated operation is required.

    3. Press the switch again within 3 seconds or after 5 seconds after the first release, the system will automatically restore to the original state, and repeated operation is required.

    4. After full release, the system will not respond to any function in this case, executing “maintenance apply” or pressing the EPB switch is required to exit the maintenance release state.

    Caution
    After maintenance release in accordance with the above two methods, all functions of EPB will be invalid, so as to avoid accidental action during the maintenance process and result in EPB drive assembly damage. After the maintenance is completed and the vehicle is restored to the normal state, execute "maintenance apply" to restore the EPB system to the normal state.

Method III.

  1. Remove the wheeled EPB motor drive assembly. See Wheeled EPB Motor Drive Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Turn the spline on the brake caliper clockwise with an appropriate tool to release the brake piston. Loosen the brake piston until the thrust screw of brake caliper rotates freely.

    • Torque ≤0.2 Nm

EPB Applied for Repair

Caution
Once the EPB drive assembly is removed from the vehicle, it shall not be driven by connecting to power supply.

Method I

  1. Connect VDS to OBD interface.

  2. Set the vehicle power supply to OK, self-checking completed.

  3. Use VDS to set EPB to the “Applied for Repair” state.

    1. Connect VDS to the vehicle for communication.

    2. Click “Vehicle Diagnosis System”.

    3. Select “Passenger Car”.

    4. Select the corresponding vehicle series.

    5. Select the corresponding vehicle model.

    6. Select “Scan”.

    7. Select “EPB” module.

    8. Select motion testing.

    9. Select “Repair Mode Applied”

Method II

  1. Set the vehicle power supply to OK.

  2. Press and hold the brake pedal, and the effect shall be “brake light keeps on steadily”.

  3. Apply and hold EPB switch for 10 seconds and above, the alarm indicator on the instrument panel will start flashing.

  4. Release the EPB switch, and press down the EPB switch once again within 5 s;

  5. EPB will be applied, the alarm indicator goes out, and EPB state light lights up.

  6. Release the brake pedal, operation completed.

    Reminder

    1. The brake pedal should be depressed in the whole process;

    2. The switch must be held for more than 10 s when being pressed down for the first time, otherwise the system will return to the initial state;

    3. The switch must be pressed down again within 5 s after being released for the first time, otherwise the system will return to the initial state;

    4. After pressing the switch to exit the maintenance release state, the EPB system will resume to the normal state, and will be able to respond to all functions.
