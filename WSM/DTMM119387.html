Safety Measures for Maintenance of High Voltage System

Insulation protective devices

  • Insulating protective clothing.

  • Insulating shoes.

  • Safety goggles.

  • Insulating gloves.

  • Protective mask.

Warning

The maintenance personnel must wear insulation protective devices before operating.

Insulating tools

  • Insulating foot mats.

  • Insulating tool kit.

Maintenance site

  • High voltage warning sign.

  • Carbon dioxide fire extinguisher.

  • Warning line.

  • Special maintenance station ground wire.

Warning

  • Before maintenance, take isolation measures, use warning lines, and set high voltage warning signs to warn irrelevant personnel to stay away and avoid safety accidents.

  • Before maintaining the high voltage equipment, connect the vehicle body to the ground wire of the special maintenance station for electric vehicles.

  • Keep the working environment clean and well ventilated. And keep away from liquids and combustibles.
