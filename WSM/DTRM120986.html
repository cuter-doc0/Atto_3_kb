Condenser Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

Caution
After removing pipeline connections, properly seal all pipe fittings as soon as possible to prevent dust, impurities, and foreign objects from entering the A/C system and causing system faults.

  1. Remove the motor radiator assembly I. See Motor Radiator Assembly I - Removal and Installation 

  2. Recover the refrigerant See Recovery and Filling of Refrigerant 

  3. Remove the radiator upper mount bushing on both sides. See Radiator Upper Mount Bushing - Removal and Installation 

  4. Detach the wiring harness and pipeline connection of the electronic fan and the condenser assembly.

    1. Disconnect 1 connector.

    2. Detach 1 wiring harness clips.

    3. Remove 1 fixing bolt,and detach the connection between the compressor discharge pipe assembly 3 assembly upper and the condenser.

      • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  5. Remove 1 fixing bolt,and detach the connection between the compressor discharge pipe assembly 3 assembly lower and the condenser.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  6. Take out Electronic fan assembly and condenser assembly.

  7. Remove 3 fixing bolts, and take out the condenser assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 5±1N•m

Installation

Caution
For the installation, all “O-rings” of the pipeline must be replaced, and refrigerant oil shall be applied on the surface of the seal rings and the seal rings shall be connected according to the stipulated torque.

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps. To replace the condenser assembly, replenish refrigerant oil when filling refrigerant.
