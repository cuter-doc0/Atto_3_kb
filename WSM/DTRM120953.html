Compressor Suction Pipe Assembly 4 - Removal and Installation

Removal

Caution
After removing pipeline connections, properly seal all pipe fittings as soon as possible to prevent dust, impurities, and foreign objects from entering the A/C system and causing system faults.

  1. Power off the low voltage electrical system. See Power-off and Power-on of Low Voltage Electrical System 

  2. Remove the front subframe rear crash bar. See Front Subframe Rear Crash Bar - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove the refrigeration tube assembly 6. See Refrigeration Tube Assembly 6 - Removal and Installation 

  4. Remove the refrigeration tube assembly 7. See Refrigeration Tube Assembly 7 - Removal and Installation 

  5. Remove the refrigeration tube assembly 1. See Refrigeration Tube Assembly 1 - Removal and Installation 

  6. Detach the wiring harness connection of compressor suction pipe assembly 4.

    1. Disconnect 1 connector.

    2. Detach 1 wiring harness clips.

  7. Detach the connection between compressor suction pipe assembly 4 and compressor suction pipe assembly 3

    1. Remove 1 fixing bolt.

      • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

    2. Remove 2 fixing bolts, and detach the connection between compressor suction pipe assembly 4 and the electric compressor assembly 3.

      • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  8. Remove compressor suction pipe assembly 4.

    1. Disconnect 2 connector.

    2. Remove 1 fixing bolt, detach the compressor suction pipe assembly 4 from the power battery assembly, and take it out.

      • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

Installation

Caution
For the installation, all “O-rings” of the pipeline must be replaced, and refrigerant oil shall be applied on the surface of the seal rings and the seal rings shall be connected according to the stipulated torque.

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
