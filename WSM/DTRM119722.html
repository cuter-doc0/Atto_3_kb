Left Rear Towing Arm Mounting Bracket - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Remove the left rear wheel assembly. See Wheel Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the power battery assembly. See Power Battery Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  3. Detach the floor wiring harness from the rear suspension left towing arm assembly.

  4. Remove the left rear towing arm mounting bracket.

    1. Remove 4 fixing bolt.

      • Tightening torque: 60±5N•m

    2. Remove 4 fixing bolts, and take out the rear suspension left towing arm assembly with the left rear towing arm mounting bracket.

      • Tightening torque: 90±5N•m

  5. Remove 1 nut/bolt, and separate the rear suspension left towing arm assembly from the left rear towing arm mounting bracket.

    • Tightening torque: 90±5N•m

    Caution

    Before removing the mounting bracket of the left rear towing arm, use a marker pen to mark the assembly position in case it is not on the same plane during installation.

Installation

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
