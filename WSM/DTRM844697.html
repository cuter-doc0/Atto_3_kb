Visual inspection of chassis protection

Caution

  • Risk of electric shock! Before operation, high-voltage components in the operating area shall be visually checked.

  • Any work on a vehicle equipped with a high-voltage system shall be only carried out by qualified personnel.

  • Operate carefully to avoid the risk of electric shock.

During visual inspection, attention must be paid to the floor protection layer, mudguards and other baffle trim panels:

  • Check whether all wires or pipelines are secured in the brackets.

  • Check whether all plugs are complete.

  • Check the floor protection layer for damage.

  • Check mudguards and other baffle trim panels for damage.

The identified damage must be repaired to avoid rust and burn-through.
