Precautions

Caution

  • For parts of harness subjecting to relative movement at the corner shall not be stressed tightly.

  • At positions passing through holes and bypass sharp corner, rubber, felt mats or conduit shall be used as protection, so as to protect the harness from wear, for which may result in ground, short circuit or event fire accident.

  • Each terminal must be reliably connected and in good contact.

  • According to the actual installation position of the harness on the vehicle, in order to avoid the sag and displacement of the harness, taking into consideration the weight of the harness, the fixing mode and the fixing position, sufficient anchor points shall be provided and rational fixing mode shall be adopted for fixing.

  • Select the type and size of fixing clips according to the shape and diameter of the harness, and the harness weight shall also be taken into consideration.

  • The clearance with the pipeline shall be even, and the clearance with the surrounding parts shall be reasonable.

  • Direct contact with the sharp edge of the body shall be avoided. First, a certain clearance shall be provided, second, protection shall be provided to prevent the outer insulation of the harness from being damaged by the sharp edge of the body and resulting in short circuit.

  • During the assembly, overstressed harness shall be avoided (especially for harness adopting horizontal arrangement), so as to prevent disconnection/poor contact/conductor parameter change or conductor breakage due to position change of the anchor points caused by bumping during driving.

  • After the harness is assembled, sufficient clearance shall be available around the harness, so as to prevent the harness from being pressed and squeezed by or collide with other parts and their fasteners. Grounding and other fault caused by cut, wear or breakage of harness insulation shall be avoided.

  • Direct exposure of the connector to water, outdoor environment, and oil shall be avoided, where the connector must arranged in aforesaid area, protective measures shall be taken. Rubber connectors, rubber covers or rubber sleeves can be used, or Vaseline can be filled inside the connector to prevent eater from entering the connector and resulting in surface oxidation.

  • Rubber parts on the harness shall be reliable. Through-hole rubber sleeves shall be reliably installed, and in the normal operating condition, the rubber sleeve shall not fall off from the mounting position.

  • For harness at the wheels and under the floor, appropriate additional protections shall be provided. Driving on off-road, gravel road or snowy and icy road in winter shall be avoided, for the flying hard stones and ice blocks may cause damage to the harness.
