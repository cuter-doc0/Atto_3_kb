Ground Wiring Harness of Main Motor Control Module Housing - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Power off the high voltage electrical system. See Power-off and power-on of high voltage electrical system 

  2. Remove the right front wheel assembly. See Wheel Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  3. Remove the right front wheel mudguard assembly. See Right Front Wheel Mudguard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  4. Remove the ground wiring harness of main motor control module housing.

    1. Remove one fixing bolt connecting the ground wiring harness of main motor control module housing to the body.

      • Tightening torque: 22±2N•m

    2. Remove one fixing bolt connecting the ground wiring harness of main motor control module housing to the main motor control module.

      • Tightening torque: 18±2N•m

    3. Release one fixing buckle, and take out the ground wiring harness of main motor control module housing.

Installation

  1. Follow the removal steps in reverse order to install.
