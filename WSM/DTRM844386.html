Inspection of washer fluid anti-freezing temperature

Repair tools and equipment required

  • Coolant freezing point tester (glass cleaning agent anti-freezing detector)

Caution

The test must be carried out in strict accordance with the "User Manual" of the coolant freezing point tester (glass cleaning agent anti-freezing detector).

Reminder

Due to climatic reasons in some countries and regions, there is no need to check the anti-freezing temperature of washer fluid in case of no freezing weather.

Inspection steps

  • Check the anti-freezing temperature of washer fluid with a coolant freezing point tester (glass cleaning agent anti-freezing detector).

  • If the anti-freezing ability of washer fluid is very poor, drain and refill the washer fluid.

Fill the windshield washer fluid (full) according to the customer requirements.

Reminder

  • The original washer fluid shall be filled throughout the year.

  • The finished washer fluid does not need to be diluted with water.

  • The original washer fluid can be used to prevent the nozzle, washer fluid reservoir and connecting hose from freezing.

  • All vehicles equipped with fan-shaped nozzles must be filled with the original windshield washer fluid, because this kind of fluid has a small viscosity below zero. Otherwise, the crystallized washer fluid may block the nozzle system, so that the washer fluid cannot be sprayed in a fan shape. The use of original windshield washer fluid can ensure that the fan-shaped nozzle can still work normally even in a low-temperature environment.

  • Original windshield washer fluid should also be used in warm seasons. Due to powerful cleaning ability, it can remove wax and oil residues on the windshield.

  • The anti-freezing temperature of washer fluid must be below the local minimum ambient temperature.
