Inspection of electrical functions

Inspection contents, such as:

  1. Power on the vehicle (OK mode).

  2. Check that the instrument display screen lights up normally, all kinds of warning lights and indicators are clearly visible, and there is no obvious pixel failure, or display faults such as vertical strips, blank areas or black spots.

  3. Check that the infotainment display screen lights up normally, the touch function is normal, and there is no obvious pixel failure, or display faults such as vertical strips, blank areas or black spots.

  4. Check that the sound system works normally.

  5. Check the function of rearview mirror.

    • Check that the rearview mirror adjustment function is normal.

    • Check that the rearview mirror folding function is normal (if equipped).

    • Check that the rearview mirror heating function is normal (if equipped).

  6. Check that the central locking works normally.

  7. Press the horn button to check that the horn sounds normally.

  8. Check that the A/C temperature and air supply mode are normal, and vent outlets work properly.

  9. Check that the accessory power interface and USB work normally.

  10. Check that the panoramic view system and parking radar work normally.

    • If necessary, conduct inspection in the R gear.

  11. Check that the seat adjustment function is normal.

  12. Check that the on-board fragrance system works normally (if equipped).

    Reminder

    • If the fragrance bottle is exhausted, remind the customer to replace it in time.

  13. Obtain a new work order for repair or replacement.
