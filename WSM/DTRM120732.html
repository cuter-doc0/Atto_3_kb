Rear Small Probe - Removal and Installation

Removal

Reminder
There are 4 rear small probes, and the removal and installation steps are the same.

  1. Remove the rear bumper assembly. See Rear Bumper Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the rear small probe.

    1. Disconnect 1 connectors.

    2. Detach the fixing clip in the direction of the arrow, and take out the rear small probe.

Install

  1. The installation is performed in reverse order of removal.
