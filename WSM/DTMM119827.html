Precautions

Caution

  • Operating EPB in the driving process is prohibited, except for emergency braking.

  • For EPB maintenance, maintenance release is required, in this case, all functions of EPB will be invalid, so as to avoid accidental action during the maintenance process and result in EPB drive assembly damage. After the maintenance is completed and the vehicle is restored to the normal state, execute "maintenance apply" to restore the EPB system to the normal state.

  • The EPB system must be maintained by trained and qualified technicians, and only original parts shall be used for replacement.

  • To avoid accidental activation of the EPB drive assembly during replacement, make sure the harness connectors are disconnected before removing the EPB drive assembly from the brake caliper body.

  • When removing the EPB drive assembly, the O-ring shall be replaced. Remove the O-ring from the O-ring slot on the brake caliper body with a tool with soft tip. When removing the O-ring, make sure not to damage the O-ring slot on the brake caliper body, scratches will result in leakage and drive assembly damage even result in brake failure.

  • New O-rings must be free of dust and contamination. Assembly fluid shall not be used so as to prevent the EPB drive assembly from being contaminated by the fluid. Check O-ring for distortion and tear after installation.

  • When tightening bolts, no torque, shear or tension should be generated between the EPB drive assembly and the caliper body.

  • Anti-loose glue is applied on the bolt threads, which can only ensure that the bolts are locked for the first use. Therefore, old bolts should not be reused. It may result in bolt loose. When installing the EPB drive assembly, new bolts shall be used.

  • When the line connector is connected to the socket of the EPB drive assembly, the maximum thrust and tensile shall not exceed 100N.
