Inspection of Wiper Blade Position

Inspection of initial position of front windshield wiper blade

Inspection steps

  1. Power on the vehicle (OK mode).

  2. Operate the "inching wiping" to run the windshield wiper arm to the terminal position.

  3. Power off the vehicle.

  4. Check that the windshield wiper blade is positioned correctly on the windshield:

    • The wiper blade strip is aligned with the logo "a" on the glass black primer (hollow oblong hole), and the wiper blade does not block the VIN code frame.

    • Deviation: ±5 mm

    If the position of windshield wiper blade is incorrect, be sure to re-adjust the windshield wiper arm. Refer to Front Wiper Left Wiper Arm Assembly - Removal and Installation  or Front Wiper Right Wiper Arm Assembly - Removal and Installation 

    Reminder

    Incorrect position of the wiper blade may affect the wiping effect, even cause component damage or shorten the service life of the blade.

  5. Obtain a new work order for repair or replacement.

Inspection of initial position of rear windshield wiper blade

Inspection steps

  1. Power on the vehicle (OK mode).

  2. Operate the "inching wiping" to run the windshield wiper arm to the terminal position.

  3. Power off the vehicle.

  4. Check that the windshield wiper blade is positioned correctly on the windshield:

    • The wiper blade proximal strip is aligned with the mark "a" on the glass black primer (hollow oblong hole), and the strip is lower than the black edge.

    • Deviation: ±5 mm

    Caution

    If there is no mark on the glass black primer (hollow oblong hole), the middle strip of wiper blade shall be aligned with the upper edge of the glass black primer and the strip shall be lower than the upper edge of the glass black primer.

    • When the middle of the strip is aligned, the deviation is: 0-5 mm

    If the position of windshield wiper blade is incorrect, be sure to re-adjust the windshield wiper arm. Refer to Rear Wiper Arm and Blade Assembly - Removal and Installation 

    Reminder

    Incorrect position of the wiper blade may affect the wiping effect, even cause component damage or shorten the service life of the blade.

  5. Obtain a new work order for repair or replacement.
