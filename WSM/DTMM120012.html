Precautions

Caution

  • The interior trim crow plate shall be used to remove the interior trim. If there is no interior trim crow plate, it can also be replaced by a screwdriver or equivalent, but it shall be wrapped.

  • Since most interior trims are fixed by plastic clips, ensure that all clips have been removed before removing the interior trims.

  • When installing parts, check whether the fixing clips of parts are missing. If so, add acceptable fixing clips.

  • Place interior trims in a clean and flat position to avoid rubbing the paint surface.

  • Check and replace all damaged fixing clips.

  • The connector shall be properly inserted, avoiding virtual insertion or terminal bending, and the secondary locking shall be in place.

Warning

  • Do not remove the interior trim with sharp tools.

  • Never forcibly remove the interior trims.
