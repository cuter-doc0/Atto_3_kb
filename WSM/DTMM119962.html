System Function Principle

Bumpers are used for safety protection, vehicle decoration and improving the aerodynamic characteristics of vehicles. In terms of safety, when a low-speed collision accident occurs, the bumpers can play a buffer role, protect the vehicle body and pedestrians.

  1. Dispersing the impact force: When the vehicle collides, the bumpers are the first contacted, which disperse the force to the energy absorption boxes on both sides, transmit it to the left and right rear longitudinal beams, and then transmit it to other body structures.

  2. Protecting pedestrians: the plastic and foam rear bumper can reduce the injury to pedestrians when they are hit.
