Front Subframe and Power Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

  1. Remove the front motor lower mudguard assembly. See Front Motor Lower Mudguard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  2. Discharge coolant. See Change of Coolant 

  3. Recover the refrigerant See Recovery and Filling of Refrigerant 

  4. Remove the battery assembly. See Battery Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  5. Remove the lower bracket of the battery. See Lower Bracket of Battery - Removal and Installation 

  6. Power off the high voltage electrical system. See Power-off and Power-on of High Voltage Electrical System 

  7. Remove the front subframe rear crash bar. See Front Subframe Rear Crash Bar - Removal and Installation 

  8. Remove the left front wheel. See Wheel Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  9. Remove the right front wheel. See Wheel Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  10. Remove the high voltage distribution wiring harness sub-assembly. See High Voltage Distribution Wiring Harness Sub-assembly - Removal and Installation 

  11. Remove the right front wheel mudguard assembly. See Right Front Wheel Mudguard Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  12. Remove the left and right front stabilizer bar tie rod and ball joint assembly See Stabilizer Bar Tie Rod and Ball Joint Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  13. Remove the left front lower swing arm assembly. See Left Front Lower Swing Arm Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  14. Remove the right front lower swing arm assembly. SeeRight Front Lower Swing Arm Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  15. Remove the left front drive half shaft assembly. See Left Front Drive Half Shaft Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  16. Remove the right front drive half shaft assembly. See Right Front Drive Half Shaft Assembly - Removal and Installation 

  17. Remove the front drive electric assembly package III. See Front Drive Electric Assembly Package III - Removal and Installation 

  18. Remove drive motor control module access cover.

    1. Remove 5 fixed bolts.

      • Tightening torque: 3.5±0.3N•m

    2. Remove 2 fixing bolts, and take out the drive motor control module access cover.

      • Tightening torque: 3.5±0.3N•m

  19. Remove 4 fixing bolts from the integrated AC/DC charging vehicle socket sub-assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  20. Remove one fixing bolt, and detach the integrated AC/DC charging vehicle socket sub-assembly from the drive motor control module assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  21. Disconnect one high voltage connector of the integrated AC/DC charging vehicle socket sub-assembly.

  22. Detach the positive insulating cover.

  23. Remove one fixing nut, and detach the positive power line of the front compartment wiring harness from the drive motor control module assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

  24. Detach 3 fixing clips from the front compartment wiring harness.

  25. Disconnect 4 low voltage connectors, and take out the front compartment wiring harness.

  26. Remove one fixing bolt, and detach the compressor suction pipe assembly 1 from the electric compressor assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

    Caution
    To replace the air conditioner pipeline involves the O-ring of the component, you need to replace the O-ring with a new one every time.

  27. Remove one fixing bolt of the electric compressor assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 22±2N•m

  28. Remove one fixing bolt, and detach the compressor discharge pipe assembly 1 from the electric compressor assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 9±1N•m

    Caution
    To replace the air conditioner pipeline involves the O-ring of the component, you need to replace the O-ring with a new one every time.

  29. Remove one fixing bolt, and take out the ground wiring harness of front motor assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 18±2N•m

  30. Remove one fixing bolt, and take out the ground wiring harness of main motor control module housing.

    • Tightening torque: 18±2N•m

  31. Loosen one fixing clamp, and detach the cooling pipe assembly 5 from the inlet pipe tee.

  32. Detach one fixing clip of cooling pipe assembly 5.

  33. Loosen one fixing clamp, and detach the motor coolant pump inlet pipe assembly from the inlet pipe tee.

  34. Loosen one fixing clamp, and detach the cooling pipe 1 from the inlet pipe tee.

  35. Loosen one fixing bolt,detach the connection between cooling pipe assembly 2 and integrated intelligent front drive motor control module assembly.

  36. Remove the wiring harness.

    1. Disconnect one connector.

    2. Detach 2 fixing clips, and move away the wiring harness.

  37. Remove 2 fixing bolts, and move away the electric coolant pump assembly I.

    • Tightening torque: 10±1N•m

  38. Remove one cotter pin from the left outer ball joint of the steering gear.

  39. Remove one fixing nut, and detach the left outer ball joint of the steering gear from the left front steering knuckle with a tool.

    • Tightening torque: 50±5N•m

  40. Remove one cotter pin from the right outer ball joint of the steering gear.

  41. Remove one fixing nut, and detach the right outer ball joint of the steering gear from the right front steering knuckle with a tool.

    • Tightening torque: 50±5N•m

  42. Remove one fixing bolt, detach the electric power steering column and universal joint assembly from the mechanical steering gear with tie rod assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 70±5N•m

  44. Remove the front subframe and power assembly.

    1. Detach one fixing clip from the front drain.

    2. Remove the front subframe and powertrain 4 fixing bolts.

      • Tightening torque: 200±10N•m

    Caution

    • Use the workbench to support and secure the front subframe and the powertrain, make sure the same is free of swaying.

    • Lift the vehicle slowly, and several technicians shall work together to observe and prevent other accessories of the body from scratching the powertrain.

Installation

  1. Follow the removal steps in reverse order to install.

  2. After the installation, fill in the coolant. See Change of Coolant 

  3. After the installation, fill in the refrigerant. See Recovery and Filling of Refrigerant
