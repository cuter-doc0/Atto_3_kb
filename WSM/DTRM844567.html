Inspection of Brake Disc

Test

Reminder

  • Each time the brake pads are replaced, the condition of the brake discs shall be checked.

  • If there are commercially available tools which make it not necessary to remove the wheel assembly for inspection, such tools are preferred.

Repair tools and equipment required

  • Torque wrench (60-340 N•m)

  • Micrometer

  1. Check the thickness of the front brake disc.

    1. Use a micrometer to measure the thickness of the brake disc 10 mm away from the outer edge of the brake disc. If the minimum measured value is less than the maximum service limit, replace the brake disc.

      Thickness of front brake disc

      Standard thickness

      26±0.1 mm

      Maximum service limit

      24 mm

  2. Check the thickness of the rear brake disc.

    1. Use a micrometer to measure the thickness of the brake disc 10 mm away from the outer edge of the brake disc. If the minimum measured value is less than the maximum service limit, replace the brake disc.

      Thickness of rear brake disc

      Standard thickness

      12 mm

      Maximum service limit

      10 mm

  3. Obtain a new work order for replacement.
