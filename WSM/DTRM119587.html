Radiator Upper Mount Bushing - Removal and Installation

Removal

Reminder
The removal and installation methods of the left and right radiator upper mount bushings are the same. The left side is taken as an example.

  1. Remove radiator upper mount bracket I. See Radiator Upper Mount Bracket I - Removal and Installation 

  2. Remove the upper mounting bushes of radiator.

Install

  1. The installation is performed in reverse order of removal.
