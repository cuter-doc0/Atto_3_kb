Tire Pressure Monitoring Module - Removal and Installation

Removal

Reminder
The removal and installation methods of the tyre pressure monitoring modules of the four wheels are the same. The left front wheel tyre pressure monitoring module is taken as an example.

  1. Remove the tires. See Radial Tire - Removal and Installation 

  2. Pry up the tire pressure monitoring module with the tool from the space between the tire pressure monitoring module and the aluminum alloy wheels.

    Reminder
    When removing, never scratch the surface of the aluminum alloy wheels, so as to avoid air leakage.

Installation

  1. Apply grease on the contact face of the tire pressure monitoring sensor, and install the sensor onto the aluminum alloy wheels.

  2. Conduct the wheel dynamic balancing after the installation.
