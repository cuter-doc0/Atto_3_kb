Handling of IPB System Assembly

Handling

Warning

  • The following contents shall be strictly followed during handling of the IPB system assembly.

  • Failure to follow the correct handling method may cause damage to the IPB system assembly.

  1. Wrong handling method

    1. Do not handle it by holding the mounting plate.

    2. Do not handle it by holding the motor.

    3. Do not handle it by holding the fluid reservoir.

    4. Do not handle it by holding the thrust rod.

    5. Do not handle it by holding the ECU port.

    Items

    Description

    a

    IPB system assembly

    b

    Motor

    c

    Fluid reservoir

    d

    Thrust rod

    e

    ECU port

  2. Correct handling method.

    1. Handling by holding its motor and auxiliary area.

    2. Handling by holding its mounting plate and auxiliary area.

    Items

    Description

    a

    Motor

    b

    Auxiliary area

    c

    Mounting plate

  3. Permissible contact area

    • Contact in the blue area is allowed to keep balance.

    • No contact is allowed in the gray area.
