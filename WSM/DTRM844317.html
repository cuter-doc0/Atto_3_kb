Inspection of exterior light function

Reminder

  • Test the exterior light function with the mirror provided by the authorized service center or with the help of an assistant.

  • If the beam of low beam or high beam is too low or too high, adjust the illumination distance and range of the headlight.

Inspection contents

  1. Power on the vehicle (OK mode).

  2. Check whether the exterior lights work normally.

    • Low beam

    • High beam

    • Fog light

    • Turn signal

    • Position light

    • Daytime running light

    • License plate light

  3. Check whether the manual height adjustment function of headlight (if equipped) works normally.

  4. Press the brake pedal to check whether the stop lights light up, and release the pedal to check whether they go off.

  5. Press the brake pedal and shift to R to check whether the reverse lights light up, and shift out of R to check whether they go off.

  6. Obtain a new work order for repair or replacement.
