Visual inspection of electric powertrain and front compartment components

Caution

  • Risk of electric shock! Before operation, high-voltage components in the operating area shall be visually checked.

  • Any work on a vehicle equipped with a high-voltage system shall be only carried out by qualified personnel.

  • Operate carefully to avoid the risk of electric shock.

Reminder

  • Before inspection, remove the front motor lower mudguard assembly and open the hood.

  • Visually check the electric powertrain (from upper and lower parts) and front compartment components for leakage and damage.

Inspection steps

  1. Power off the vehicle.

  2. Check that the powertrain is free of leakage, bumping or damage.

    • Check that the CV joint oil seal is free of leakage or damage.

  3. Check that the front compartment components are free of leakage, wear or damage, and the bracket is firmly fixed at the correct position.

    • Pipelines, hoses and interfaces

    • Refrigeration and heating systems

    Reminder

    • Eliminate all faults identified within the scope of inspection.

    • In case of an abnormal loss of coolant, it is necessary to determine the reason and eliminate the fault.

  4. Obtain a new work order for repair or replacement.
