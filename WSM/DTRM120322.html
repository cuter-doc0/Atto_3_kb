Rear Wall Step Assembly - Removal and Installation

Removal

Caution
Be careful when removing the trim panel to avoid scratching the surface material.

  1. Remove the tool box in the middle of the trunk. See Tool Box in the Middle of the Trunk - Removal and Installation 

  2. Detach part of the tailgate frame weather strip.

  3. Remove 2 fixing bolts of the rear wall pedal assembly.

    • Tightening torque: 2±0.5N•m

  4. Pry up the rear wall pedal assembly.

Installation

Caution

  • Before installation, it is necessary to check whether all the clips are missing or damaged, and if so, install new clips, and then install the trim panel;

  • After installation, check whether all clips are installed in place. The installation position of the trim panel may deviate, resulting in warping or abnormal gaps.

  1. The installation steps are opposite to the removal steps.
