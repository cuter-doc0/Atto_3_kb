Inspection of tires

Caution

For the sake of driving safety, all wheels must be equipped with radial tires with the same model, size (rolling circumference) and tread pattern as those in the factory status.

Repair tools and equipment required

  • Tire tread ruler

Inspection of tire condition

Caution

When a fault is identified, please check whether it is necessary to replace the tire with a new one.

Inspection items during maintenance

  • Check that the tread and sides of tires are free of damage and foreign matters, such as nails or fragments.

  • Check that the tire is free of fading due to rain wash, the tread is free of wear on one side, the tire wall is free of loose cracks, cuts or punctures, and the tire surface is free of cracks or bulges.

  • Check that the tires are not flat.

  • Check that the tire installation meets the requirements (rotation direction or internal and external positioning).

Reminder
In case of any abnormality, it must be dealt with immediately.

Inspection of tire tread

It can be judged whether it is necessary to check the toe-in and wheel camber angle according to the running status of tires:

  • Burrs on the tire tread indicate incorrect toe-in.

  • In most cases, the serious unilateral wear of tire tread is caused by front wheel camber misalignment.

If such wear is found, determine the cause of fault through four-wheel alignment.

Inspection of tread depth

Check the tread depth:

  • Minimum tread depth: 1.6 mm.

Reminder

It can be checked by measurement with a tire tread ruler or visual inspection of the tire tread and wear marks.

Reminder

  • Due to different regulations, the tread depth varies in individual countries. The requirements of local laws and regulations shall prevail.

  • The minimum tread depth is reached if multiple 1.6 mm high wear marks on the tire circumference no longer have a pattern.

  • If the tread depth is close to the minimum allowed by law, inform the customer of potential risks.

  • If the difference between tread depths is greater than or equal to 2 mm, it is recommended to perform tire rotation. Refer toTire rotation 

Reminder

If any abnormality is found in the above inspection, please obtain a new work order for repair or replacement.
