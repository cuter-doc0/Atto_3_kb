System Function Principle

  1. EPS (Electric Power Assistant Steering, hereinafter referred to as EPS) refers to the steering system that uses EPS motor to provide steering power and assist the driver in steering operation. Like other control systems, this system is composed of sensors (torque angle sensors), control modules (EPS electronic control units), actuators (EPS motors) and related mechanical components.

  2. EPS applies the latest electronic technology and high-performance motor control technology to automobile steering system on the basis of mechanical steering system. On the basis of the original automobile steering system, the following parts are modified and added: EPS electronic control unit, torque and angle sensor, EPS motor, etc. The transmission mechanism of the system is driven by motor, which replaces the traditional mechanical hydraulic mechanism. It can provide real-time steering wheel assistance in various environments.

  3. EPS is usually composed of the following parts: torque and angle sensor, EPS electronic control unit, EPS motor and related mechanical structures. EPS is powered by EPS motor, where the power assistance is adjusted and controlled in real time by EPS electronic control unit. Different power assistance is provided according to different vehicle speeds to improve the steering characteristics, reduce the control force in parking and low-speed driving, improve the steering control stability in high-speed driving, and then improve the active safety of the vehicle.

The EPS system mainly gets the following functions:

Boost control

EPS is speed sensitive, that is, under the same steering wheel torque input, the target current of the motor changes with the vehicle speed, which can better meet the requirements of portability and road feel. EPS adopts segmented power assistance. EPS motor applies power torque according to the deviation direction of steering wheel, so as to ensure light steering at low speed, stable operation at high speed and better road feel.

Return control

During steering, the steering wheel supports automatic return due to kingpin caster angle and kingpin inclination angle. EPS motor and reduction mechanism are added on the basis of mechanical steering mechanism. EPS, through the EPS electronic control unit, controls the EPS motor, and carries out the steering return action together with the return torque generated by the front wheel positioning, so as to make the steering wheel return quickly, suppress the steering wheel oscillation, maintain the road feel, improve the steering sensitivity and stability, optimize the steering return characteristics, and shorten the convergence time. The return control generates the return torque by adjusting the return compensation current, which makes the steering wheel return in a certain direction.

Damping control

When the vehicle is running at high speed, the damping control is carried out by controlling the damping compensation current to enhance the road feel and improve the steering stability.
