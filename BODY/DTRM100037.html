Replacement of Left Door Sill Inner Panel Assembly

Removal

  1. Remove the left side wall assembly. See  

  2. Clear 37 welding spots, and take out the reinforcement plate of left door sill inner panel.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Clear the welding spots (new panel in the outside)

  3. Clear 7 welding spots, and take out the left door sill inner panel assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Clear the welding spots (new panel in the outside)

Installation

  1. Secure the left door sill inner panel assembly to the mounting position.

  2. Check and adjust the relative position and dimensions of the left door sill inner panel assembly and the surrounding parts.

  3. Weld 7 welding spots on the left door sill inner panel assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Spot welding (new panel in the outside)

  4. Fix the reinforcement plate of left door sill inner panel to the mounting position.

  5. Check and adjust the relative position and dimensions of the reinforcement plate of left door sill inner panel and the surrounding parts.

  6. Weld 37 welding spots on the reinforcement plate of left door sill inner panel.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Spot welding (new panel in the outside)

  7. Apply body sealant and internal coating at corresponding positions.

  8. After the external coating is applied, the anti rust agent shall be applied on the inner panel of the welding spots of the closed section structure.
