Overview of Painting

Definition of Painting

Reminder
During vehicle painting and maintenance, the construction personnel will use paint materials, painting equipment and tools. They will inevitably come into contact with toxic and harmful substances. If the personnel do not fully understand the basic knowledge of painting, the composition of paint, properly use the equipment, and take protective measures, it may lead to coating defects, sudden work accidents and serious occupational diseases.

  1. Painting.

    • Painting refers to the process of applying a thin layer of liquid paint on the surface of workpiece, through drying and curing, forming a hard film layer.

Purpose of Painting

  1. Knowledge points.

    • Protective materials: the surface of materials such as iron, aluminum, wood or plastic can be protected by paint to prevent rust and corrosion and to prolong the service life.

    • Improve appearance: provide an object with color and gloss, so as to improve its visual effect.

    • Identification: coat the vehicle body with specific color to facilitate identification, such as patrol cars, emergency ambulances.
