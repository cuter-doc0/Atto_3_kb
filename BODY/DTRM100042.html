Replacement of Right Rear Longitudinal Member Assembly

Removal

  1. Remove the middle floor front beam assembly. See Replacement of Middle Floor Front Beam Assembly 

  2. Remove the rear wall panel assembly. See Replacement of Rear Wall Panel Assembly 

  3. Remove the right side wall assembly. See  

  4. Remove the rear floor right sealing plate. See Replacement of Rear Floor Right Sealing Plate 

  5. Clear 79 welding spots from the right rear longitudinal member assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Clear the welding spots (new panel in the outside)

  6. Clear 5 welding spots from the right rear longitudinal member assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Clear the welding spots (new panel in the outside)

  7. Clear 9 welding spots, and take out the right rear longitudinal member assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Clear the welding spots (new panel in the outside)

Installation

  1. Fix the right rear longitudinal member assembly to the mounting position.

  2. Check and adjust the relative position and dimensions of the right rear longitudinal member assembly and the surrounding parts. See Underbody 

  3. Weld 9 welding spots on the right rear longitudinal member assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Spot welding (new panel in the outside)

  4. Weld 5 welding spots on the right rear longitudinal member assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Spot welding (new panel in the outside)

  5. Weld 79 welding spots on the right rear longitudinal member assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Spot welding (new panel in the outside)

  6. Apply body sealant and internal coating at corresponding positions.

  7. After the external coating is applied, the anti rust agent shall be applied on the inner panel of the welding spots of the closed section structure.
