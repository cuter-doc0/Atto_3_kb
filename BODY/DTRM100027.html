Replacement of Rear Wall Panel Assembly

Removal

  1. Clear 62 welding spots from the rear wall panel assembly.

  2. Clear 50 welding spots from the rear wall panel assembly, and take out the rear wall panel assembly.

Installation

  1. Secure the rear wall panel assembly to the mounting position.

  2. Check and adjust the relative position and dimensions of rear wall panel assembly and surrounding parts. See Rear Compartment 

  3. Weld 50 welding spots on the rear wall panel assembly.

  4. Weld 62 welding spots on the rear wall panel assembly.

  5. Apply body sealant and internal coating at corresponding positions.

  6. After the external coating is applied, the anti rust agent shall be applied on the inner panel of the welding spots of the closed section structure.
