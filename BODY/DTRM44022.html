Painting Equipment and Tools

Spray Booth

  1. Knowledge points.

    • Low temperature paint repair is adopted for vehicle maintenance, and the baking temperature is generally at 60-80°C.

    • By structure, it can be divided into chamber-type and channel-type.

    • The chamber-type spray booth integrates spraying and baking.

    • The channel-type paint booth is a production line where vehicle enters from one side for paint spraying and exits from the other side for baking.

    • By energy type, it can be divided into fuel type, gas type and electric heat type.

    • By filtering type, it can be divided into wet filtering and dry filtering.

  2. Operational points.

    • The spray booth generally adopts positive pressure air supply, and the supply pressure is generally kept at 4-12bar higher than the outdoor air. Positive pressure air supply can prevent the outdoor air from enter the spray booth and keep the spray booth clean. The pressure is regulated through the damper.

    • During spraying, the air flow rate shall reach 0.2-0.6m/s, and during heating and baking, the air flow rate is generally 0.05m/s.

    • The lighting in the spray booth shall reach 800lx and above. Generally, multiple groups of fluorescent lights are installed on both sides of the roof. In addition, the noise in the spray booth during operation shall not exceed 80dB.

    • The best spraying condition for water-based paint is a temperature of 25°C and a relative humidity less than 70%.

    Caution

    • All spraying personnel shall be familiar with the operation process of the spray booth. Untrained personnel shall not work in the spray booth. Start the spray booth according to the specification.

    • Smoking and open fire are prohibited in the spray booth. Non workers are prohibited from entering the booth. Operators shall wear anti-static work clothes, activated carbon protective masks, protective goggles, safety boots and other protective equipment.

    • The vehicle must be cleaned before entering the spray booth. Never stick, grind or polish in the spray booth, which will pollute the spray booth.

    • During spraying operation, close the doors of the spray booth. Never enter and exit the spray booth at will. After the operation, take out all the remaining paints in the spray booth. Never heat up and bake the paints in the spray booth.

    • After spraying operation in the spray booth, clean away all sundries, such as masking paper and residual waste. Coil the compressed air supply hose and hang it on the spray gun bracket.

    • The use of short wave infrared or other thermal radiation equipment will greatly improve the drying speed of the paint film, and has the advantages of thorough drying and consistency inside and outside. Check the circuits of the thermal radiation drying equipment every year and determine whether they need to be replaced. The power circuit of the thermal radiation equipment has the problem of rapid aging.

    • Clean the spray booth once a day. Remove all sundries. Vacuum the floor, wall, grille, etc. And ensure the booth is clean and tidy.

    Reminder

    • For the maintenance and regular service of the spray booth, the main power switch of the spray booth must be cut off.

    • Clean and replace the filter cotton at the air inlet regularly according to the manufacturer's requirements, where the general service life is 60-80h.

    • Replace the top filter cotton regularly according to the manufacturer's requirements, where the general service life is 350-400h.

    • Replace the bottle filter cotton regularly according to the manufacturer's requirements, where the general service life is 80-400h.

    • Check the furnace of the spray booth regularly for carbon residue inside according to the manufacturer's requirements. Remove the carbon residue inside if necessary.

    • Check the activated carbon filter screen in the activated carbon cabinet regularly every quarter. If the surface is covered with paint, replace it in time to ensure smooth ventilation.

    • Check all wires and cables in the spray booth every six months, and replace them if they are aging.

    • Clean the whole spray booth every six months. Check the circulating air valve, air inlet and exhaust fan bearings. Check the smoke exhaust channel of the burner. Clean the sediment in the oil tank. Clean the water-based protective film in the spray booth and spray it again.

Compressed Air Supply System

Air compressor

  1. Knowledge points.

    • By mechanical movement mode, the air compressor can be divided into two types, including reciprocating piston air compressor and screw-type air compressor.

    • The reciprocating piston air compressor compresses air by means of reciprocating movement of piston, which is featured by medium air flow, rapid performance degradation with the operation time, and oil or oil vapor may enter the compressed air pipeline.

    • The screw-type air compressor produces pressure by means of high-speed movement of two uneven rotors, which is featured by constant air pressure and air flow, low noise, large air output, clean air, energy saving and high efficiency. It is suitable for the maintenance shop with large gas consumption.

  2. Operation and daily maintenance.

    • The air compressor should be installed in a room with good ventilation and heat dissipation, and ensure that the air intake is clean. The temperature should not exceed 40°C during normal operation.

    • The air compressor should be installed at least 30cm away from the wall, so as to facilitate overhaul, maintenance and heat dissipation.

    • Drainage pipes shall be provided to the air compressor for efficient discharge of water from the air compressor, air storage cylinder, and freeze drying machine, so as to ensure the cleanliness of the air compressor room.

  3. Daily maintenance of air compressor.

    • Drain water from air storage cylinder, oil-water separator, and freeze drying machine.

    • Check and confirm the oil level of crankcase falls between the MAX and the MIN marks of the dipstick.

    • Clean dust on the air compressor.

  4. Monthly maintenance of air compressor.

    • Clean the air filter element.

    • Check and add oil.

    • Check the air pressure gauge for normal function.

    • Check the safety valve of air storage cylinder for normal function.

    • Check the tightness of the V-belt and adjust it as necessary.

    • Check the accessories and air supply pipelines of air compressor for oil leakage and air leakage.

Air compressor system support equipment

  1. Knowledge points.

    • The compressed air output from the air compressor should first enter the air storage cylinder for temporary storage. The air storage cylinder is equivalent to an energy storage device, which can stabilize the pressure and standardize the air flow.

    • Generally, the air storage cylinder used in an aftersales workshop is 1-2m³, and the working pressure is 1MPa. The specific selection depends on the number and use frequency of pneumatic tools, i.e., it is related to the consumption of compressed air.

Freezer drying machine

  1. Knowledge points.

    • The temperature of air from the air compressor reaches up to 100-150°C, and only when the temperature of the compressed air is reduced to below the dew point, the oil and water contained in the compressed air will liquefy to oil drops and water drops which can be filtered and discharged.

    • Specifications of the freeze drying machine used in a workshop with a maintenance capacity of 400 vehicles: processing capacity is 3.5-4.0m³/min, working pressure: 1.3mpa.

Precision filter

  1. Knowledge points.

    • For precision filters, different filtering grades are available, a coarse filter can remove dust with a particle size up to 1μm, and remove oil up to 1x10-6. Fine filter can remove dust with a particle size up to 0.01 μm, remove oil up to 0.1x10-6. ultra-fine filter can remove remove oil up to 0.003X10-6.

    • For a maintenance workshop with a monthly maintenance capacity of 400 vehicles, the processing amount of precision filter should reach 3.5-4m³/min.

Oil-water separator

  1. Knowledge points.

    • In order to ensure high quality spraying effect, the compressed air shall be dry and free of dust, therefore, an oil-water separator must be installed on the air supply pipeline at upstream where the air compressor enters the spray booth.

    • The oil-water separator may separate water and oil through the guide plates, centrifuge, expansion chamber, vibration plate and filter inside the device and discharge the same manually through automatic drainage valve, so as to ensure the output air is clean and dry.

Compressed Air Supply System

  1. Knowledge points.

    • The air supply system refers to the combination of equipment, equipment and pipelines from the air compressor to each work station of workshop, also include air supply pipeline, connectors and valves.

    • The air supply main shall be set in a circular form above the workshop, so as to ensure the constant and stable pressure supply to the workshop; the pipe diameter should be calculated and determined based on the consumption of air compressor, and in general, the inner diameter shall reach 2-3in (1in=25.4mm), and the air supply main pipeline shall be able to withstand a pressure of 1.2-1.6MPa. It is recommended that galvanized pipes, stainless steel pipes, modified PVC pipes or aluminum alloy pipes should be used.

    • The air supply main should gradually slope down towards the drainage end, with a gradient of 1/100, and an automatic drainage valve should be set at the drainage end.

    • The air supply pipeline should be as short as possible, the pipeline shall be laid in straight line as far as possible, and the number of elbows and valves shall be minimized, for these may result in pressure loss.

    • The air supply branch shall be lead from the supply main through an upside down U section to a height required by the work station, and in general, the inner diameter of the pipe shall reach 1-2in (1in=25.4mm).

    • Am oil-water separator shall be installed between the branch and the rubber hose for further oil-water separation, so as to ensure the grinding and spraying quality. In general, common work stations for grinding and dust removal should be provided with a single-section oil-water separator, and work station for spraying should be provided with a two-section or three section oil-water separator.

Air supply hose

  1. Knowledge points.

    • The inner diameter of rubber hoses shall be 8-10mm. If a HVLP spray gun is used, it is recommended that a rubber hose with a inner diameter of 10mm should be used to ensure sufficient compressed air supply. The length of the rubber hose should not exceed 10m to avoid output pressure reduction.

    • Never stand on or bend the hose during spraying. Never make the hose contact the plate surface to be painted.

Dry-Grinding System

Grinding equipment and tool

  1. Knowledge points.

    • During car repair and coating, grind and remove the aged or thick old film on the workpiece, so as to ensure the coating quality.

    • Grind the putty for trimming and shaping.

    • Grind the smooth coating surface to increase adhesion between coatings.

Dust-free dry grinding system

  1. Knowledge points.

    • The dust-free dry grinding system is composed of grinding tools, air supply and dust collection pipes, dust collection equipment, grinding sandpaper and auxiliary systems.

    • By the driving mode, grinding tools can be divided into two types, including pneumatic type and electric type. Pneumatic tools are widely used thanks to their long service life, easy to use, simple maintenance and safety.

    • By the motion mode, grinding tools can be divided into one-way rotary grinder, rail grinder and eccentric vibration grinder.

    • The one-way rotary grinder is also called single-action grinder, and the grinding pad perform one-way circular motion, which is mainly used for removing rust and old coating.

    • Rail grinder: the grinding pad reciprocates along the elliptical trajectory, providing uniform grinding force and cutting force at each part and avoid uneven grinding, which is mainly used for putty shaping and grinding.

    • Eccentric vibration grinder is also called double-action grinder, and the grinding pad rotates about the rotary shaft. The rotary shaft is a eccentric shaft, so that the grinding pad performs double circular motion. The double-action grinder applies to different applications according to the eccentric distance, greater eccentricity is suitable for coarse grinding (putty), and smaller eccentricity is suitable for fine grinding (intermediate primer).

Drying Equipment

Drying Equipment

  1. Knowledge points.

    • The principle of radiation drying is to directly radiate the heat energy onto the object to be heated in the form of electromagnetic waves, use the radiant heat to heat and dry the object. Generally, infrared, and far infrared heating equipment is used.

    • The advantage of shortwave infrared ray is that the infrared radiate can penetrate paint film surface, reach metal surface and then reflect, so that the bottom layer of coating can absorb energy to heat up first, and the paint film can be dried from inside to outside.

  2. Operational points.

    • For the distance between the radiation source and the heating surface, in general, the distance between the hot light and the workpiece surface shall be greater than 50cm, short distance will cause rapid temperature rise, resulting in bubbles, pinholes and rashes on putty.

    • Different colors provide different reflectance and absorption rates, and different infrared ray absorption rate will provide different drying speeds. For example: dark colors dry faster than light colors.

Blower

  1. Knowledge points.

    • It is used to dry the paint surface after applying water-based paint.

    • Based on the Venturi principle, in addition to compressed air supply to the system, the air blower also intakes and discharges large amount of air from and to the surrounding environment, so as to dry the water-based paint efficiently and effectively.

    Reminder

    • Generally, the air consumption is 270 L/min, and the airflow of the blower is about 3000 L/min.

    • The blowing direction of the blower must be consistent with that of the spray booth.

    • Blowing distance: 30-80cm.

Blower bracket

  1. Knowledge points.

    • The mobile blower system is suitable for being used in spray booths and work stations in workshop. The blower can aim the workpiece directly, turn on the air source, and the blowing system can complete the water-base paint drying independently.

    Reminder

    • Appropriate air flow rate blowing to the plate will take away more moisture in the paint film, ensure that the inner layer of water-based paint is dried more evenly and avoid too dry paint film surface.

    • The blowing direction of the blower must be consistent with that of the spray booth. Place the blower above the top of most of the plates.

Spraying Equipment

Spray gun

  1. Knowledge points.

    • It is used for applying paint. It is an important tool precisely designed and manufactured for painting repair, and the atomization ability of the spray gun significantly affect the coating and repair effect.

    • Various types and specifications of the spray gun are available, and the spray gun should be reasonably selected according to the type of paint and the construction process.

  2. Operational points.

    • Select an appropriate spray gun type according to the paint type, including primer spray gun, water-based or solvent-based spray gun and topcoat spray gun.

Gun tail meter

  1. Knowledge points.

    • It is used to adjust the air supply pressure of the spray gun. Reasonable spray pressure is an important factor to ensure ideal atomization of spray gun during construction. A barometer can be installed at the tail of the spray gun to measure and monitor the stability of air pressure.

Auxiliary Tools and Materials

Tack cloth

  1. Knowledge points.

    • It is used to remove foreign matters or dust from sprayed surface.

  2. Operational points.

    • Tack cloth is a gauze soaked in a viscous scavenger.

    Reminder

    • To use a piece of new tack cloth, spread it flat, and then fold it again to make it soft.

Measuring glass

  1. Knowledge points.

    • It is used to mix paint. A mixing cup of appropriate volume should be selected before mixing the paint. For water-based paint, metal container shall be avoided. A plastic container or a mixing cup with coating shall be used for paint mixing or storage.

    Reminder

    • Disposable plastic cups are usually used.

Mixing ruler

  1. Knowledge points.

    • It is used for paint mixing. a suitable paint ruler shall be selected according to the volume of paint to be mixed, a scale is provided on the mixing ruler, which facilitate paint mixing by volume.

Electronic scale

  1. Knowledge points.

    • It is used for paint preparation and measurement. The electronic scale should be calibrated regularly to ensure accuracy. Overload shall be avoided, and frequent overload will lead to accuracy degradation of the electronic scale.

    • The electronic scale should be placed level on the paint mixing table. When using the scale, air flow around the scale shall be avoided. Otherwise, it may result in inaccurate weighing.

Filtering funnel

  1. Knowledge points.

    • Filter the paint and remove impurities from the paint before pouring the same into the spray gun.

    Reminder

    • The paper funnel has a variety of meshes. Select the appropriate mesh size according to the paint type.

    • For water-based paints, a filter funnel of about 125㎛ is recommended.

Air blower

  1. Knowledge points.

    • Remove dirt and dust by air pressure.

Spare parts support

  1. Knowledge points.

    • It is used to spray new parts to be installed. It can adjust the support point of the spare parts support, so as to facilitate plate spraying and grinding.
