Replacement of Right Front Wheel Casing Assembly

Removal

  1. Remove the right front wheel casing upper beam assembly. See Replacement of Upper Beam Assembly of Right Front Wheel Casing 

  2. Clear 23 welding spots, and take out the right side wall front connecting plate assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Clear the welding spots (new panel in the outside)

  3. Clear 33 welding spots from the outside of the right front wheel casing assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Clear the welding spots (new panel in the outside)

  4. Clear 7 welding spots from the inside of the right front wheel casing assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Clear the welding spots (new panel in the outside)

  5. Clear 13 welding spots, and take out the right front wheel casing assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Clear the welding spots (new panel in the outside)

Installation

  1. Fix the right front wheel casing assembly to the mounting position.

  2. Check and adjust the relative position and dimensions of the right front wheel casing assembly and the surrounding parts. See Front Compartment 

  3. Weld 13 welding spots on the upper part of the right front wheel casing assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Spot welding (new panel in the outside)

  4. Weld 7 welding spots on the inside of the right front wheel casing assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Spot welding (new panel in the outside)

  5. Weld 33 welding spots on the outside of the right front wheel casing assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Spot welding (new panel in the outside)

  6. Secure the right side wall front connecting plate assembly to the mounting position.

  7. Check and adjust the relative position and dimensions of the right side wall front connecting plate assembly and the surrounding parts. See Front Door 

  8. Weld 23 welding spots on the right side wall front connecting plate assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Spot welding (new panel in the outside)

  9. Apply body sealant and internal coating at corresponding positions.

  10. After the external coating is applied, the anti rust agent shall be applied on the inner panel of the welding spots of the closed section structure.
