Replacement of Front Wall Reinforcement Beam Assembly

Removal

  1. Remove left front longitudinal member front assembly. See Replacement of Left Front Longitudinal Member Front Section Assembly 

  2. Remove the left front longitudinal member sealing plate assembly. See Replacement of Left Front Longitudinal Member Sealing Plate Assembly 

  3. Remove right front longitudinal member front assembly. See Replacement of Right Front Longitudinal Member Front Section Assembly 

  4. Remove the right front longitudinal member sealing plate assembly. See Replacement of Front Right Longitudinal Member Sealing Plate Assembly 

  5. Remove the left front wheel house assembly. See Replacement of Left Front Wheel Casing Assembly 

  6. Remove the right front wheel house assembly. See Replacement of Right Front Wheel Casing Assembly 

  7. Clear 3 welding points of the front wall reinforcement beam assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Clear the welding spots (new panel in the outside)

  8. Clear 45 welding points and take out the front wall reinforcement beam assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Clear the welding spots (new panel in the outside)

Installation

  1. Fix the front wall reinforcement beam assembly to the mounting position.

  2. Check and adjust the relative position and size of the front wall reinforcement beam assembly and the surrounding parts.

  3. Weld 45 welding points of the front wall reinforcement beam assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Spot welding (new panel in the outside)

  4. Weld 3 welding points of the front wall reinforcement beam assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Spot welding (new panel in the outside)

  5. Apply body sealant and internal coating at corresponding positions.

  6. After the external coating is applied, the anti rust agent shall be applied on the inner panel of the welding spots of the closed section structure.
