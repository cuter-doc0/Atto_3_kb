ALAXTA Solvent-Based Anvanc Series Paint Construction Flow Chart

Items

Process standard

Body with internal anti-rust treatment and painting for sheet metal completed

Check vehicle body condition and planned construction requirements

Grease and wax removing

(AC960)

Clean with AC960 and cleaning cloth, and adopt the cleaning method of wet clean combining dry clean (AC960 can only be used for cleaning before grinding).

Bevel edges grinding and finishing

Grind the position subjected to sheet metal repair and putty filling with Audurra sandpaper P80/P120-180 (remove excessive or defect paint on body as necessary)

Dust removing and cleaning

(AC960)

Clean dust and dirt on the vehicle body with a compressed air gun and degreaser AC960

Cover and protection

Properly cover and protect the positions without needing repair as necessary

Putty filling

(AC510)

Mix 2K sheet metal putty (adding curing agent AC520 according to a mixing proportion of 100:1-3%), fill it into the dents on the body, and then bake it dry or let it dry naturally (at 25 °C) for 10-15 minutes.

Grinding putty

Grind the dried putty with Audurra sandpaper P80/P120-P180-P240, and check the putty for flatness, pinhole and sandpaper mark by feeling with hands or according to the grinding indication layer.

Putty application

(AC510)

If necessary, apply a thin layer of putty AC510 to fill pinholes, sandpaper marks and small dents (note: it can only be applied on primer/putty).

Grinding

Thoroughly grind the old paint on the vehicle body where to be sprayed with intermediate coat with Audurra sandpaper P280/P320

Dust removing and cleaning

(AC960)

Clean dust and dirt on the vehicle body with a compressed air gun and degreaser AC960

Cover and protection

Paste the masking paper by adopting the reverse pasting technique as necessary

Spray intermediate coat

(AC710)

Mix 2K high curing agent proportion and fast drying intermediate primer

Grinding process: add curing agent AC810/AC811 and diluent AC910/AC911 according to the mixing ratio of 4:1:5-10%, spray 1-2 layers, wait for 10-15 minutes for each layer, and then bake it dry at 60°C for 30 minutes or at 20°C for 1-2 hours.

Indication layer spraying and grinding

Before grinding, spray a thin layer of grinding indication layer for checking the grinding effect.

Intermediate coat grinding

Grind the fully dried intermediate coat the Audurra sandpaper P400-500, and small area should be grind with gray scouring pad or sponge sandpaper P600-800 as necessary.

Inspection, dust removing and cleaning

(AC960)

Check the grinding effect, conduct the fine filling and grinding as necessary, clean dust and dirt on the vehicle body with a compressed air gun and degreaser AC960.

Cover and protection

Cover the vehicle body properly

Dust removing and cleaning

Purge the dust spot on the vehicle body with a compressed air gun, clean dirt on vehicle body with silica removal detergent AC960, blow dust out of gaps and slots on the body with compressed air gun, and remove dust on the vehicle body with tack cloth.

Two-process base coat

(Anvanc two-process base coat)

Mix Anvanc two-process primer according to the mix proportion of 1:0.6-0.8(AC920/AC921), and spray 2-3 layers. Note: the next course of base coat shall be sprayed after the previous course is flash-off to the matte state.

Fully dry with a blower, and then spray vanish.

Varnish spraying

(AC610/AC660/AC680)

Mix and spray 2 layers of AC610 standard varnish/AC660 efficient varnish (adding curing agent AC810(fast)/AC811(standard) according to the mixing ratio of2:1), wait for 5 minutes before spraying the next layer.

Bake drying

Set it aside for 5 minutes and then dry at 20°C for 15-30 minutes or at 60°C for 20-30 minutes according to product requirements

Waxing and polishing

Grind minor sagging or dust spots with polishing sandpaper P1200-1500 as necessary, and remove sandpaper marks and polish with Audurra polishing wax A8.

ALAXTA Water-Based Syrox Series Paint Construction Flow Chart

Items

Process standard

Body with internal anti-rust treatment and painting for sheet metal completed

Check vehicle body condition and planned construction requirements

Grease and wax removing

Clean with detergent and cleaning cloth, and adopt the cleaning method of wet clean combining dry clean.

Bevel edges grinding and finishing

Grind the position subjected to sheet metal repair and putty filling with Audurra sandpaper P80/P120-180 (remove excessive or defect paint on body as necessary)

Dust removing and cleaning

Clean dust and dirt on the vehicle body with a compressed air gun and degreaser.

Cover and protection

Properly cover and protect the positions without needing repair as necessary

Putty filling

(S1100)

Mix 2K sheet metal putty (adding curing agent H-100 according to a mixing proportion of 100:2-3%), fill it into the dents on the vehicle body, and then let it dry naturally at 20°C for 10-15 minutes or bake it dry with SW infrared paint curing light for 2-3 minutes (half power).

Grinding putty

Grind the dried putty with Audurra sandpaper P80/P120-P180-P240, and check the putty for flatness, pinhole and sandpaper mark by feeling with hands or according to the grinding indication layer.

Putty application

(S1100)

If necessary, apply a thin layer of putty S1100 to fill pinholes, sandpaper marks and small dents (note: it can only be applied on primer/putty).

Grinding

Thoroughly grind the old paint on the vehicle body where to be sprayed with intermediate coat with sandpaper P280/P320

Dust removing and cleaning

Clean dust and dirt on the vehicle body with a compressed air gun and degreaser AC960

Cover and protection

Paste the masking paper by adopting the reverse pasting technique as necessary

Spray intermediate coat

(S3020/S3060)

Mix S3020 environment friendly light-gray intermediate primer or S3060 environment friendly dark-gray intermediate primer

Grinding process: add curing agent S6000(slow)/S6010(standard)/S6020(fast) and S8060(extra-fast)/S8080(standard)/S8090(fast) diluent according to the mixing proportion of 4:1:0-10%, spray 1-3 layers, wait for 5-10 minutes before spraying the next layer. Then bake it dry at 20°C for 12-16h, or at 60-65°C for 25-30 minutes.

Indication layer spraying and grinding

Before grinding, spray a thin layer of grinding indication layer for checking the grinding effect.

Intermediate coat grinding

Grind the fully dried intermediate coat the Audurra sandpaper P400-500, and small area should be grind with gray scouring pad or sponge sandpaper P600-800 as necessary.

Inspection, dust removing and cleaning

Check the grinding effect, conduct the fine filling and grinding as necessary, clean dust and dirt on the vehicle body with a compressed air gun and degreaser AC960.

Cover and protection

Cover the vehicle body properly

Dust removing and cleaning

Purge the dust spot on the vehicle body with a compressed air gun, clean dirt on vehicle body with silica removal detergent, blow dust out of gaps and slots on the body with compressed air gun, and remove dust on the vehicle body with tack cloth.

Two-process base coat

(S100-S940 water-based paint)

Add 20% S940 water-based viscosity modifier, and spray 2.5 courses of paint in the wet-on-dry mode

Note: the next course of base coat shall be sprayed or bake drying can be started after the previous course is flash-off to the matte state.

Varnish spraying

(S5000/S5800)

Mix and spray 2 layers of S5000 environment friendly all-purpose varnish/S5800 bright varnish (adding curing agent S6020(fast)/S6010(standard) according to the mixing proportion of 2:1), and wait for 5-10 minutes before spraying the next layer.

Bake drying

Set it aside for 5 minutes and then dry at 25°C for 30 minutes or at 60°C for 30 minutes according to product requirements

Waxing and polishing

Grind minor sagging or dust spots with polishing sandpaper P1200-1500 as necessary, and remove sandpaper marks and polish with polishing wax A8.
