Basic Maintenance Process of Body Steel Plate Damages

Manual Shaping

Reminder

  • Master the fundamental using methods of tools and equipment, and get familiar with various tips of the maintenance process.

  • Basic process of manual shaping maintenance:damage judgment — accessory removal — shaping — trimming and grinding — grinding effect.

Damage judgment

Reminder
There are four ways of damage judgment: visual judgment, touch judgment, ruler judgment and profile gauge judgment.

  1. Visual judgment.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • Be able to judge the damage conditions quickly.

      • Angles and positions can influence the judgment results.

    2. Operation steps.

      • Select suitable light source.

      • Observe the deformed area in the front view.

      • Observe the deformed area in the side view.

  2. Touch judgment.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • You can judge the dent state by touching, and check the strength change of panels by pressing.

    2. Operation steps.

      • Touch and move on the deformed area in the shape of *.

      • Judge the deformed position by feeling the shape change with your palm.

      • Judge the deformed position by feeling the shape change with your palm.

    Reminder
    The touch judgment method requires that the sheet metal technician has long term work experiences to obtain good effects.

  3. Ruler comparison judgment.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • Be able to judge the damage conditions quickly and intuitively.

    2. Operation steps.

      • Select a position symmetrical with the damaged position for comparison.

      • Check the damage conditions by attaching a ruler to the deformed area, and compare the conditions at both sides to judge the damage conditions.

      Reminder
      There are many methods available for comparison judgment, symmetrical comparison with tools.

  4. Profile gauge judgment.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • Be able to detect the deformation during maintenance.

      • Be able to inspect the effect of maintenance.

    2. Operation steps.

      • Select an undeformed position symmetrical with the damaged position for comparison.

      • Attach the teeth of profile gauge with the undeformed panel, and make the teeth profile consistent with the panel profile.

      • Compare the teeth profile formed with the deformed with to check the deformation.

Accessory removal and repair preparation

  1. Preparations.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • Select suitable removal and installation tools.

      • Remove accessories influencing maintenance on the vehicle body.

      • Protect the undamaged parts of the vehicle properly.

    2. Operation steps.

      • Disconnect the negative pole of battery for quenching when necessary.

      • Protect the surrounding parts and components with shielding materials.

      • Complete other removal and preparation jobs.

    Caution
    Always remove the negative pole of battery when using the dent puller for maintenance, to avoid large current passing through the vehicle body and damaging lines on the vehicle.

Shaping

  1. Preparations.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • Select sheet metal hammers or crowbars suitable to the shape of deformed section for repair.

      • Knock gently, and then knock harder for repair.

    2. Operation steps.

      • Select crowbars with small angles for prying of edges and corners.

      • Select suitable line chisels suitable to shapes of edges.

      • Select the manual jacking block with gentle edges and corners on the surface for fine repair of plates.

    Caution
    Observe the outward direction principle for plate repair, i.e. repair the inner structural plates before the outer ones.

Grinding after repair

  1. Grind the original paint film.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • Select the pneumatic or electric grinder as well as sand paper.

      • Clear the paint film in the damaged area as small as possible carefully.

    2. Operation steps.

      • Remove uneven traces formed by the manual jacking block, hammer and crowbar.

      • Grind the damaged area with suitable sand paper.

      • Grind roughly, and then finely.

    Caution
    Grind the damaged area only. Never grind excessively.

Effect after grinding

  1. Effect after grinding.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • Form good surface: the edge where the exposed metal contacts with the coat shall be in the linear shape after repair and grinding, thus facilitating efficiency improvement in the next process.

Cases

  1. Grind the original paint film (poor case).

    1. Defect.

      • The conventional grinding wheel has strong ability of cutting steel plates. Sparks fly during grinding.

    2. Consequences

      • The steel plates of the vehicle body is thinned, influencing durability of the vehicle body.

    3. Solution

      • Replace the conventional grinding wheel with the carbonate grinding wheel or gauze flap disc.

  2. Effect after grinding (poor case).

    1. Defect.

      • A poor surface is formed.

    2. Consequences

      • Traces of hammering and quenching are obvious on the exposed metal surface of the steel plate after grinding, and the section in contact with the coat is in the zigzag shape, resulting in difficulties of feather edge preparation in the next process, extended operating time and decreased efficiency.

    3. Solution

      • Never grind beyond the marked lines of the damaged area.

Dent Puller - Maintenance Method

Reminder

  • The dent puller is usually used for repairing the damaged plates with the inner area inaccessible, e.g. the external panel of door, the external board of rocker panel and the front section of the rear fender.

  • Procedure of steel plate repairing with dent puller: damage judgment — paint film removal — dent puller commissioning — welding and pulling — welding trace grinding — corrosion protection of inner cavity.

Damage judgment

  1. Method of deformation judgment.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • Select the standard method of damage judgment the same as that in the manual shaping process, and judge the damage degree and scope of the damaged plate.

      • Mark the range of damage with a color pen in order to prevent damage expansion during maintenance which will otherwise decrease the work efficiency.

Paint film removal

  1. Removal of paint film.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • Remove the original paint film with the pneumatic or electric grinding tool as well as sand paper to expose the steel plate.

      • Remove the dust using the air gun and dust cloth.

    Caution

    • Never remove the paint film using the grinding tool with excessively large particles since such large particles would thin the metallic plate and reduce its strength.

    • Only remove the paint film over the area to be welded with washers.

    • The width of paint film ground shall be that of the damaged area ± 10 mm.

Adjustment of dent puller

  1. Commissioning of dent puller.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • Select the working mode of the dent puller.

      • Regulate the current.

      • Make test weld on plates made of the same material and of the same thickness.

    Caution
    Never operate the dent puller on the operating panel without commissioning to avoid burning through the plate.

Welding and drawing

  1. Repair of plate plane.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • When drawing the dent part on the plate of vehicle body, you may knock the part protruding due to extrusion around the dent part with a sheet metal hammer at the same time.

      • Pull the plate surface repeatedly with the increasing force, instead of one-time pulling forming, till the height before deformation is recovered.

      • Measure and check with suitable tools in a timely manner during pulling, in order to prevent excessively high surface caused by over pulling.

    2. Operation steps.

      • Select a suitable washer.

      • Adjust the current.

      • Make test welding.

      • Weld the gaskets or wave shaped wires one by one after plate grinding.

      • Pull with a puller.

    Caution
    The recovered plate surface shall be a bit lower than the original surface, i.e. 0.5~1 mm. Make sure there is no high point or hole.

  2. Ridge repair.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • Pull from the inside out. Never pull from the position with the least severe deformation, which will lead to excessive steel plate extension or too many high spots.

      • Use the fixed type tower puller for easier pulling when repairing a large deformed area.

    Caution

    • Knock the area around the pulled dent with a sheet metal hammer when pulling.

    • The ridges after repair shall be about 0.5 mm lower than those original ones on the vehicle.

Weld mark grinding

  1. Weld mark grinding.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • Remove the weld marks on the repair plate with the pneumatic or electric grinder as well as sand paper.

      • Trim high points with the tip of finishing hammer.

    Caution
    Never remove the sharp high points directly with a grinder. Otherwise, the steel plate will be thinned or ground through.
    Reminder

    • Press the repaired plate to inspect its strength.

    • Quenching is required if the strength is decreased.

    • Further pulling is required in case of any undesirable dents.

Corrosion protection of inner cavity

  1. Corrosion protection of inner cavity

    1. Knowledge points.

      • Take measures of corrosion protection with anti-corrosion wax at the inner side of the plate welded by the dent puller.

      • For the positions unaccessible, insert a hose into the cavity for spraying of anti-corrosion wax.

      • Re-apply the area with sealant when necessary after proper treatment.

    Reminder
    Corrosion protection is a necessary process after plate maintenance.

Cases

  1. Excessive grinding after repair (poor case).

    1. Defect.

      • Paint film grinding after repair goes beyond the damage range.

      • The exposed metal surface of plate contacts with the paint disorderly.

    2. Consequences

      • The damage area is enlarged artificially due to the failure of observing requirements for damage range marking during paint film grinding, thus increasing the workload.

    3. Solution

      • Use the grinder carefully.

      • Keep the exposed metal joining with the paint film in the linear shape.

      • Never grind beyond the range marked in damage evaluation.
