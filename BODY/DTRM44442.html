Assessing Damage

Overview

  1. Sheet spray is one of the most basic business of after-sales service department. It reflects the technical level of after-sales maintenance, specialization of after-sales service process, and is also a key to improve customer satisfaction.

  2. Sheet spray operators need to understand that sheet spray operations are closely related and indispensable. Sheet spray process consists of two parts: “sheet process” and "painting process”. The two processes are looped together. Otherwise, the overall efficiency and maintenance quality of sheet spray will be directly affected. Shop managers shall monitor the collaboration between the two processes timely, ensure team efficiency and ultimately improve the operating benefit of the sheet spray business.

Assessing Damage

  1. Knowledge points.

    • Assess the damaged parts of the vehicle. Confirm the damage range and determine whether replacement of spare parts is required.

  2. Operation steps.

    • Incoming inspection.

    • Assist in insurance reporting and loss assessment.

    • Confirm the maintenance parts and spare parts for replacement.

    • Estimated cost and delivery time.
