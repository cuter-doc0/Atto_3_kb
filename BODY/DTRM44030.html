Safety Protection Measures for Painting Operation

Cleaning and Degreasing Process

Process

Cleaning and degreasing

Hazardous ingredients

Organic solvent

Hazards

inhalation, splashing, skin, eyes

Personal prevention and protection

Organic gas-proof mask, solvent resistant gloves, protective goggles

Basic protection

Environment with sound ventilation, and workshop installed with an exhaust system

Process

Process

Hazards

inhalation, splashing, skin, eyes

Personal prevention and protection

Organic gas-proof mask, self-contained respirator, solvent resistant gloves, protective goggles, anti-static work clothes

Basic protection

Workshop installed with an exhaust system
