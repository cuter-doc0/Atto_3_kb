Replacement of Left Front Longitudinal Member Front Section Assembly

Removal

  1. Remove the front bumper beam left mounting plate assembly. See Replacement of Front Bumper Beam Left Mounting Plate Assembly 

  2. Clear 10 welding spots from the left front longitudinal member front section assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Clear the welding spots (new panel in the outside)

  3. Clear 36 welding spots, and take out the left front longitudinal member front section assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Clear the welding spots (new panel in the outside)

Installation

  1. Secure the left front longitudinal member front section assembly to the mounting position.

  2. Check and adjust the relative position and dimensions of the left front longitudinal member front section assembly and the surrounding parts. See Underbody 

  3. Check and adjust the relative position and dimensions of the left front longitudinal member front section assembly and the surrounding parts. See Front Compartment 

  4. Weld 36 welding spots on the left front longitudinal member front section assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Spot welding (new panel in the outside)

  5. Weld 10 welding spots on the left front longitudinal member front section assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Spot welding (new panel in the outside)

  6. Apply body sealant and internal coating at corresponding positions.

  7. After the external coating is applied, the anti rust agent shall be applied on the inner panel of the welding spots of the closed section structure.
