Replacement of Rear Floor Panel Assembly

Removal

  1. Clear 26 welding spots.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Clear the welding spots (new panel in the outside)

  2. Clear 11 welding spots.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Clear the welding spots (new panel in the outside)

  3. Clear 11 welding spots.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Clear the welding spots (new panel in the outside)

  4. Clear 132 welding spots, and take out the rear floor panel assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Clear the welding spots (new panel in the outside)

Installation

  1. Secure the rear floor panel assembly to the mounting positions.

  2. Check and adjust the relative dimensions of the rear floor panel assembly and the surrounding parts. See Underbody 

  3. Weld 132 welding spots on the rear floor panel assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Spot welding (new panel in the outside)

  4. Weld 11 welding spots on the rear floor assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Spot welding (new panel in the outside)

  5. Weld 11 welding spots on the rear floor panel assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Spot welding (new panel in the outside)

  6. Weld 26 welding spots on the rear floor panel assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Spot welding (new panel in the outside)

  7. Apply body sealant and internal coating at corresponding positions.

  8. After the external coating is applied, the anti rust agent shall be applied on the inner panel of the welding spots of the closed section structure.
