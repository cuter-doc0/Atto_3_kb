Bulges on repaired surfaces

Gently put your hands on the plate surface, and slowly move your hand from undamaged surface to the damaged surface and then back to the undamaged surface to feel the surface and make judgment.

Observe the damaged position from different angles, and make judgment according to the scene mirrored on the plate surface. Where it is difficult to observe, move your body to another position for observing.

Gently rub the repaired surface with sandpaper 80 ~ 120# by using a hand plane to leave marks on the repaired surface. No rubbing marks will leave in dent areas while rubbing marks while will leave in bulged areas.

Move a scale from the surface free of deformation to the repair surface, determine the dents and bulges according to the clearance between the scale and the metal.

Press the repair surface with a finger, and disperse the force on the whole plate, and check for soft or sinking positions.

Welding defect

Weld cracks, small pores exist.

Burrs are identified on the welding position, and the welding melting point is too small.

Excessive welding slag inside the welding spot.

A large gap is identified at joint of steel plates.

Welding position is uniform and equidistant.

The number of welding points is more than 1.3 times that of the original vehicle.

NDT: insert a wedge or a chisel into welding side, no weld shall fall off. If the welding position can be seen, stop inserting the wedge; for plates with identical thickness, open by 3-4mm, for plates with different thickness, open by1.5-2mm.

Welding standard

Plug welding

Surface

The welded plates shall be free of gap

The surface of welding spots shall be free of pores

The whole holes must be fully filled.

The bead height shall not exceed 2mm

Bead with: hole diameter +2mm

Back

Free of melt down

With obvious penetration, height of deep penetration welding spot: 1mm

Butt welding

Surface

Connected plate surface shall be free of obvious thermal deformation

The plate surface shall be free of pores

The welding edge of plate shall be free of undercut

Each bead in the weld pass shall overlap the adjacent beads by 1/3

The weld pass is fully welded

The height of weld pass shall not exceed 2mm

The width of weld pass shall not exceed 5mm

Back

Free of melt down

With obvious penetration, height of deep penetration welding spot: 1mm

The back shall be free of cracks, and the weld pass shall be free of deviation.
