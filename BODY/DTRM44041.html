Use of Curing Agent

  1. Knowledge points.

    • Curing agent is a main ingredient of paint, which reacts with resin chemically, resulting in molecule bonding, and forming a hard coating.

    • Curing agent is mainly used in two-component paint, which can react with synthetic resin chemically to from a film layer.

    • It generally contain amines, isocyanates and organic peroxides and other components.

    • Isocyanates contained in curing agent may easily react with moisture contained in the air, so attention shall be paid during storage and use.

    Reminder

    • This substance is toxic and should be noted during construction. When mixing and applying the paint, wear a gas mask and rubber gloves.
