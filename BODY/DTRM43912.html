Damage Type and Treatment of Body Steel Plate

Elastic Deformation Damage

Reminder

  • Elastic deformation refers to the deformation that can return to the original shape when the external force is cleared. The elastic deformation is relatively easy to repair.

  • Elastic deformation exists in most deformed plates and can be maintained first.

  1. Knowledge points.

    • Mode of deformation: the original shape can be recovered after the external force is cleared.

    • Principle of deformation: the external force applied does not exceed the elastic limit of the plate.

    Caution
    In sheet metal repair, maintain the dent area with elastic deformation on the basis of elastic deformation principle, and maintain the plates with large deformation in the inward direction.

Plastic Deformation

Reminder

  • Plastic deformation refers to the deformation that can not return to the original shape when the external force is cleared.

  • Compared with damages caused by elastic deformation, damages caused by plastic deformation is more difficult to maintain, and the damage types are also complex as well.

Damage of plastic deformation

  1. Knowledge points.

    • Mode of deformation: the original shape does not recover after the external force is cleared.

    • Principle of deformation: the external force applied exceeds the elastic limit of the plate.

  2. Operation steps.

    • Grind the area to be maintained before operating the dent puller.

    • Repair the severely damaged part using the crowbar or via multi-point welding.

    • For the severely damaged part, use the dent puller or shaping hammer for fine repair and quenching after rough repair.

Crushing shrink fold

  1. Knowledge points.

    • Crushing shrink fold is a deformation type of plastic deformation.

    • It indicates a large deformation occurs in case of inward shrinkage of the plate after being impacted.

    • Crushing shrink deformation of the plate also occurs in case of excessive shrinkage.

Reminder
Use the jacking block and the sheet metal hammer for expansion operation to repair the shrunk shape.

Other plastic deformation

  1. Knowledge points.

    • Warping.

    • Bending.

    • Stretching.

    • Tearing or punching.

    Reminder

    • Use the jacking block and the sheet metal hammer for expansion operation to repair the shrunk shape.

    • Weld the torn or punched area before pulling with the dent puller.
