Replacement of Left Side Wall Assembly

Removal

  1. Remove the top cover assembly. See Replacement of Top Cover Assembly 

  2. Remove the top cover front beam. See Replacement of Top Cover Front Beam 

  3. Remove the top cover rear beam assembly. See Replacement of top Cover Rear Beam Assembly 

  4. Remove the front flow tank rear section assembly. See Replacement of Front Flow Tank Rear Section Assembly 

  5. Remove the rear wall panel assembly. See Replacement of Rear Wall Panel Assembly 

  6. Clear 13 welding spots at the front of the left side wall assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Clear the welding spots (new panel in the outside)

  7. Clear 2 welding spots in the middle of the left side wall assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Clear the welding spots (new panel in the outside)

  8. Clear 28 welding spots at the rear of the left side wall assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    		Clear the welding spots (new panel in the outside)

    Clear the welding spots (new panel in the inside)

  9. Clear 88 welding spots on the side and take out the left side wall assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Clear the welding spots (new panel in the outside)

Installation

  1. Secure the left side wall assembly to the mounting position.

  2. Check and adjust the relative position and dimensions of the left side wall assembly and the rear door parts. See Front Door 

  3. Check and adjust the relative position and dimensions of the left side wall assembly and the rear compartment parts. See Rear Door 

  4. Check and adjust the relative position and dimensions of the left side wall assembly and the underbody parts. See Rear Compartment 

  5. Check and adjust the relative position and dimensions of the left side wall assembly and the front door parts. See Underbody 

  6. Weld 88 welding points on the side of the left side wall assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Spot welding (new panel in the outside)

  7. Weld 28 welding points at the rear of the left side wall assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning
    		Spot welding (new panel in the outside)

    Spot welding (new panel in the outside)

  8. Weld 2 welding points in the middle of the left side wall assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Spot welding (new panel in the outside)

  9. Weld 13 welding points at the front of the left side wall assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Spot welding (new panel in the outside)

  10. Apply body sealant and internal coating at corresponding positions.

  11. After the external coating is applied, the anti rust agent shall be applied on the inner panel of the welding spots of the closed section structure.
