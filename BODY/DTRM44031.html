Body Color Type

Solid paint

  1. Knowledge points.

    • The paint coating of vehicle body shall have the identical color effect when viewing from the front and sides, and the paint coating is free of particles when observing from sunlight.

    Reminder

    • The solid paint mainly includes tinting pigments and resin components.

    • For single process solid paint, once it is sprayed, the paint coating gets hiding power and brightness. It is not necessary to spray varnish layer separately.

    • For double process solid paint, spray varnish layer after the end of base color spraying, so as to provide paint film brightness.

    • For the original paint spraying process, solid paint is usually applied in the way of “three-stage spraying and three-stage baking” or “four-stage spraying and three-stage baking”.

Aluminum Powder Paint

  1. Knowledge points.

    • Aluminum powder paint provides metal gloss in the sun light, and the color viewing from the front is quite different from that viewing from sides.

    Reminder

    • The aluminum powder paint includes tinting pigment and aluminum pigment.

    • The aluminum pigment in the aluminum powder paint is completely reflective of light.

    • For the original paint spraying process, the aluminum powder paint is usually applied in the way of “four-stage spraying and three-stage baking”.

Pearlescent Paint

  1. Knowledge points.

    • Provide a transparent and gentle glittering effect in the sun, and provide the solid paint effect indoors.

    Reminder

    • The coating includes tinting pigment and pearl mica pigment.

    • Nacrolacquer has excellent transparency and depth feel.

    • In direct sunlight, it has a fuzzy and soft flash effect.

    • For the original paint spraying process, nacrolacquer is usually applied in the way of “four-stage spraying and three-stage baking” or “five-stage spraying and three-stage baking”

Aluminum Powder Pearlescent Paint

  1. Knowledge points.

    • Provide gentle and fuzzy metal glittering effect in the sun, and provide metal glittering effect indoors.

    Reminder

    • It includes tinting pigment, titanium film mica pigment and aluminum pigment.

    • Where there is sufficient light, it emits a metallic flash that is more vague and soft than only aluminum paint.

    • For the original paint spraying process, it is usually applied in the way of “four-stage spraying and three-stage baking”.
