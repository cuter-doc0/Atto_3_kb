Body Measurement and Calibration

There are two types of vehicle body measurement and correction tools, i.e. the conventional measuring tools (tape and vehicle body measuring gauge), and the special correction and measuring equipment (e.g. The mold type measuring equipment and the electronic measuring equipment).

Measuring Tools and Equipment

  1. Tape measure.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • The tape, a simple measuring tool, is small sized and portable. It mainly applies to measurement of linear plane of the vehicle body.

  2. Ruler.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • It is mainly used for ridge repair, and can also be used for comparison with the undeformed plate.

      • Never scratch the paint coat when comparing with the undamaged reference plate.

  3. Universal measuring scale.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • It is mainly used for measuring curves on the vehicle body and the distance between two points with a barrier between them.

      • Calibrate the measuring scale before using and handle it gently.

  4. Electronic measuring.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • Easy and fast operation, timely output and recording of measurement dimensions using functions such as Bluetooth, and accurate judgment of data changes.

      • Multiple three-dimensional dimensional data can be measured between the upper part, lower part and components of the vehicle body.

      • The huge database can store the vehicle body data of various models. The measurement data is accurate, with very small errors only.

Measuring Methods

  1. Comparison method.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • In the comparison method, measure the dimensions of the undamaged side and those of the damaged side, and compare the measurement results to determine the pulling amplitude of maintenance.

  2. Left-right symmetric method.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • In the symmetric method, measure the two diagonal sizes of the frame and compare the two sizes to judge whether the vehicle body is deformed or not.

  3. Body centerline method.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • The body centerline method is to hang a suitable number of body hanging rulers at the correct positions at the bottom of the body, and check whether the body is deformed by observing whether the center points of all body hanging rulers are on the same straight line.

Correction Tools and Equipment

In case of deformation of external plates and internal structures caused by severe impact, tools and equipment for vehicle body correction and measurement are needed for pulling correction, measurement, partial cutting operations of the accident vehicle, so as to recover the original appearance and quality of the vehicle.

  1. Lifting calibration platform.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • It is easy and fast and is usually used with the electronic measuring system.

      • The bluetooth allows for timely output and recording of measured dimensions and accurate judgment of the data variation.

      • Multiple three-dimensional dimensional data can be measured between the upper part, lower part and components of the vehicle body.

      • The huge database can store the vehicle body data of various models. The measurement data is accurate, with very small errors only.

  2. Mobile calibration platform.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • It can move the maintained vehicle to the position required.

      • It is convenient for correction and maintenance of the vehicle body with side damages.

  3. Small damage repair and calibration platform.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • It is also called as ground frame correction system. It is embedded underground with the surface flush with the ground.

      • By cooperating with mobile correction platform,it will easy to maintain small damage vehicle.

  4. Platform-type calibration platform.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • It allows for easier and faster mounting of clamps at the bottom of vehicle body, and simultaneous mounting of clamps at the bottom of vehicle body at multiple positions.

  5. Ground cage calibration platform.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • It allows for all-round correction and polling operations for the accident vehicle.

  6. Tower puller

    1. Knowledge points.

      • It is secured at sides of the calibration platform.

      • It can be used for pulling of damages and deformations with different angles at different positions.

      • See the operating instructions in advance, get familiar with the installation requirements, using and maintenance methods.

  7. Vehicle body clamps.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • There are four clamps generally.

      • They are used for securing the bottom of the middle section of the vehicle body so as to form secure support for pulling correction operations.

      • Always check the gap between vehicle body and the clamps during pulling correction.

      • If it has any abnormal,it need to be re-installed and tightening bolts.

  8. Body chassis fixing bracket.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • It is used together with the clamps.

      • It is used for vehicle body fixing and height adjustment.

      • It features flexible use with the front, rear, left and right supporting positions adjustable.

  9. Pulling clamp.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • It is a tool necessary for correction and pulling.

      • Select a suitable clamp before operation based on the conditions and position of the damage, and use it correctly.

  10. Border type pulling clamp.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • It is mainly used for securing at the border position of vehicle body.

  11. Plane pulling clamp.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • It is mainly used for securing at the border position without a barrier of the vehicle body.

  12. Hook type pulling clamp.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • It is mainly used for securing at the border position of vehicle body.

  13. Dual direction pulling clamp.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • It can be mounted with two pulling chains for pulling in two directions at same time.

  14. Security cables.

    1. Knowledge points.

      • Install safety cables before pulling correction to avoid safety hazards caused by falling of clamps or rupture of chains.

Correction - Methods

Principles of vehicle body correction

  1. Vector pulling.

    • The vectors include resultant force and component force from the perspectives of direction and angle of forces, i.e. the angle and direction of the resultant force of multiple forces or the component force.

    • As shown in the diagram, the effect of pulling F1 and F2 is the same as that of pulling F3 solely.

    • When pulling at some special position, use the vector pulling mode based on the principle of force decomposition.

  2. Multi-point pulling.

    • Reverse single point pulling may be performed for slight deformation.

    • In case of severe or large deformation of the vehicle body caused by crash, the multi point reverse pulling correction may be performed so as to avoid tearing or uneven pulling force.

    • For multi-point pulling, it is unnecessary to hold all clamps at one position, you may select several points with severe deformation to fix the clamps. Several tower pullers or steel chains may be used for facilitating the operations.

  3. Pulling angle

    • An angle of 90 is recommended between the chain and the hydraulic cylinder. A larger angle may cause uneven force and damages of chains.

  4. Pulling without auxiliary support.

    • Check whether auxiliary support is required or not before pulling correction. Otherwise, secondary damage may occur.

  5. Pulling with auxiliary support.

    • Use the auxiliary support correctly in order to avoid secondary damage and improve the pulling efficiency.

  6. Pulling without auxiliary fixing.

    • Check whether auxiliary fixing is required or not before pulling correction. Otherwise, secondary damage may occur.

  7. Pulling with auxiliary fixing.

    • Use the auxiliary fixing correctly in order to avoid secondary damage and improve the pulling efficiency.

  8. Clamp installation and pulling angle.

    • Install the clamp correctly before pulling correction, keep a proper pulling angle, and prevent the clamp from falling.

  9. Correct pulling point.

    • Select a correct pulling point before pulling.

  10. Damage pulling analysis.

    • Analyze the damaged workpiece before pulling correction and select a suitable pulling method.

  11. Tips for pulling of front longitudinal member.

    • Auxiliary pulling is needed for such deformation in order to improve the pulling efficiency.

    • Pull in two directions for higher efficiency.

  12. Tips for pulling of rocker panel.

    • Insert a pole for pulling if the rocker panel is deformed largely.

  13. Tips for pulling of rear fender.

    • Welding in combination with auxiliary pulling point may be selected for pulling before the largely damaged rear fender is removed for higher efficiency.

Procedures of vehicle body correction

  1. See the dimensional drawing of vehicle body before correction.

    • Check the dimensional data of vehicle body of the corresponding model.

    • Measure the deformation in the damaged area of the vehicle body with a suitable measuring tool.

  2. Installation of pulling equipment.

    • Select a suitable pulling correction equipment.

    • Mount the tower puller at a proper position and adjust the pulling angle.

  3. Inspection of vehicle body clamp.

    • Check whether the vehicle body clamp is fastened before pulling correction.

  4. Installation of pulling tools.

    • Select a suitable pulling point before installing the pulling tools.

  5. Installation of security cables.

    • Install the security cables before correction of the vehicle, so as to avoid personal injuries or other damages caused by the flying ruptured pulling tools.

    • Connect one end of the security cable with the steel chain close to the clamp, and the other end with the vehicle body correction position.

    • Never knock the steel chain during pulling to avoid rupture.

  6. Installation of air source connector.

    • Ensure the air source pressure is sufficient and he connector has no leakage before pulling correction.

  7. Safe position of operator.

    • The operator shall stand at a position beside the pulling direction when working and pay attention to safety of the surrounding vehicles and personnel.

  8. Pulling angle

    • Keep an angle of 90° between the chain and the pulled position during pulling.

  9. Measurement after pulling.

    • Check the pulling extent of this time during or after pulling for convenience of checking the pulling extent needed next time.

  10. Whole body change inspection during pulling.

    • Always check the whole body change during pulling correction, so as to judge the pulling effect and prevent secondary damage of the vehicle caused by excessive pulling.

    • Check changes of gaps between doors and those between the door and the front fender.

    • Never pull excessively in one time. Measure and pull repeatedly for the proper final state.

  11. Stress clearance after pulling.

    • An internal stress is generated by the plate in the corrected zone during pulling correction. In this case, knock at the corresponding position of the plate to clear the internal stress.

  12. Measurement after secondary pulling.

    • Measure the zone of correction pulling after pulling, check variation of relevant dimension and measure at each point to see whether value measured is ideal.

  13. Value after correction pulling.

    • Perform pull correction accurately till the dimensions of the damaged area approach the original dimension of the vehicle body.

    • The error shall be ≤±2mm.
