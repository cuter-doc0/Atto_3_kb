Anti-Corrosion Treatment

Anti-corrosion Materials

Purpose of anti-corrosion after steel plate maintenance: galvanized steel sheets subjected to phosphating treatment are used as vehicle body steel plates for rust protection. In the steel plate maintenance process, the rust protection will be damaged after welding, grinding and other thermal works, therefore, the plates shall be subjected to anti-corrosive treatment after the maintenance, so as to prevent durability degradation of steel plates due to corrosion.

Anti-rust primer

  1. Functions.

    • Anti-rust primer may prevent plate rusting, which shall be sprayed on the joints and areas subjected to grinding.

  2. Use method.

    • Apply anti-rust primer on completion of sheet metal repair.

    • Remove water, grease and other substances.

    • Spraying or painting on bare metal plates.

    Caution
    Remove moisture and stains from the surface before application.

Sealant

  1. Functions.

    • Sealant has good sealing and waterproof property.

  2. Knowledge points.

    • Sealant cures quickly, so that the plates can be handed over to the next process sooner.

  3. Use method.

    • Remove water and dirt on plate surface.

    • Apply anti-rust primer.

    • Apply sealant with a sealant gun after the anti-rust primer is dried.

    • After repair, apply sealant on positions where original sealant is damaged.

    Caution
    The sealant must be applied after the anti-corrosion paint on the panels has dried.

Chassis sealant

  1. Functions.

    • It can prevent the paint from being damaged by flashing stones and from rusting, and can provide sound and shock absorbing effect.

  2. Knowledge points.

    • Chassis sealant is soft after fully dried.

    • It is mainly used at sill bottom or bottom plates.

  3. Use method.

    • Remove water and dirt on plate surface.

    • Wait until the plate surface is fully dried.

    • Spray chassis sealant with a special chassis sealant gun.

Caution
The chassis glue can only be used on the lower part of the sill plate and the bottom plate, and cannot be sprayed on other positions.

Special detergent and degreaser

  1. Functions.

    • Remove oil stains on plate surface.

  2. Knowledge points.

    • The anti-corrosion effect cannot be guaranteed without removing grease with degreaser.

    • Degreaser shall be used for cleaning before most anti-corrosion treatments.

Caution
Degreasing agent is generally an organic solvent liquid, and personal protection must be done when using it.

Cavity anti-corrosion wax

  1. Functions.

    • For the anti-corrosion and anti-rust treatment for unaccessible positions.

    • For applying wax oil-based internal anti-rust agent.

    • For anti-corrosion for door pillars and sill cavities.

    • Apply on damaged areas after sheet metal repair (in cavities without paint)

  2. Knowledge points.

    • Provide anti-rust treatment for all surfaces according to the process specifications, including unaccessible positions.

Caution
Failure to take cavity anti-corrosion measures may result in steel plate corrosion, rust and other quality problems.

Anti-corrosion Tool

Spray gun

  1. Functions.

    • It is used for spraying anti-corrosion paint.

  2. Knowledge points.

    • Suitable for large-area painting operation.

    • Provides even spraying effect.

Sealant gun

  1. Functions.

    • It is used for applying sheet metal sealant, as a special tool for barrel-type sealant.

Caution
The glue gun is divided into pneumatic, manual and electric type. If you use a pneumatic or electric glue gun, the pressure should not be too large, and the pressure should be gradually adjusted in order from small to large.

Cavity anti-corrosion spray gun

  1. Functions.

    • For the anti-corrosion and anti-rust treatment for unaccessible positions.

    • For applying wax oil-based internal anti-rust agent.

    • For anti-corrosion for door pillars and sill cavities.

    • Apply on damaged areas after sheet metal repair (in cavities without paint)

  2. Knowledge points.

    • Provide anti-rust treatment for all surfaces according to the process specifications, including unaccessible positions.

    • Failure to take cavity anti-corrosion measures may result in steel plate corrosion, rust and other quality problems.

    • For the unaccessible cavity of parts, a spray gun with a hose should be used for spraying cavity anti-corrosion wax.

Caution
The spray gun with the hose has a large spraying angle, and only needs to change the angle to meet the surface coverage of the inner cavity in almost all positions.

Brush

  1. Functions.

    • It is used for applying liquid primer and sealant.

    Caution
    Soak and wash after using.
