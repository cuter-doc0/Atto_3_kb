Personal Protection

Caution

Personal protection is the last barrier for keeping operators safe and protecting them from being injured. Protective equipment, as the auxiliary preventive measures, can effectively prevent or reduce the occurrence of industrial accident, avoid occupational disease and occupational poisoning when used correctly.

Occupational Disease - Prevention

  1. Knowledge points.

    Causes of occupational disease include:

    • Dust.

    • Radiation.

    • Chemicals.

    • Toxic dust.

  2. Preventive measures.

    • Learn the knowledge of safety protection.

    • Use the protective equipment correctly.

Personal Protective Equipment

  1. Work clothes.

    1. Functions.

      • Work clothes are protective suits to be worn during working and production.

    2. Knowledge points.

      • It can protect human body against water, dust, and oil.

  2. Goggles.

    1. Functions.

      • Goggles are devices that have transparent lenses and safeguard the eyes of the users, while enabling them to see the work area clearly.

    2. Knowledge points.

      • Easy to wear and use.

      • Protect your eyes from splashes during grinding.

      • Protect your eyes from contacting the solvent.

      • Avoid damage to the eyes from small particles when using air guns.

  3. Protective mask.

    1. Functions.

      • The protective mask is a kind of protective equipment used to protect the eyes and face from dust, chemicals, heat, debris and other harmful substances.

    2. Knowledge points.

      • Easy to wear and use.

      • The protective mask applies to the dusty grinding operation with intensive splashing and can protect the whole face.

      • Protect your eyes from splashes during grinding.

      • It can protect the facial skin from injuries caused by flying metal sparks.

      • It can protect eyes from small particles during use of the air gun.

  4. Welding mask.

    1. Functions.

      • The welding mask is protective equipment. It is hands-free and can ensure high quality welding and protect eyes and facial skin of the welding operator from radiation or scalding.

    2. Knowledge points.

      • The operator can clearly observe the welding zone, thus improving the welding quality.

      • It eliminates repeated masking actions at arcing of welding, thus improving the working efficiency.

      • It is easy to wear with the head band adjustable based on the head circumference.

  5. Anti-dust mask.

    1. Functions.

      • Anti-dust mask is the protective equipment used to filter dust, to filter the dust floating in the air, and to filter partial hazardous gas and dust fog.

    2. Use method.

      • It is easy to wear by fixing the elastic ropes on the back side of head and the neck.

      • Cover the upper part of the nose and pinch the upper metal strip so that it fits close to the bridge of the nose.

      Caution

      Store them properly after using to avoid secondary pollution.

  6. Smoke mask.

    1. Functions.

      • The smoke mask is protective equipment used for filtering the smoke generated during welding and the dust particles floating in the air.

    2. Use method.

      • It is easy to wear by fixing the elastic ropes on the back side of head and the neck.

      • Cover the upper part of the nose and pinch the upper metal strip so that it fits close to the bridge of the nose.

      Caution

      Store them properly after using to avoid secondary pollution.

  7. Solvent-proof mask.

    1. Functions.

      • The solvent-proof mask is protective equipment used for filtering the toxic gas volatilized during painting and the dust particles floating in the air.

    2. Use method.

      • It is easy to wear by fixing the elastic ropes on the back side of head.

      • Cover the upper part of nose and mouth, and make it coincide with the profile of nose bridge.

      Caution

      Regularly check and replace activated carbon and the rubber seal in contact with the face.

  8. Protective earplugs.

    1. Functions.

      • Protective earplugs are personal protective equipment made of silicone or low pressure foam, and high elastic polyester, and closely contact with the earlap after being inserted into the auditory meatus for sound insulation and auditory system protection of operators

    2. Knowledge points.

      • Reduce the ear-splitting noise generated in grinding.

      • Reduce the noise of knocking.

      • Reduce the other impact of noise during work.

      Warning

      • Frequent exposure to high noise levels can cause severe hearing impairment.

      • Never wear protective ear plugs when listening is required for learning of the maintenance conditions (e.g. welding and operation of the calibration platform).

  9. Protective earmuffs.

    1. Functions.

      • Protective earmuffs are personal protective equipment that can reduce the noise level exceeding the limit (above 64 dB) which is bearable to the human body, and protect the auditory system of operators.

    2. Knowledge points.

      • Earmuffs are superior to earplugs in respect of protection performances.

      • Reduce the sharp noise generated in knocking and grinding.

      Caution

      Never wear protective earmuffs when listening is required for learning of the maintenance conditions (e.g. welding and operation of the calibration platform).

  10. Safety boots.

    1. Functions.

      • Safety boots are protective shoes that are installed with protective steel plates at the front ends for protection from crushing and squeezing. The soles are made of electro-insulating rubber for protection against electric shock and slipping.

      Caution

      • Wear suitable safety boots as required when entering the workshop.

      • Ensure the safety boots fit well.

      • Make long shoelaces fit.

  11. Cotton gloves.

    1. Functions.

      • Protect operators’ hands when handling body parts.

      • Protect operator's hands when using basic tools.

      • Protect operator's hands when grasping hard objects.

      Warning

      Never wear cotton gloves when using sharp tools or objects to prevent the gloves from being penetrated and hurting fingers.

  12. Solvent-proof gloves.

    1. Functions.

      • Solvent-proof gloves are used in case of skin injuries for protecting the skin from being exposed to corrosive liquid.

      • Protect skin from solvents when using detergent.

      • Protect skin from solvents when applying sealant.

      • Protects skin from solvents when exposed to corrosive liquids.

    2. Knowledge points.

      • Solvent-proof gloves are made of rubber and most of them are disposable.

      • They can effectively isolate the skin of hands from corrosive solvent.

  13. Leather welding gloves with long sleeves.

    1. Functions.

      • Welding gloves with long sleeves are dedicated for welding. They can provide safety protection and comfortable use for welding operators and are applicable to cutting,welding, handling and so on.

    2. Knowledge points.

      • The leather welding gloves with long sleeves can isolate high temperature and ultraviolet ray.

      • They are suitable to use in the work place with hard light, welding slags and sparks.

  14. Leather gloves.

    1. Functions.

      • Leather gloves are protective equipment and are used by operators in operation of pneumatic drill, electric drill and bench drill.

    2. Knowledge points.

      • Protect your fingers from being injured when holding sharp articles.

      • Protect the operator in grinding or high temperature work.

      Caution

      Each type of leather gloves are suitable to specific applications. Never wear different gloves or those unsuitable to the applications.

Welding Protection

  1. Knowledge points.

    • Hazards of hard light and radiation.

    • Hazards of smoke and toxic gas.

    • High temperature and splashing.

  2. Injuries caused by hazards.

    • Ultraviolet radiation to eyes.

    • Respiratory system injuries caused by smoke.

    • Scalding and burning caused by splashing.

  3. Protective equipment.

    • Work clothes.

    • Welding mask.

    • Leather welding gloves with long sleeves.

    • Smoke mask.

    • Safety boots.
