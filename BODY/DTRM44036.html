Body Paint Type

Paint for Vehicle Manufacturing

  1. Thermal polymer paint.

    • Paints mainly used on the production line of the OEM. After applying the paint on vehicle body, heating to about 140°C for curing.

    • In the heating process, chemical reaction will occur when resin molecules will combine and form large particles, thus forming a cured coating on the vehicle body, and the coating is not easily dissolved even if it is exposed to solvent.

Paint for Vehicle Repair and Recoating

Two-component polymer paint

  1. Knowledge points.

    • For two-component products, paint must be mixed with the curing agent before use.

    • The commonly used paint is acrylic polyurethane paint.

    • Curing agent will react with resin chemically, forming a coating that does not dissolve easily even exposed to solvent.

    Reminder

    • To mix the paints, read the paint product manual, select the matching curing agent or diluent. And mix them according to the standard proportion.

    • During construction, select painting materials reasonably according to the requirements of the operation process. Improper selection of paint products will lead to painting defects.

Volatilization solvent-based paint

  1. Knowledge points.

    • Single-component paint, mix with diluent for use.

    • After spraying, the solvent will volatilize, and the resin will form the coating.

    • The plate will dry quickly after application.

    • Nitrolacquer coating is easily dissolved by solvent, therefore, when recoating plates with nitrocellulose coatings, attention shall be paid to prevent shrinkage.

Water-Based Paint

  1. Knowledge points.

    • It is composed of waterborne polyurethane resin, pigment and additives, and water is used as solvent.

    • Improve the working environment and construction conditions, eliminate fire hazards during construction, and significantly reduce pollution and save resources.

    • The coating tools can be cleaned with water, which can reduce the consumption of cleaning solvent, and minimize the harm to the construction personnel.

    Reminder

    • Mix the water-based paint with reference to the proportion in the product manual. Apply varnish coating when the water-based primer is dried.

Curing Type of Paint

Paint curing type

  1. Knowledge points.

    • Paints, by film forming mode, can be classified into volatilization solvent-based paint, oxidized polymer paint, baking polymer paint and two-component polymer paint, by draying type, can be classified into, evaporation drying type and reaction drying type.

Solvent evaporation drying

  1. Knowledge points.

    • The film is formed after the solvent in wet film evaporated, without resin molecular association (net), therefore, the film will be dissolved by wiping with diluent (nitrolacquer and air drying primer are of this type).

Thermal polymerization drying

  1. Knowledge points.

    • By heating the coating to a certain temperature (generally above 120°C), resin will be subjected to polymerization reaction, forming a dense net structure, after drying, a high-performance film will be formed.

    • After drying, the coating will be no longer dissolved in organic solvents. Nowadays, almost all new vehicles are provide with this type of coating.

Two-component polymerization drying

  1. Knowledge points.

    • By mixing paint and curing agent, the resin will be subjected to chemical reaction polymerization, forming a net structure. This reaction can also be performed at room temperature, as temperature rises, the reaction accelerates. During the construction, for the sake of efficiency, forced drying at 60°C is provided.

    • The net structure provides a density equivalent to that provided by hot polymerization drying, and film provides excellent performance. The commonly used polyurethane paint is of this type.

    • In the construction, curing agent shall be mixed according to the proportion specified in the product manual, and matched curing agent shall be selected for two-component paints. Otherwise, due to the different proportion of chemical composition, it may result in poor adhesion, poor hardness of coating, poor brightness and other problems.
