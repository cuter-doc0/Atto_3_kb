Plate Cutting Principle

Principle of Cutting Position

  1. Standard cutting scheme.

    • The positions without enhancement parts in the inner side should be selected for cutting.

    • The cutting position shall be free of stress concentration area (crumple design)

    • Welding joint surface in the cutting position shall be as small as possible, so as to facilitate spraying operation

    • Position with less accessories or with weld covered after accessories installation shall be selected.

    • The front longitudinal member assembly must be replaced as a whole.

  2. Reason of not suitable for cutting

    • If reinforcement structural parts are installed on the inner side of the plate to be cut, the normal cutting route will be affected, besides, the risk of damaging the reinforcement structural parts during the cutting process will increase.

    • For the crumple zone, after cutting and welding, in case of another front impact on this position, the crumple performance will be lost or degraded, which may affect the impact energy absorption ability, and degrade the vehicle body safety.

    • Positions with smaller area should be selected for cutting or welding, for the plate will be subjected to smaller thermal deformation due to less heat production during welding, thus improve the efficiency of spraying process.

    • Positions covered by trims and accessories shall be selected for cutting and welding, so as to reduce the time for finishing after welding thus improve the work efficiency (such as the positions for installation of rear fender triangular window and triangular window rear trim strip of Song).

    • The front longitudinal member of the integrated vehicle body is provided for ensuring the torsional rigidity of the frame, bearing longitudinal load, and supporting main parts of vehicles, therefore, in case of bending or deformation after impact, the whole assembly as supplied must be replaced, cutting for partial replacement is not allowed.
