Replacement of Front Bumper Beam Left Mounting Plate Assembly

Removal

  1. Remove the front end module locating mounting plate. See Front Module Locating Mounting Plate 

  2. Clear 2 welding spots from the front bumper beam left mounting plate assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Clear the welding spots (new panel in the inside)

  3. Clear 5 welding spots, and take out the front bumper beam left mounting plate assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Clear the welding spots (new panel in the outside)

Installation

  1. Secure the front bumper beam left mounting plate assembly to the mounting position.

  2. Check and adjust the relative position and dimensions of the front bumper beam left mounting plate assembly and surrounding parts.

  3. Weld 2 welding spots on the front bumper beam left mounting plate assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Spot welding (new panel in the inside)

  4. Weld 5 welding spots on the front bumper beam left mounting plate assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Spot welding (new panel in the outside)
