Replacement of Front Flow Tank Rear Section Assembly

Removal

  1. Clear 17 welding spots on the left front side of the front flow tank rear section assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Clear the welding spots (new panel in the outside)

  2. Clear 15 welding spots on the right front side of the front flow tank rear section assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Clear the welding spots (new panel in the outside)

  3. Clear 21 welding spots at the left rear side of the front flow tank rear section assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Clear the welding spots (new panel in the outside)

  4. Clear 16 welding points at the right rear side and take out the front flow tank rear section assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Clear the welding spots (new panel in the outside)

Installation

  1. Fix the front flow tank rear section assembly to the mounting position.

  2. Check and adjust the relative position and size of the front flow tank rear section assembly and the surrounding parts.

  3. Weld 17 welding points on the left front side of the front flow tank rear section assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Spot welding (new panel in the outside)

  4. Weld 15 welding spots on the right front side of the front flow tank rear section assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Spot welding (new panel in the outside)

  5. Weld 21 welding points at the left rear side of the front flow tank rear section assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Spot welding (new panel in the outside)

  6. Weld 16 welding points at the right rear side of the front flow tank rear section assembly.

    Maintenance method indicator

    Meaning

    Spot welding (new panel in the outside)

  7. Apply body sealant and internal coating at corresponding positions.

  8. After the external coating is applied, the anti rust agent shall be applied on the inner panel of the welding spots of the closed section structure.
