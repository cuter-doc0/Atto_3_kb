Operation and Maintenance of Shaping Tools

Manual Shaping and Maintenance Tools - Introduction

Reminder
Manual shaping tool and maintenance method: Manual sheet metal shaping (manual shaping in short) is a fundamental skill that all sheet metal operators shall possess. Proper use of manual shaping tools can improve maintenance efficiency and quality.

Sheet metal hammer

  1. Functions.

    • The sheet metal hammer is used for repairing the flat and smooth steel plate surface and the basic ridges.

    • The pick end hammer is used for knocking the folds or high points on the plate.

    • The shrinkage hammer is used for recovering the original strength of slightly stretched metal by “holding” the metal with the serrated face and knocking the bulge (keep holding the iron support by hand). In this way, the metal will be shrunk back to the original shape.

    • The rubber hammer applies to repair of a large area or the soft material. For example, the rubber hammer can avoid hammering traces on the plate when repairing the aluminum plate.

  2. Knowledge points.

    • The ends of hammers have the arc surface and the horizontal surface.

    • Hammer handles are usually made of wood.

    • The sheet metal hammers are classified into pick end hammers, pick hammers, shrinkage hammers, rubber hammers and others.

    • The pick end hammer has a conical passivated chisel at one end and usually a round impact plane at the other end.

    • The shrinkage hammer has round smooth face at one end and square serrated face at the other end.

    • Heads of rubber hammers are made of soft or rigid rubber. Hence, they are light-weight and easy to use.

  3. Use method.

    • Only wrist swinging is required for slight knocking.

    • Swing with the help of elbow for increased force.

    • Avoid force exertion with shoulder swing for manual shaping and repair when possible.

    • Hold the rear part of the handle with the clearance of 1-2cm from the end, and exert force with your thumb and index finger.

    • Keep the hammer face parallel with the plate surface during knocking.Tap with rebounding.

    Caution

    • Check the head and handle of the hammer to see whether they are secure. Never knock sharp objects.

    • Use the sheet metal hammer on the vehicle body discreetly. Never use it for maintenance of body structural members with large force, or use it as a common hammer.

    • Check the hammer surface regularly, and take proper measures for repair in case of obvious traces discovered. Otherwise, the surface of repaired plate will be scratched.

    • Trim with sand paper #120, #180, #320 and #1000 in order till the surface becomes smooth.

Manual jacking block

  1. Functions.

    • The manual jacking block is used together with the vehicle body hammers for correction of plate damage back to the original plate surface as much as possible.

  2. Knowledge points.

    • Hold the back face of the depression formed by the manual jacking block by hands, and knock the protrusion at the other side with the a hammer.

    • The manual jacking blocks have many shapes for matching with a variety of profiles. To recover the original profile of the plate, make sure the diameter of the manual jacking block is smaller than that of the original profile of the plate.

    • The manual jacking blocks are made of high strength steel. There are high, medium and low work faces and combination of multiple work faces of different shapes available.

  3. Use method.

    • Work with your palm as the point of force application.

    • Close your fingers to hold the edge of the manual jacking block to prevent slipping and falling.

    • The vertical point of the post rod and the hammer is kept when knocking the plate in the aligned manner. This is in compliance with the principle of solid knocking.

    • The jacking block and the hammer are not vertical to each other at a point when knocking with deflected support, and the contact point is beside the knocking point of the hammer. This is in compliance with the principle of virtual knocking.

    Caution

    • Never hold the sharp tip of jacking block against the internal plane of the steel plate to avoidf unexpected deformation of steel plate surface.

    • It is generally recommended that the radian of the surface of jacking block is about 80% of the original radian of the steel plate. Never use the contact face with the radian of surface too small.

    • Never support in the vertical direction where possible because the plate is liable to expand when supported in such a manner.

    • Check and service the jacking block regularly in order to keep the work face in good conditions.

Line chisel

  1. Functions.

    • It is used with the hammer for knocking, and applies to deformation with edges and ridges to be repaired.

  2. Knowledge points.

    • It can be used for repair of ridges of different length and shapes.

Profile gauge

  1. Functions.

    • The profile gauge is an auxiliary inspection tool for repair of external plates of the vehicle body.

    • It features the extension type needle structure.

    • For measuring the damaged plate for repairing.

    • For inspection during plate repairing.

    • For inspection of flatness and radian of the plate after repairing.

  2. Knowledge points.

    • It can be used for repair of ridges of different length and shapes.

  3. Use method.

    • Place the profile gauge on a standard reference or plate before the maintenance, set the shape in advance and place the profile gauge at the damaged area in order to measure the deformation through shape and clearance comparison.

    • For the optimum effect, you may check the maintenance conditions of the damaged area by comparing the reference with the face maintained.

    Caution
    After the un-deformed plate is shaped, never press hard when comparing it with the deformed area. Otherwise the judgment result would be affected due to change of the profile gauge.

Crowbar

  1. Functions.

    • It applies to locations unaccessible by hand or the narrow space.

  2. Knowledge points.

    • There are many types of crowbars available. You can select a suitable type based on different shapes of steel plates.

    • They are usually made of carbon steel, which has good toughness and can effectively resist bending and deformation.

    • A crowbar consists of the handle, the force application end and the contact end.

  3. Use method.

    • To repair with the help of a crowbar, make sure the shape of the front end of the crowbar is similar to the depression on the plate, and the angle of crowbar is slightly smaller than that of the depression.

    • Select a proper supporting point to avoid secondary damage at the supporting location.

    • Increase the force applied gradually. Never exert force excessively to avoid secondary stretching of the plate.

    • You may knock the area around the depression with a sheet metal hammer when prying the depression with a crowbar. Similar to the principle of virtual knocking, make sure the hammer and the contact end of the crowbar are not in a line.

    Caution

    • When using the crowbar, pay attention to parts and components at the supporting point to avoid secondary damage of the vehicle artificially.

    • Never use the contact face with a small angle. Otherwise, small protruding point difficult to maintain will be formed.

File

  1. Functions.

    • A file is the tool used for high point removal in sheet metal repair. You may judge the protrusion and depression conditions of the plate based on the filing traces during manual shaping.

  2. Knowledge points.

    • The file is used for finishing of protruding points left after operations with the hammer, hand jacking block and spoon shaped iron.

    • You may use it for facilitating measurement based on the protruding and depression conditions of the area repaired.

  3. Use method.

    • Push and pull the file at an angle of 30°~45° where possible. Never exert force excessively.

    Caution
    When using the body file for the plate repaired by the dent puller, do not exert force excessively. Otherwise, the plate will become thinner and the strength be reduced.

Hand grinder

  1. Functions.

    • The hand grinder is a tool used for large area grinding.

  2. Knowledge points.

    • There are pneumatic and electric grinders available.

    • The rotating speed of grinder is adjustable.

    Caution
    Hold the handle tightly, adjust the revolving speed properly and use the protective equipment.

Scriber

  1. Functions.

    • A scriber is a tool used for marking the position and shape of the ridge.

Ruler

  1. Functions.

    • It is mainly used for ridge repair, and can also be used for comparison with the undeformed plate.

    Caution
    Never scratch the paint coat when comparing with the undamaged reference plate.

Manual Shaping - Method

Reminder
Manual shaping includes solid knocking and virtual knocking.

Method of solid knocking

  1. Knowledge points.

    • Keep the hammer face parallel to the plate.

    • Make the hammering point coincide with the holding point of jacking block.

    • Exert force increasingly.

    • Check the positions of solid knocking: knocking position of hammer, position of steel plate and position of jacking block.

    Caution
    Excessive solid knocking will result in expansion deformation of the plate, and more severe solid knocking will cause uneven stress distribution on the plate, thus making the plate unusable.

Method of virtual knocking

  1. Knowledge points.

    • Keep the hammer face parallel to the plate.

    • Make the hammering point away from the holding point of jacking block.

    • Exert force increasingly.

    • Check the positions of virtual knocking: knocking position of hammer, position of steel plate and position of jacking block.

    Caution
    Exert force increasingly for virtual knocking, i.e. knock tentative before increasing the force gradually.

Shaping Equipment - Introduction

Dent puller

  1. Functions.

    • The dent puller is used for repairing plates of the vehicle body. It bonds the gasket and the metal external plate of the vehicle body together by virtue of low voltage and release of transient high current for pulling and shaping.

  2. Knowledge points.

    • The dent puller features easy operation (it is unnecessary to install too many other accessories), high efficiency, smaller heat-affected zone and quality assurance.

    Caution

    • Do a check for electricity leakage before using.

    • Remove the negative pole of the vehicle before using the dent puller.

    • Test welding is required. Otherwise, the plate may be burnt through.

Accessories of dent puller

  1. Functions.

    • As accessories of the dent puller, they are used for pulling and shaping.

  2. Knowledge points.

    • Pulling hammer.

    • Sucker.

    • Wave-shaped wire.

    • Washer (round).

    • Washer (triangular).

    • Pulling hook.

    • Carbon rod and copper bar.

Shaping Machine - Operating Procedures

Reminder
Steps of using shaping machine: power connection — adjustment of equipment parameters — test plate commissioning — multi point pulling — single point pulling.

  1. Connect the power.

    • Select a suitable power connection according to the instructions.

    • Turn on the operating switch.

    Caution
    Check the wiring of dent puller before using, in order to avoid the electric shock caused by electric leakage.

  2. Adjust parameters of the machine.

    • After the dent puller enters the normal operating mode, make adjustments according to the instructions.

    • Select the operation function mode firstly, and adjust the current.

    Caution
    Select a correct working mode of the dent puller.

  3. Welding commissioning.

    • Make test weld on plates made of the same material and of the same thickness.

    • Re-adjust the dent puller based on the result of test weld, and weld the maintained plate after a suitable current is obtained.

  4. Multi-point pulling.

    • Align the washers or wave-shaped wires in a straight line for welding, insert a steel rod into the washers and pull with a single-hook pulling hammer. In case of wave-shaped wires, use the multi-hook pulling hammer for pulling.

    • Pull along the direction vertical to the surface of the plate, and knock the protrusions around the depression with a sheet metal hammer till the depression is recovered to the original state by pulling.

    • Remove the gaskets or other gaskets welded on the plate in sequence after pulling, and grind the repaired area.

    Caution
    Small dents will be left on the plate surface after pulling. Fill them with primer or putty after grinding.

  5. Single-point pulling.

    • Select suitable gaskets based on the type of damage, and weld and pull point by point.

    • Adjust the welding current from small to large to avoid excessive current burning through the plate.

    • Keep the welding torch vertical to the plate surface, and knock the surrounding area with a sheet metal hammer.

    • Grind the welding traces after pulling.

    Caution

    • Perform test welding before repair to avoid excessive current burning through the plate.

    • Observe the operation instructions of the equipment. Over load is forbidden.

    • Faults shall be repaired by professional technicians. Never repair by yourself.

    • Service the dent puller and its accessories regularly. Always cut off the power before servicing.
