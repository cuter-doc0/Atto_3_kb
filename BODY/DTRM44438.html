Quality Inspection Standard of Painting Maintenance

  1. Flatness.

    • The hand feel must be smooth, and there shall be no sags and crests when viewed from the front. There shall be no sags and crests when viewed in any angle in outdoor.

    • Lines shall be flat and smooth. Wave and height inequalities are not allowed.

  2. Paint quality.

    • The paint surface shall be smooth and free of flow marks, fish eyes, blistering and other defects.

    • No defects such as dust spots and pinholes shall be seen when observed within one meter.

  3. Color quality.

    • When the whole vehicle is renovated, the color must be the same without any difference.

    • Paint touch-up is subject to adjacent colors. The color shall be consistent when observed within one meter. There shall be no obvious color difference in long-distance and multi angle observation.

  4. Paint film brightness.

    • Paint surface shall be full of luster and consistent in reflection. No matt surface or uneven paint film texture is allowed.

    • Except for special requirements, no obvious orange peel is allowed on common paint surfaces.

  5. Quality of base coat.

    • Metallic paint particles must be the same in size, density and reflectivity as the original paint surface.

    • Sandpaper marks are not allowed on base coat.

  6. Quality of paint inside plates.

    • Observe the inner surface before sheet metal assembly, and ensure that paint is consistent with that of the original vehicle.

    • The painting of the internal uncovered surface is the same as the painting of the external surface.

    • For internal covered surface, apply putty and grind it, where the paint surface requirements may be slightly lower than those for exterior surface.

    • Paint all tire covers, wiper arms, spray nozzles, and door handles of refurbished vehicles. Paint the inside and outside of the chassis.

  7. Color bar quality.

    • Color bars must be pasted correctly and the deviation must not exceed 1mm.

    • Color bars shall be flat and waves are not allowed.

    • No obvious bubbles are allowed in color bars. The diameter of bubbles is not greater than 1mm. And there are no more than 2 bubbles in each color bar.

  8. Non-machined surface protection.

    • No sandpaper marks are allowed on electroplating and glass surfaces.

    • Scratch and paint mist are not allowed on non-sprayed surfaces.

    • Exhaust pipes, trim panel and rubber strips must not be painted.

  9. Cleaning quality.

    • Before sheet metal assembly, clear sediment and water inside the vehicle.

    • During process inspection, the paint surface must be clean and free of dust, water beads, residual wax, etc.

    • The vehicle must be clean inside and outside at the time of completion inspection.
