Hand hammer and manual jacking block repair process

  1. Knowledge points.

    • Combine the solid hammering and hollow hammering technique according to the damage condition.

    • Solid hammering and hollow hammer technique is adopted for most parts.

    • First of all use a wood hammer or a rubber hammer for preliminary repair.

    • Repair sequence: edges -- curved surfaces -- planes

    • When handling dents, press with the manual jacking block hardly; when handling bulges, set the manual jacking block properly so that it can properly support the steel plate.

    • Determine the position of the manual jacking block according to the hammering sound.

    Caution

    • Excessive solid knocking is easy to cause the steel plate to expand, which requires quenching.

    • Always check for depressions and protrusions on the surface of the plate.

    • Push out the depression on the inside of the plate with the top iron with your hand, and knock down the high point with a hand hammer.

    • The top iron is always placed at the lowest point of the depression inside the plate.

Repair with dent puller

  1. Knowledge points.

    • Film area subjected to grinding shall be 10mm upwards and downwards from deepest dent point of the plate.

    • The clearance of welding gasket shall be about 10mm, and the welding angle shall be 90° relative to the welding surface.

    • The drawing amount is 2-3mm higher than the original plane of the plate. The drawing angle is 90 ° to the damaged surface. Hammer the plastic deformation part during drawing.

    • The gasket shall be removed by turning.

    Caution

    • When using triangular mesons or electrode heads for single-point drawing of the board, pay attention to multi-point light drawing to prevent high points from being generated.

    • In order to prevent holes in the plate during drawing, the current of the welding machine must be adjusted correctly before welding the gasket.

Quenching process

  1. Knowledge points.

    • There are two quenching modes including, quenching with copper electrode and quenching with carbon electrode.

    • For positions subjected to small tension or for high points, quenching shall be carried out first.

    • Copper electrode quenching technology applies to position subjected to large tension; while carbon electrode quenching technology applies to positions subjected small tension or to large-area maintenance.

    • Carbon electrode continuous quenching can be divided into the spiral mode and the line mode, which is selected according to the condition of the damaged surface.

    Caution

    • Before quenching, use the material equivalent to the thickness of the damaged steel plate to debug the quenching equipment.

    • The cooling method is to use the air gun to cool down for 5--6 seconds.

    • After repairing, spray anti-corrosion wax on the back of the board.

Plastic Parts Quick Repair Process

Reminder

  • If the bumper and the plastic parts of the body are punctured or cracked due to impact or scraping, it is necessary to maintain the damaged bumper and other plastic parts quickly because of the untimely supply of spare parts or from the economic point of view, thus reducing the time spent on vehicle maintenance.

  • There are two process methods for the repair of body plastic parts: plastic gluing process and plastic welding process.

Plastics gluing process

  1. Grind and clean the damaged area.

    • Grind the inner side and the outer side of damaged area of spare parts with a special ball pneumatic grinder to remove paint and debris.

    • Grinding range requirement: 10mm leftwards and inwards from the crack, 10mm from the tail end of the crack, with a depth of 1mm.

    • Use dedicated plastic impurities cleaning spray.

    • Spray and wipe the inner side and the outer side of damaged area with a wipe cloth.

    • Cleaning requirements: clean in two times with detergent and wipe cloth, and dry the inner side and the outer side of the damaged position to keep it dry.

  2. Gluing.

    • Select suitable special soft or hard two-component glue according to the material of plastic parts to be repaired.

    • Spray the area to be glued with a special plastic primer so as to improve the adhesion of the adhesive glue.

    • Hold the outer side of the damaged area with a special cloth, and apply two-component filler glue to the inner side of damaged area.

    • Cut the dedicated inner mesh adhesion membrane to appropriate size, and glue the same to the inner side with glue applied.

    • Dry naturally for 30 minutes or dry with infrared paint curing light for 15 minutes, and finish and polish the exterior surface of plastic parts.

Plastics welding process

  1. Grind and clean the damaged area.

    • Grind the inner side and the outer side of damaged area of spare parts with a special ball pneumatic grinder to remove paint and debris.

    • Grinding range requirement: 10mm leftwards and inwards from the crack, 10mm from the tail end of the crack, with a depth of 1mm.

    • Use dedicated plastic impurities cleaning spray.

    • Spray and wipe the inner side and the outer side of damaged area with a wipe cloth.

    • Cleaning requirements: clean in two times with detergent and wipe cloth, and dry the inner side and the outer side of the damaged position to keep it dry.

  2. Welding.

    • Use the special plastic welder.

    • Choose appropriate plastic welding wire.

    • Adjust the temperature of the welding torch to a grade suitable for plastic material.

    • Weld the inner side of the damaged area first, and reinforce with a reinforced wire mesh, with a depth of 1–2mm.

    • Then weld the cracks need to be welded on the surface.

    • Wait for weld cooling down, and place a ruler on the external weld joint.

    • The weld joint shall not be 1mm higher than the surface of substrate.

    • Conduct external polishing with a pneumatic grinder.

Hand hammer and manual jacking block repair process

  1. Knowledge points.

    • The hammering method for aluminium shaping is the same as that for steel plate repair.

    • The repair condition is that the corner of the aluminium alloy plate is free of bending and deformation.

    • For damages involving internal plate deformation, heat the coating with open fire before maintenance.

    • Control the heating temperature at 150°C－200°C with a thermometer or temperature measuring paper.

    Caution

    • Anti-corrosion: Equipped with a set of hand-shaping tools specially used for aluminum plate maintenances, and tools for maintaining steel preparations can be used after removing iron filings.

    • Prevent Oxidation: Treat base metal immediately after sanding and degreasing.

    • Prevent work hardening and cracking: Use heated tools to heat the repaired area to improve repairability.

Repair with dent puller

  1. Knowledge points.

    • Remove the paint film on the damaged area with a single-action grinder and sand paper 80#

    • After adjusting welding machine settings, weld the aluminium dent puller onto the damaged surface.

    • Pull the damaged area with a puller or pull bracket, and hammer the plastic deformation area with a dedicated aluminium maintenance hammer.

    • In case of plate extension after repair, the extended area shall be subjected to quenching treatment.

    Caution

    • If the aluminum alloy plate has the following conditions, it cannot be maintained:

    • Surface cracks and openings.

    • The depressions on the ridges are wrinkled.

    • Deformation of inner panels with buckling and warping.

Nondestructive Paint Repair

Reminder

  • If an impact or external force causes pits and scratches on the sheet metal parts on the exterior of the body, the traditional sheet metal repair is completed through the steps of disassembly, drawing by a meson, grinding and painting, and the repair will damage the antirust function of the original paint and panels.

  • The non-destructive repair of the body paint surface is to complete the non-destructive repair of the body paint surface through advanced tools and technologies such as adhesive drawing and inner side prying of steel plates.

  • There are two methods for non-destructive repair of paint surface: adhesive drawing method and inner prying method.

Glue pull

  1. Repair conditions for glue pull

    • If the paint in the damaged area is damaged or has fallen off, the non-destructive repair is impossible.

    Reminder

    • The working temperature of hot melt adhesive is 0-40 degrees. If the temperature is lower than 0 degrees, the hot melt adhesive will solidify quickly and cannot complete the bonding.

    • Solvent is a flammable and explosive alcohol product, pay attention to safety in use.

    • The gun body of the hot melt glue gun will generate high temperature during use, and the operator needs to prevent burns.

Gluing procedures

  1. Clean the dent area with a cleaning cloth, and mark the damaged point with a marker.

  2. Connect to power and preheat the hot-melt glue gun.

  3. Apply hot-melt glue on the suction cup for pulling, attach the same to the damaged area of the vehicle body, and wait for 20 seconds for drying.

Caution
The hot melt adhesive will generate high temperature when heated or used, and the operator needs to prevent the danger of scalding.

Pull repair procedures

  1. Pull the pulling pad with a special slide pull hammer, sliding the pull hammer gently while hammering the plastic deformation area with a rubber hand hammer.

  2. Spray glue remover on the pad position, and remove the pull pad.

  3. Clean the plate surface with detergent

Reminder

  • Sufficient light is required when pulling and repairing, and auxiliary lights for repairing are required when necessary.

  • If there are still unrepaired dents on the surface of the panel after inspection, repeat the above steps.

Caution
Never use too much force when pulling, which will easily lead to excessive pulling, resulting in high spots and failure of non-destructive repair of the paint surface.

Internal pry & jack repair tools

  1. High strength steel crowbar kit.

  2. Auxiliary repair light.

  3. Rubber hammering rod.

  4. Rubber hand hammer.

  5. Crowbar.

    Caution

    • When selecting the crowbar, the end of the crowbar in contact with the plate should be smooth.

    • When prying, control the force, operate at multiple points, or use other auxiliary accessories as the fulcrum.

    Caution
    The end of the crowbar must be smooth enough and free from other impurities. The sunken plate may be damaged, resulting in failure of non-destructive repair of the paint surface.

Internal pry & jack repair procedure

  1. Clean the dent area with a wipe cloth, and mark the damaged point with a marker.

  2. Install the auxiliary light for repair.

  3. Select an appropriate steel crowbar.

  4. Select an appropriate fulcrum for prying, if no suitable hole can be find in this position, other auxiliary tools can be used as a fulcrum.

  5. Check the damaged surface after prying with the auxiliary light source.

  6. Hammer the bulged points (if any) with a special rubber rod.
