B2AB573 Startup Failed

DTC Description

B2AB573 Startup Failed

Symptom

Functions of refrigeration of the air conditioning system fail.

Possible cause

  1. Radiator fan failure

  2. The condenser has too much dirt.

  3. The refrigerant is not in the standard range.

  4. The air conditioner pipeline is blocked.

  5. The refrigerant leaks.

  6. Compressor failure

Set fault condition

Electric compressor cannot be started.

Fault triggering condition

Set the START/STOP button to ON, and enable the air conditioning refrigeration.

Diagnostic Steps

1
Check the DTC of motor compressor.
  1. Connect the VDS to the data link connector.
  2. Set the START/STOP button to ON.
  3. Clear DTCs.
  4. Set the START/STOP button to OFF and wait for a few seconds.
  5. Set the START/STOP button to ON again, and read the DTC.
  6. Check whether the same DTC is displayed.
    7. Check for “intermittent fault”.
2
Check the cooling fan.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OK and turn on the air conditioner.
  2. Check whether the cooling fan is normal.
    3. Diagnose the “Radiator fan fails not work”.
3
Check the condenser.
  1. Check the condenser for excessive dirt?
    2. Clean or replace the condenser.
4
Check whether there is too little refrigerant in the air conditioning system.
  1. Connect the refrigerant recovery and filling machine.
  2. Check the refrigerant pressure of air conditioning system.
    Pressure gauge Condition Pressure
    Air conditioning system pressure Disable the air conditioner refrigeration mode 0.73~0.83MPa
  3. Check whether the results are normal.
    4. Jump to Step 6.Jump to Step 6. 
5
Check whether the air conditioner pipeline leaks.
  1. Use the refrigerant recovery and filling machine to pressurize the air conditioner pipeline.
  2. Pressurize the air conditioner pipeline to 20bar.
  3. Close the manual valves of the high and low pressure gages on the refrigerant recovery and filling machine.
  4. Wait for 20 minutes and observe the readings of pressure gage. A decrease in reading indicates a leakage in the air conditioning system.
  5. Check the air conditioning system for leaks.
    6. Use electronic leak detection or fluorescent dye leak detection methods to troubleshoot and replace related pipelines.Check and adjust the air conditioning system refrigerant.
6
Check whether the air conditioner pipeline is blocked.
  1. Recover the air conditioning system refrigerant.
  2. Close the low pressure valve at the equipment end.
  3. Use the refrigerant recovery and filling machine to pressurize the high pressure side of the air conditioner pipeline.
  4. Check whether the pressure on the low pressure side of the air conditioner is unresponsive or rises slowly.
    5. The air conditioner pipeline is blocked. Clean or replace the air conditioner pipeline.
7
Check the electric compressor high voltage fuse.
  1. Check whether the electric compressor high voltage fuse is normal
    2. Replace the electric compressor high voltage fuse. Replace the electric compressor .
