Right Front Daytime Running Light Not Working

Circuit Diagram

Terminal Definition

Connector Terminal number Terminal Definition

Right front combination light

3 Power of right daytime running light
10 Ground

Right domain control module

44 Power of right daytime running light

Diagnostic Steps

1
Check the right daytime running light fuse.
  1. Check whether the fuse in the front compartment fuse box F1/20 (7.5A) is normal.
    2. Replace the fuse.
2
Check the wiring harness connector of front combination light.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF
  2. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of front combination light B06.
  3. Check whether the wiring harness connector of front combination light is normal.
    4. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
3
Check the ground line of right front combination light for open circuit.

  1. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness connector of right front combination headlight B06-10 and the ground.

    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
4

    3. Replace the right combination headlamp .
5
Check the wiring harness connector of front compartment fuse box.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF
  2. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of front compartment fuse box B1C.
  3. Check whether the wiring harness connector of front compartment fuse box is normal.
    4. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
6

  2. Check whether the wiring harness connector of right domain control module is normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
8
