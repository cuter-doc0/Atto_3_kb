Diagnosis Description

Introduction

To diagnose the high-mount stop light, understand and get familiar with the working principle of the high-mount stop light. This helps to determine the correct troubleshooting steps, and more importantly, it also helps to determine whether the situation described by the customer is normal. Any fault diagnosis of the high-mount stop light should start with inspection of the high brake light to guide the maintenance technician to take the next logical step for the fault diagnosis.

General equipment

  • Multimeter

  • VDS

Reminder

  • When using a multimeter to measure the voltage, select the closest range.

  • When using a multimeter to measure the resistance, select the closest range.
