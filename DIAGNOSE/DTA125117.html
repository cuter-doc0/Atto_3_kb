Diagnostic Steps

1
Check the electronic smart key.
  1. Check whether the electronic smart key battery is installed reversely and whether the electricity is normal.
  2. Check whether the electronic smart key battery holder is kept in improper contact or damaged.
  3. Check whether the waterproof test paper on the electronic smart key PCB board has changed color.
  4. Check whether the electronic smart key PCB board is oxidized or corroded.
    5. Replace the electronic smart key. .
2
Check the wiring harness connector of right front door handle micro switch.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF
  2. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of right front door handle micro switch U10.
  3. Check whether the wiring harness and connector are normal.
    4. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
3
Check the right front door handle micro switch.
  1. Hold the right front door handle micro switch.
  2. Measure the resistance between the right front door handle micro switch U10-5 and U10-6.
  3. Check whether the results are normal.
