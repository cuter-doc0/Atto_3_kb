Diagnostic Steps

1
Check the communication network of the electric compressor .
  1. Connect the VDS to the data link connector.
  2. Set the START/STOP button to ON.
  3. Use a VDS to execute the network test.
  4. Check whether the electric compressor passes the network detection.
    5. Check for “intermittent fault”.
2
Check the electric compressor fuse.
  1. Check whether the fuse in the front compartment fuse box F1/12 (7.5A) is normal.
    2. Replace the fuse.
3
Check the wiring harness connector of electric compressor.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF
  2. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of electric compressor BA17.
  3. Check the wiring harness connector of electric compressor for corrosion, damage, pin withdrawing, etc.
  4. Check whether the wiring harness connector of electric compressor is normal.
    5. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
4
Check the power of electric compressor.

  1. Set the START/STOP button to ON
  2. Measure the voltage between the wiring harness connector of electric compressor BA17-1 and the ground.

    4. Go to step 7.Go to step 7. 
5
Check the wiring harness connector of front compartment fuse box.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF
  2. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of front compartment fuse box B1D.
  3. Check the wiring harness connector of front compartment fuse box for corrosion, damage, pin withdrawing, etc.
  4. Check whether the wiring harness connector of front compartment fuse box is normal.
    5. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
6
