Diagnostic Steps

1
Check the wiring harness connector of charging port cover motor.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF
  2. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of charging port cover motor.
  3. Check whether the wiring harness connector of charging port cover motor is normal.
    4. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
2
Check the wiring harness connector of right domain control module.
  1. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of right domain control module.
  2. Check whether the wiring harness connector of right domain control module is normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
3
Check the charging port cover motor unlock drive line for open circuit.
  1. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness connector of charging port cover motor BK40-2 and the wiring harness connector of right domain control module KG86(E)-17.
    Connector Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    BK40-2 KG86(E)-17 Throughout Lower than 1Ω
  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
4
Check the charging port cover motor unlock drive line for short to ground.
  1. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness connector of charging port cover motor BK40-2 and the ground.
    Connector Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    BK40-2 Ground Throughout Above 10K Ω
  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
5
Check the charging port cover motor lock drive line for open circuit.
  1. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness connector of charging port cover motor BK40-1 and the wiring harness connector of right domain control module KG86(E)-18.
    Connector Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    BK40-1 KG86(E)-18 Throughout Lower than 1Ω
  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
6
Check the charging port cover motor lock drive line for short to ground.
  1. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness connector of charging port cover motor BK40-1 and the ground.
    Connector Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    BK40-1 Ground Throughout Above 10K Ω
  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
7
Check the charging port cover motor.
  1. Replace the charging port cap motor
  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. Replace the charging port cap motor Replace the right domain control module .
