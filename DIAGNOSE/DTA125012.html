B1C0D71 Washer Motor Stalling

DTC Description

B1C0D71 Washer Motor Stalling

Symptom

Washer Motor Not Working

Possible cause

  1. Wiring harness or connector fault

  2. Washer motor short-circuited

  3. Right domain control module fault

Fault setting conditions

 -

Fault triggering condition

 -

Diagnostic Steps

1
Check the DTC of right body control module
  1. Connect a VDS to the data link connector.
  2. Set the START/STOP button to ON.
  3. Use a VDS to record and then clear the DTC.
  4. Set the START/STOP button to OFF, and wait for a few seconds.
  5. Set the START/STOP button to ON again, and read the DTC.
  6. Check whether the same DTC is displayed.
    7. Check for “intermittent fault”.
2
Check the wiring harness connector of windshield washer motor
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF.
  2. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of windshield washer motor B16.
  3. Check the wiring harness connector of windshield washer motor for corrosion, damage, pin withdrawing, etc.
  4. Check whether the results are normal.
    5. Maintain or replace the damaged wiring harness or connector.
3
Check the wiring harness connector of right body control module
  1. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of right body control module BG86(B) .
  2. Check the wiring harness connector of right body control module for corrosion, damage, pin withdrawing, etc.
  3. Check whether the results are normal.
    4. Maintain or replace the damaged wiring harness or connector.
4
Check the windshield washer motor
  1. Replace the windshield washer motor with a new one and restore the vehicle.
  2. Set the START/STOP button to ON, and conduct a test run to check its function.
  3. Check whether the results are normal.
    4. Replace the windshield washer motor . Replace the right domain control module .
