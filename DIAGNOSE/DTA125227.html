Connector Terminal number Terminal Definition

Left domain control module

Diagnostic Steps

1
Check the right exterior side mirror wiring harness connector.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF
  2. Disconnect the right rearview mirror wiring harness connector U03.
  3. Check whether the right exterior side mirror wiring harness connector is normal.
    4. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
2
Check the ground line of right foot light for open circuit.

  1. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness connector of right rearview mirror U03-12 and the ground.

    Connector

    Condition

  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
3
Check the power of right foot light.

  1. Set the START/STOP button to ON
  2. Turn on the right foot light.
  3. Measure the voltage between the wiring harness connector of right rearview mirror U03-2 and the ground.

    Connector

    Condition

    Voltage

    (+)

    (-)

    U03-2

  4. Check whether the results are normal.
    5. Replace the right foot light .
4
Check the wiring harness connector of left domain control module.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF
  2. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of left domain control module G64(G).
  3. Check whether the wiring harness connector of left domain control module is normal.
    4. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
5
Check the power line of right foot light for open circuit.

  1. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness connector of right rearview mirror U03-2 and the wiring harness connector of left domain control module G64(G)-48.
