Diagnosis Description

The core control component of the rear wiper system is the left body control module and the right body control module. In case of system faults, understand and get familiar with the working principle of the rear wiper system before diagnosing it. If the cause cannot be identified through visual inspection, communicate with the customer patiently. Customers are the best source of information for such faults, especially for intermittent faults. By talking with customers, we can find out the current symptoms and occurrence of faults. It also helps to determine whether the situation described by the customer is normal. Check the relevant service notification as well.

The combination switch is generally used as a power lead-in switch in the electrical control line, for directly turning on or off low power motor or controlling motor normal and reverse rotation. In case of system faults, understand and get familiar with the working principle of the combination switch before diagnosing it. If the cause cannot be identified through visual inspection, communicate with the customer patiently. Customers are the best source of information for such faults, especially for intermittent faults. By talking with customers, we can find out the current symptoms and occurrence of faults. It also helps to determine whether the situation described by the customer is normal. Check the relevant service notification as well.

It is more difficult to diagnose intermittent faults than to diagnose persistent faults. Most intermittent faults are caused by poor electrical connections or faulty circuits. Checking the suspected circuits helps to avoid replacing the functional parts.

General equipment

  • Socket wrench kit

  • Screwdriver

  • Interior trim crow plate

  • Torque wrench

  • VDS

  • Multimeter

Reminder

  • When using a multimeter to measure the voltage, select the closest range.

  • When using a multimeter to measure the resistance, select the closest range.

Precautions

Warning

  • Never pull the wiring harness when disconnecting the connector for fear of damage to the wiring harness.

Caution

  • Never check the electrical signal of the system by piercing the wiring harness with a fine needle.

  • After the installation, check whether the specified torques are applied to the fasteners.
