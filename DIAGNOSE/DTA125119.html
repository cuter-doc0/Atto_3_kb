B22A613 Exterior Right Front Detection Antenna Open-Circuited

DTC Description

B22A613 Exterior Right Front Detection Antenna Open-Circuited

Symptom

Some functions of PEPS failed.

Possible cause

  1. Wiring harness or connector fault.

  2. The right outside handle micro switch fault.

  3. The left domain control module fails.

Set fault condition

The right outside handle micro switch for open circuit.

Fault triggering condition

When the left domain control module receives the signal of the right outside handle micro switch open circuit, the DTC will be generated.

Diagnostic Steps

1
Check the DTC of left domain control module.
  1. Connect a VDS to the data link connector.
  2. Set the START/STOP button to ON
  3. Clear DTCs.
  4. Set the START/STOP button to OFF, and wait for a few seconds.
  5. Set the START/STOP button to ON again, and read the DTC.
  6. Check whether the same DTC is displayed.
    7. Check for “intermittent fault”.
2
Check the wiring harness connector of right outer handle micro switch.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF
  2. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of right outer handle micro switch U10.
  3. Check the wiring harness connector of right outer handle micro switch for normal function.
    4. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
3
Check the wiring harness connector of left domain control module.
  1. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of left domain control module KG64(E).
  2. Check whether the wiring harness connector of left domain control module is normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
4
Check the line of right outer handle micro switch for open circuit.

  1. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness connector of left domain control module KG64(E)-9 and the wiring harness connector of right outer handle micro switch U10-1.
  2. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness connector of left domain control module KG64(E)-3 and the wiring harness connector of right outer handle micro switch U10-2.
    Connector Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    KG64(E)-9 U10-1 Throughout Lower than 1Ω
    KG64(E)-3 U10-2
  3. Check whether the results are normal.
    4. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
5
Replace the right outer handle micro switch, and check the DTC.
  1. Replace the right outer handle micro switch .
  2. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of right outer handle micro switch U10.
  3. Connect the wiring harness connector of left domain control module KG64(E).
  4. Set the START/STOP button to ON
  5. Clear DTCs.
  6. Set the START/STOP button to OFF, and wait for a few seconds.
  7. Set the START/STOP button to ON again and read the DTC of left domain control module.
  8. Check whether the same DTC is displayed.
    9. The system is normal. Replace the left domain control module .
