B22A813 Exterior Trunk Detection Antenna Open-circuited

DTC Description

B22A813 Exterior Trunk Detection Antenna Open-circuited

Symptom

Some functions of PEPS failed.

Possible cause

  1. Wiring harness or connector fault.

  2. The interior detection antenna (rear bumper) fails.

  3. The left domain control module fails.

Set fault condition

The interior detection antenna (rear bumper) circuit is opened.

Fault triggering condition

When the left domain control module receives the signal indicating interior detection antenna (rear bumper) open circuit, the DTC will be generated.

Diagnostic Steps

1
Check the DTC of left domain control module.
  1. Connect a VDS to the data link connector.
  2. Set the START/STOP button to ON
  3. Clear DTCs.
  4. Set the START/STOP button to OFF, and wait for a few seconds.
  5. Set the START/STOP button to ON again, and read the DTC.
  6. Check whether the same DTC is displayed.
    7. Check for “intermittent fault”.
2
Check the wiring harness connector of interior detection antenna (rear bumper).
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF
  2. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of interior detection antenna (rear bumper) RK23.
  3. Check the wiring harness connector of interior detection antenna (rear bumper) for normal function.
    4. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
3
Check the wiring harness connector of left domain control module.
  1. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of left domain control module KG64(E).
  2. Check whether the wiring harness connector of left domain control module is normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
4
Check the interior detection antenna (rear bumper) line for open circuit.

  1. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness connector of left domain control module KG64(E)-12 and the wiring harness connector of interior detection antenna (rear bumper) RK23-1.
  2. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness connector of left domain control module KG64(E)-16 and the wiring harness connector of interior detection antenna (rear bumper) RK23-2.
    Connector Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    KG64(E)-12 RK23-1 Throughout Lower than 1Ω
    KG64(E)-16 RK23-2
  3. Check whether the results are normal.
    4. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
5
Replace the interior detection antenna (rear bumper), and check the DTC.
  1. Replace the interior detection antenna (rear bumper) .
  2. Connect the wiring harness connector of interior detection antenna (rear bumper) RK23.
  3. Connect the wiring harness connector of left domain control module KG64(E).
  4. Set the START/STOP button to ON
  5. Clear DTCs.
  6. Set the START/STOP button to OFF, and wait for a few seconds.
  7. Set the START/STOP button to ON again and read the DTC of left domain control module.
  8. Check whether the same DTC is displayed.
    9. The system is normal. Replace the left domain control module .
