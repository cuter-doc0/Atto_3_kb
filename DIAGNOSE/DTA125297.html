B1CDD13 Right Front Door Light Drive Circuit Broken

DTC Description

B1CDD13 Right Front Door Light Drive Circuit Broken

Symptom

Right Front Door Light Not Going On

Possible cause

  1. Wiring harness or connector fault

  2. Right Front Door Light Fault

  3. Left domain control module fault

Fault setting conditions

Broken drive circuit detected

Fault triggering condition

  1. History DTCs will be cleared automatically after 40 times of ignition cycles

  2. History DTCs will be cleared automatically if no fault is detected in 40 consecutive drives.

Diagnostic Steps

1
Check the DTC of left domain control module
  1. Connect a VDS to the data link connector.
  2. Set the START/STOP button to ON.
  3. Use a VDS to record and then clear the DTC.
  4. Set the START/STOP button to OFF, and wait for a few seconds.
  5. Set the START/STOP button to ON again, and read the DTC.
  6. Check whether the same DTC is displayed.
    7. Check for “intermittent fault”.
2
Check the wiring harness connector of right front door light
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF.
  2. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of right front door light U07.
  3. Check the wiring harness connector of right front door light for corrosion, damage, pin withdrawing, etc.
  4. Check whether the results are normal.
    5. Maintain or replace the damaged wiring harness or connector.
3
Check the wiring harness connector of left domain control module
  1. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of left domain control module TG64(H).
  2. Check the wiring harness connector of left domain control module for corrosion, damage, pin withdrawing, etc.
  3. Check whether the results are normal.
    4. Maintain or replace the damaged wiring harness or connector.
4
Check the wiring harness of right front door light drive for open circuit

  1. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness connector of right front door light U07-2 and the wiring harness connector of left domain control module TG64(H)-40.

    3. Maintain or replace the damaged wiring harness.
5
Check the right front door light
  1. Replace it with a new right door light, and restore the vehicle.
  2. Set the START/STOP button to ON, and conduct a test run to check its function.
  3. Check whether the results are normal.
    4. Replace the right door light . Replace the left domain control module .
