B116212 Water Temperature Sensor Short-circuited

DTC Description

B116212 Water Temperature Sensor Short-circuited

Symptom

-

Possible cause

  1. Coolant temperature sensor is faulty.

  2. Integrated intelligent control module fault.

  3. Wiring harness and connector fault.

Set fault condition

Water temperature sensor is short circuited.

Fault triggering condition

When the vehicle is powered on, and a short circuit in the water temperature sensor is detected, the DTC will be generated.

Diagnostic Steps

1
Check the DTC of power domain control module.
  1. Connect a VDS to the data link connector.
  2. Set the START/STOP button to ON
  3. Scan the ECU module.
  4. Go to power domain control module. Read and clear the DTC.
  5. Set the START/STOP button to OFF and wait for a few seconds.
  6. Set the START/STOP button to ON again.
  7. Go to power domain control module again and read the DTC.
  8. Check whether the same DTC is displayed.
    9. Check for “intermittent fault”.
2
Check the harness and connector of the water temperature sensor for the plate heat exchanger.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF.
  2. Disconnect the harness connector B57 for the water temperature sensor for the plate heat exchanger.
  3. Check for corrosion, damage, needle retreat, and other phenomena in the harness and connector of the water temperature sensor for the plate heat exchanger.
  4. Check whether the results are normal.
    5. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
3
Check the harness and connector of the integrated intelligent controller.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF.
  2. Disconnect the harness connector BK45(B) for the integrated intelligent controller.
  3. Check for corrosion, damage, needle retreat, and other phenomena in the harness and connector of the integrated intelligent controller.
  4. Check whether the results are normal.
    5. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
4
Check if the water temperature sensor for the plate heat exchanger is shorted to power.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to ON.
  2. Measure the voltage between the harness connector B57-1/3 of the water temperature sensor for the plate heat exchanger and the ground.
