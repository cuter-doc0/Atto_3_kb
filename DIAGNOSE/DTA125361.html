Peculiar Smell When Air Conditioner Enabled

Diagnostic Steps

1
Check the air conditioner filter.
  1. Is there excessive dirt on the air conditioner filter ?
    2. Clean or replace the air conditioner filter.
2
Check the evaporator.
  1. Check the evaporator for excessive dirt.
    2. Check the odor sources inside and outside the vehicle. Clean or replace the evaporator.
