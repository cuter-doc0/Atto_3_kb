P1AC900 DC Charging Inductive Signal Disconnected

DTC Description

P1AC900 DC Charging Inductive Signal Disconnected
Symptom The vehicle shows that it cannot be charged, and the instrument shows "Check the on-board charging system".
Possible cause

  1. Wiring harness or connector fault.

  2. Integrated intelligent front drive control module fault.
Set fault condition DC charging induction signal disconnected
Fault triggering condition When DC charging induction signal disconnection is detected, the DTC will be generated.

Diagnostic Steps

1
Check the DTC of instrument panel cluster.
  1. Connect a VDS to the data link connector.
  2. Set the START/STOP button to ON
  3. Scan the ECU module.
  4. Go to instrument panel cluster. Read and clear the DTC.
  5. Set the START/STOP button to OFF and wait for a few seconds.
  6. Set the START/STOP button to ON again.
  7. Go to instrument panel cluster again and read the DTC.
  8. Check whether the same DTC is displayed.
    9. Check for “intermittent fault”.
2
Check the wiring harness connector of integrated intelligent front drive control module.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF
  2. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of integrated intelligent front drive control module BK45 (A).
  3. Check whether the wiring harness connector of integrated intelligent front drive control module is normal.
    4. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
3
Check the wiring harness connector of AC/DC charging port.
  1. Disconnect wiring harness connector of AC/DC charging port B53_3.
  2. Check whether the wiring harness connector of integrated intelligent front drive control module is normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
4
Check the wiring harness connector of CCS communication converter.
  1. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of CCS communication converter B77.
  2. Check the wiring harness connector of CCS communication converter for normal function.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
5
Check the signal line between the integrated intelligent control module and the DC charging for open circuit.

  1. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness connector of integrated intelligent front drive control module BK45(A)-29 and the wiring harness connector of DC charging port B53_3–13.
    Connector Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    BK45(A)-29 B53_3-13 Throughout Lower than 1Ω
  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
6
Check the signal line between the integrated intelligent control module and the CCS communication converter for open circuit.

  1. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness connector of integrated intelligent front drive control module BK45(A)-29 and the wiring harness connector of CSS communication converter B77–1.
    Connector Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    BK45(A)-29 B77–1 Throughout Lower than 1Ω
  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness. Replace the integrated intelligent front drive control module. .
