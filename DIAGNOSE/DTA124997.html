B1BF400 Front Wiper Reset Signal Fault

DTC Description

B1BF400 Front Wiper Reset Signal Fault

Symptom

When the HIGH switch of the wiper is turned on, the wiper not working.

Possible cause

  1. Wiring harness or connector fault

  2. Front wiper HIGH switch fault

  3. Left body control module fault

Diagnostic Steps

1
Check the DTC of the left body control module
  1. Connect a VDS to the data link connector.
  2. Set the START/STOP button to ON.
  3. Use a VDS to record and then clear the DTC.
  4. Set the START/STOP button to OFF, and wait for a few seconds.
  5. Set the START/STOP button to ON again, and read the DTC.
  6. Check whether the same DTC is displayed.
    7. Check for “intermittent fault”.
2
Check the wiring harness connector of front wiper motor.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF.
  2. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of front wiper motor closing switch B04.
  3. Check the wiring harness connector of front wiper motor for corrosion, damage and pin withdrawing.
  4. Check whether the results are normal.
    5. Maintain or replace the damaged wiring harness or connector.
3
Check the wiring harness connector of left body control module
  1. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of left body control module BG64(B).
  2. Check the wiring harness connector of left body control module for corrosion, damage, pin withdrawing, etc.
  3. Check whether the results are normal.
    4. Maintain or replace the damaged wiring harness or connector.
4
Check the front wiper reset signal for open circuit.

  1. Disconnect the front wiper motor wiring harness connector B04.
  2. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness plug of left domain control module BG64(B)-50 and the wiring harness plug of front wiper motor B04-3.
    Plug Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    BG64(B)-50 B04-3 Throughout Lower than 1Ω
  3. Check whether the results are normal.
    4. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
5
Check the front wiper reset signal for short to ground.
  1. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness of front wiper motor plug B04-3 and the ground.
    Plug Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    B04-3 Ground Throughout Above 10K Ω
  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
6
Check the front wiper motor
  1. Replace it with a new front wiper motor and restore the vehicle.
  2. Set the START/STOP button to ON, and conduct a test run to check its function.
  3. Check whether the results are normal.
    4. Replace the front wiper motor . Replace the left domain control module .
