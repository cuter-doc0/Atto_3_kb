P157E12 Outer of Charging Connection Signal Shorted to Power

DTC Description

P157E12 Outer of Charging Connection Signal Shorted to Power
Symptom Fail to Charge.
Possible cause

  1. Wiring harness or connector fault.

  2. Internal fault of on-board charger.
Set fault condition The resistance of the charging connection signal to the positive pole of the power supply is less than the specified threshold.
Fault triggering condition In DC charging state of the vehicle, when the system detects that the resistance of the charging connection signal to the positive pole of the power supply is less than specified threshold, the DTC will be generated.

Diagnostic Steps

1
Check the DTC of on-board charger.
  1. Connect a VDS to the data link connector.
  2. Set the START/STOP button to ON
  3. Scan the ECU module.
  4. Go to on-board charger. Read and clear the DTC.
  5. Set the START/STOP button to OFF and wait for a few seconds.
  6. Set the START/STOP button to ON again.
  7. Go to on-board charger again and read the DTC.
  8. Check whether the same DTC is displayed.
    9. Check the “intermittent fault”.
2
Check the on-board charger data flow.
  1. Read data flow.
  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. Go to step 5Go to step 5 
3
Check the AC charging power supply.
  1. Replace the AC supply for charging.
  2. Confirm that the vehicle is under charing normally.
  4. Check whether the results are normal.
    5. Replace the AC supply for charging.
4
Check the CC signal line for short to power.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF
  2. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of integrated intelligent front drive control module BK45 (B).
  3. The voltage between wiring harness connector BK45 (B)-30 of integrated intelligent front drive control module and the ground.

    (+) (-)

  4. Check whether the results are normal.
    5. Maintain or replace the wire wiring harness
5

  1. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of integrated intelligent front drive control module.
  2. Check whether the wiring harness and connector are aged, deformed, ablated, cracked, etc.
