Diagnostic Steps

1
Check the DTC of electronic power steering control module.
  1. Connect a VDS to the data link connector.
  2. Set the START/STOP button to ON
  3. Clear DTCs.
  4. Set the START/STOP button to OFF, and wait for a few seconds.
  5. Set the START/STOP button to ON again, and read the DTC.
  6. Check whether the same DTC is displayed.
    7. Check for “intermittent fault”.
2
Check the charging system voltage.
  1. Start up the vehicle.
  2. Check whether the instrument charging system fault warning light is on.
    3. Go to “Charging system" for diagnosis.
3
Test the battery status.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF
  2. Perform a battery condition test.
  3. Does the battery pass the test?
    4. Replace the battery .
4
Check the fuse of electronic power steering control module in the front compartment fuse box.
  1. Check whether the fuses F1/41(5A) and F1/50(70A) in the front compartment fuse box are normal.
    2. Replace the fuse.
5
Check the wiring harness plug of electronic power steering control module.
  1. Disconnect the wiring harness plug of electronic power steering control modules B22 and B23.
  2. Check whether the wiring harness and connector are normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
6
Check the constant power of electronic power steering control module.

  1. Measure the voltage between the wiring harness plug of electronic power steering control module B23-1 and the ground.
    Plug Condition Voltage
    (+) (-)
    B23-1 Ground Throughout 11~14V
  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. Go to step 9.Go to step 9. 
7
Check the electronic power steering control module constant power for open circuit.

  1. Disconnect the wiring harness plug of front compartment fuse box B44_3.
  2. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness plug of electronic power steering control module B23-1 and the front compartment fuse box wiring harness plug B44_3-1.
    Plug Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    B23-1 B44_3-1 Throughout Lower than 1Ω
  3. Check whether the results are normal.
    4. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
8
Check the electronic power steering control module constant power circuit for short circuit.

  1. Measure the voltage between the wiring harness plug of electronic power steering control module B23-1 and the ground.
    Plug Condition Voltage
    (+) (-)
    B23-1 Ground Throughout Less than 1V
  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
9
Check the ON power of electronic power steering control module.

  1. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of electronic power steering control module B22.
  2. Set the START/STOP button to ON
  3. Measure the voltage between the wiring harness plug of electronic power steering control module B22-10 and the ground.
    Plug Condition Voltage
    (+) (-)
    B22-10 Ground Throughout 11~14V
  4. Check whether the results are normal.
    5. Go to step 12.Go to step 12. 
10
Replace the battery coolant for the first 2 years or 40,000 km, whichever comes first, and thereafter for every 2 years or 100,000 km.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF
  2. Disconnect the wiring harness plug of front compartment fuse box B1C.
  3. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness plug of electronic power steering control module B22-10 and the front compartment fuse box wiring harness plug B1 C-9.
    Plug Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    B22-10 B1C-9 Throughout Lower than 1Ω
  4. Check whether the results are normal.
    5. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
11
Check the ON power of electronic power steering control module for short circuit.

  1. Measure the voltage between the wiring harness plug of combination switch B22-10 and the ground.
    Plug Condition Voltage
    (+) (-)
    B22-10 Ground Throughout Less than 1V
  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness. Replace the front compartment fuse box .
12
Check the ground circuit of electronic power steering control module

  1. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness plug of combination switch B23-2 and the ground.
    Plug Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    B23-2 Ground Throughout Lower than 1Ω
  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness. Replace the steering gear assembly .
