Diagnostic Steps

1
Check the electronic smart key.
  1. Check whether the electronic smart key battery is installed reversely and whether the electricity is normal.
  2. Check whether the electronic smart key battery holder is kept in improper contact or damaged.
  3. Check whether the waterproof test paper on the electronic smart key PCB board has changed color.
  4. Check whether the electronic smart key PCB board is oxidized or corroded.
    5. Replace the electronic smart key. .
2
Check the storage battery electric quantity.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF.
  2. Measure the battery voltage.
  3. Check whether the results are normal.

    4. Charge or replace the battery .
3
Check the communication network.
  1. Connect a VDS to the data link connector.
  2. Set the START/STOP button to ON
  3. Use a VDS to execute the network test.
  4. Check whether the left domain control module has passed the network test.
    5. Go to step 9.Go to step 9. 
4
Check the fuse of left domain control module.
  1. Check the fuse F4/02(100A) in the positive fuse box for normal function.
    2. Replace the fuse.
5
Check the wiring harness and connector of left domain control module.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF
  2. Disconnect the power terminal of left domain control module BG64(A)_4.
  3. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of left domain control module BG64(B).
  4. Check the power terminal and connector of left domain control module of normal function.
    5. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
6
Check the constant power of left domain control module.

  1. Measure the voltage between the power terminal of left domain control module BG64(A)_4-1 and the ground.

  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. Go to step 8.Go to step 8. 
7
Check the power of left domain control module for open circuit.

  1. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of positive fuse box B46_2.
  2. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness connector of left domain control module BG64(A)_4 and the wiring harness connector of positive fuse box B46_2-1.
    Connector Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    BG64(A)_4 B46_2-1 Throughout Lower than 1Ω
  3. Check whether the results are normal.
    4. Maintain or replace the wiring harness. Replace the positive fuse box.
8
Check the ground line of left domain control module.

  1. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness connector of left domain control module BG64(B)-43 and the ground.
    Connector Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    BG64(B)-43 Ground Throughout Lower than 1Ω
  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
9
Replace the electronic smart key.
  1. Replace the electronic smart key and check whether there is still a fault.
    2. The system is normal. Replace the left domain control module .
