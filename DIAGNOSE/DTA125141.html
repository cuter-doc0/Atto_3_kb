Wireless Charging Not Working

Circuit Diagram

Diagnostic Steps

1
Check the wireless charging switch of the center console.
  1. Check whether the wireless charging function is enabled.
    2. Enable wireless charging function of mobile phone.
2
Check the instrument fuse box fuse.
  1. Check whether the fuse F2/11 (7.5 A) of wireless charging module in the instrument fuse box is normal.
    2. Replace the fuse.
3
Check the wiring harness connector of wireless charging module.
  1. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of wireless charging module.
  2. Check whether the wiring harness connector of wireless charging module is normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
4
Check the power of wireless charging module.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF
  2. Measure the voltage between the wiring harness connector of wireless charging module GT20-1 and the ground.
    Connector Condition Voltage
    (+) (-)
    GT20-1 Ground Throughout 11~14V
  3. Check whether the results are normal.
    4. Go to step 6.Go to step 6. 
5
Check the power line of the wireless charging module for open circuit.
  1. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of instrument fuse box G82.
  2. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness connector of wireless charging module GT20-1 and the wiring harness connector of instrument fuse box G82-12.
    Connector Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    GT20-1 G82-12 Throughout Lower than 1Ω
  3. Check whether the results are normal.
    4. Maintain or replace the wiring harness. Replace the instrument fuse box.
6
Check the ground circuit of wireless charging module.
  1. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness connector of wireless charging module GT20-8 and the ground.
    Connector Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    GT20-8 Ground Throughout Lower than 1Ω
  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness. Replace the wireless charging module .
