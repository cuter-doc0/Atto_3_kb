B225811 Blinder Motor Short-circuited (Shorted to Ground)

DTC Description

B225811 Blinder Motor Short-circuited (Shorted to Ground)

Symptom

No response from blinder when blinder switch is operated

Possible cause

  1. Sunroof blinder motor fault

  2. Wiring harness or connector fault

  3. Right body control module fault

Fault setting conditions

A current above the threshold value is detected for not less than 200ms when the blinder motor is driven

Fault triggering condition

  1. 9~16V

  2. When the right body control module drives the blinder motor

Diagnostic Steps

1
Check the DTC of right domain control module
  1. Connect a VDS to the data link connector.
  2. Set the START/STOP button to ON.
  3. Use a VDS to record and then clear the DTC.
  4. Set the START/STOP button to OFF, and wait for a few seconds.
  5. Set the START/STOP button to ON again, and read the DTC.
  6. Check whether the same DTC is displayed.
    7. Check for “intermittent fault”.
2
Check the wiring harness connector of blinder motor.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF.
  2. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of blinder motor P05(B).
  3. Check the wiring harness connector of blinder motor for corrosion, damage, pin withdrawing and other faults.
  4. Check whether the results are normal.
    5. Maintain or replace the damaged wiring harness or connector.
3
Check the wiring harness connector of right domain control module
  1. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of right domain control module PG86(C).
  2. Check the wiring harness connector of right domain control module for corrosion, damage, pin withdrawing, etc.
  3. Check whether the results are normal.
    4. Maintain or replace the damaged wiring harness or connector.
4
Check the sunroof blinder motor control wiring harness for short to ground

  1. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness connectors of blinder P05(B)-5 and P05(B)-6 and the ground.

  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the damaged wiring harness.
5
Check the sunroof blinder motor
  1. Renew the sunroof blinder motor and restore the vehicle.
  2. Set the START/STOP button to ON, and conduct a test run to check its function.
  3. Check whether the results are normal.
    4. Replace the sunroof blinder motor . Replace the right domain control module .
