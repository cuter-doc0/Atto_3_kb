Diagnosis Description

Introduction

　　When diagnosing the faults of the supplemental restraint system, in order to understand and get familiar with the working principle of supplemental restraint system, go to the description and operation overview. Confirm the faults described by the customer before diagnosis, and then analyze the cause of the supplemental restraint system fault, so as to determine the correct fault diagnosis procedures. When inspecting and measuring the lines and components of the supplemental restraint system, it is preferred to apply the data flow to improve the diagnosis efficiency and shorten the maintenance time. After the fault is confirmed, the precautions and warnings should be understood when maintaining the supplemental restraint system, and the standard operation procedure should be implemented. Check the supplemental restraint system and confirm its working condition after the maintenance.
