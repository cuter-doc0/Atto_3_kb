How to Handle Intermittent Fault

Reminder

To identify the intermittent fault, first ask the customer about driving, weather conditions, frequency and symptom of the fault, and then try to simulate the fault. Determine if the detection circuit is damaged or normal, as well as confirm and troubleshoot. See the general symptom list of each system to narrow down the range of possible failures.

  1. Vibration method

    1. Gently vibrate the sensor that may have failed with your finger and check whether the fault recurs.

    2. Gently shake the connector horizontally and vertically to determine whether the fault recurs.

    3. Gently shake the wiring harness and connector horizontally and vertically to determine whether the fault recurs.

  2. Heating method

    1. Heat the possible fault position with a hair dryer or similar articles to determine whether the fault recurs.

      Caution

      Never damage the structure of parts during heating.

  3. Water spray method

    1. Spray water on the vehicle to determine whether the fault reappears.

      Caution

      • Never spray water directly on the front compartment and high and low voltage components.

      • If the vehicle leaks water, some electrical components will be damaged, so special care must be taken when conducting water spray test on the vehicle.

  4. Other

    1. Switch on all loads and determine whether the fault reappears.
