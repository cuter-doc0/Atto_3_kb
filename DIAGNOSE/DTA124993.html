Diagnostic Steps

1
Use a VDS to test the performance of the front wiper.
  1. Connect a VDS to the data link connector.
  2. Go to front wiper work support and operate the front wiper in the HIGH mode.
  3. Whether the front wiper work properly.
    4. Replace the instrument panel cluster .
2
Check the voltage of HIGH control line of the front wiper motor.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF
  2. Disconnect the front wiper motor wiring harness connector B04.
  3. Set the START/STOP button to ON
  4. Go to front wiper working support, and operate the front wiper to work in the HIGH mode. Measure the voltage between the HIGH control wiring harness plug of front wiper B04-4 and the ground.
    Plug Condition Operating condition
    (+) (-)
    B04-1 Ground Throughout 11~14V
  5. Check whether the results are normal.
    6. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
3
Check the front wiper motor
  1. Remove the front wiper motor.
  2. Connect the battery power to test the operation of the front wiper motor.
    Plug Condition Operating condition
    (+) (-)
    B04-1 B04-4 - High Speed
  3. Check whether the results are normal.
    4. Replace the front wiper motor .
4
Check the HIGH control line of the front wiper motor for open circuit.
  1. Disconnect the front compartment fuse box wiring harness plug B1 D.
  2. Measure the resistance between the LOW control wiring harness plug of front wiper B04-1 and the wiring harness plug of front compartment fuse box B1D-22.
    Plug Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    B04-1 B1D-22 Throughout Lower than 1Ω
  3. Check whether the results are normal.
    4. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
5
Check the HIGH control line of the front wiper motor for short to ground.
  1. Measure the resistance between the HIGH control wiring harness plug of front wiper B04-1 and the ground.
    Plug Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    B04-1 Ground Throughout Above 10K Ω
  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
6
Check the control line of wiper speed relay for open circuit.
  1. Disconnect the cable wiring harness plug BG64(B) of the left domain control module
  2. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness plug of left domain control module BG64(B)-20 and the wiring harness plug of front compartment fuse box B1D-21.
    Plug Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    BG64(B)-20 B1D-21 Throughout Lower than 1Ω
  3. Check whether the results are normal.
    4. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
7
Check the control line of wiper speed relay for short to ground.
  1. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness plug of left domain control module BG64(B)-20 and the ground.
    Plug Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    BG64(B)-20 Ground Throughout Lower than 1Ω
  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
8
Check the wiper HIGH input line for open circuit.

  1. Disconnect the wiring harness plug of left domain control module G64(G).
  2. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of combination switch G02.
  3. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness plug of left domain control module G64(G)-28 and the wiring harness plug of combination switch G02-8.
    Plug Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    G64(G)-28 G02-8 Throughout Lower than 1Ω
  4. Check whether the results are normal.
    5. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
9
Check the wiper HIGH input line for short to ground.
  1. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness plug of left domain control module G64(G)-28 and the ground.
    Plug Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    G64(G)-28 Ground Throughout Above 10K Ω
  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness. Replace the left domain control module .
