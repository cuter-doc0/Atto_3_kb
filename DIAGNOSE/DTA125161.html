Diagnosis Description

Introduction

When diagnosing the fault of park assist system, in order to understand and get familiar with the working principle of the park assist system, go to the description and operation overview. Before diagnosis, confirm the fault described by the customer, and then analyze the fault causes of the park assist system, so as to determine the correct fault diagnosis procedure. When inspecting and measuring the lines and components of the park assist system, it is preferred to apply the data flow, action test and other functions to improve the diagnosis efficiency and shorten the maintenance time. After the fault is confirmed, the precautions and warnings should be understood when maintaining the park assist system, and the standard operation procedure should be implemented. Check the park assist system and confirm its working condition after the maintenance.

General equipment

  • Multimeter

  • VDS

Reminder

  • When measuring the voltage with a multimeter, select the range that is closest to the voltage.

  • When measuring the resistance with a multimeter, select the range that is closest to the resistance.
