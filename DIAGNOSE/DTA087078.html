Diagnosis Description

Introduction

When diagnosing the faults of parking brake system, in order to understand and get familiar with the working principle of the parking brake system, go to the description and overview. Confirm the faults described by the customer before diagnosis, and then analyze the cause of the fault of parking brake system, so as to be helpful to confirm the correct fault diagnosis procedures. When inspecting and measuring the lines and components of the parking brake system, it is preferred to apply the data flow, action test and other functions to improve the diagnosis efficiency and shorten the maintenance time. After the fault is confirmed, the precautions and warnings should be understood when maintaining the parking brake system, and the standard operation procedure should be implemented. Check the parking brake system and confirm its working condition after the maintenance.

General equipment

  • Multimeter

  • VDS

Reminder

  • When measuring the voltage with a multimeter, select the range that is closest to the voltage.

  • When measuring the resistance with a multimeter, select the range that is closest to the resistance.

Precautions

Warning

  • Since the dust covering the rear brake can have harmful effects on the human body, it must be removed with a vacuum cleaner. Do not blow up dust with an air blower.

Caution

When the EPB works, the EPB indicator and parking brake switch indicator on the instrument panel cluster are on.

The brake system MIL lights up when the EPB system has any of the following faults.

  • The state between brake clamping and release cannot be judged.

  • The EPB switch fails to work.

  • The EPB motor fails to work.
