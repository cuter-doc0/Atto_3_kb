B2A2F09 Air Conditioner Pipeline in High or Low Pressure State

DTC Description

B2A2F09 Air Conditioner Pipeline in High or Low Pressure State
Symptom The compressor stops working, and some functions of the air conditioning system are not available.
Possible cause

  1. The refrigerant pressure is too high or too low.

  2. The radiator fan fails wot work.
Set fault condition The right domain module receives incorrect pressure signal.
Fault triggering condition After the vehicle is powered on, turn on the air conditioner refrigeration mode.

Diagnostic Steps

1
Check the cooling fan.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OK and turn on the air conditioner.
  2. Check whether the cooling fan is normal.
    3. NoDiagnose the “Radiator fan fails not work”.
2
Check the refrigerant pressure of air conditioning system.
  1. Connect the refrigerant recovery and filling machine, set the START/STOP button to OK position, and switch on the air conditioner.
  2. Check the refrigerant pressure of air conditioning system.
    Pressure gauge Condition Pressure
    System pressure Disable the air conditioner refrigeration mode 0.73~0.83MPa
    High pressure Enable the air conditioner refrigeration mode 1.47~1.67MPa
    Low pressure Enable the air conditioner refrigeration mode 0.15~0.25MPa
  3. Check whether the results are normal.
    4. Check the air conditioner for leakage and adjust the refrigerant pressure of the air conditioning system. Replace the air conditioner pressure sensor.
