Diagnostic Steps

1
Check the fuse in the front compartment fuse box.
  1. Check whether the fuse in the front compartment fuse box 12 V power socket F1/23 (15 A) is normal.
    2. Replace the fuse.
2
Check the 12 V power socket wiring harness connector.
  1. Disconnect the 12 V power socket wiring harness connector.
  2. Check whether the 12 V power socket wiring harness connector is normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
3
Check the power line of the 12 V power socket.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to ON
  2. Measure the voltage between the 12 V power socket wiring harness connector G 43-2 and the ground.
    Connector Condition Voltage
    (+) (-)
    G43-2 Ground Throughout 11~14V
  3. Check whether the results are normal.
    4. Go to step 5.Go to step 5. 
4
Check the power line of the 12 V power socket for open circuit.
  1. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of front compartment fuse box B1C.
  2. Measure the resistance between the 12 V power socket wiring harness connector G43-2 and the wiring harness connector of front compartment fuse box B1C-30.
    Connector Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    G43-2 B1C-30 Throughout Lower than 1Ω
  3. Check whether the results are normal.
    4. Maintain or replace the wiring harness. Replace the front compartment fuse box .
5
Check the ground circuit of 12V power socket.
  1. Measure the resistance between the 12 V power socket harness connector G43-3 and the ground.
    Connector Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    G43-3 Ground Throughout Lower than 1Ω
  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness. Replace the 12 V power socket.
