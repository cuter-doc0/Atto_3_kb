Diagnostic Steps

1
Use a VDS to test the performance of the front wiper.
  1. Connect a VDS to the data link connector.
  2. Go to front wiper work support and operate all the work of front wiper.
  3. Whether the front wiper work properly.
    4. Go to step 10.Go to step 10. 
2
Check the wiring harness and connector of left domain control module.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF
  2. Disconnect the wiring harness plug of left domain control module BG64(A)_4.
  3. Check whether the wiring harness and connector are normal.
    4. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
3
Check the fuses in positive fuse box.
  1. Check whether the positive fuse box fuse F4/02(100A) is normal.
    2. Replace the fuse.
4
Check the power line of left domain control module.

  1. Measure the voltage between the wiring harness plug of left domain control module BG64(A)_4-1 and the ground.
    Plug Condition Voltage
    (+) (-)
    BG64(A)_4-1 Ground Throughout 11~14V
  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. Go to step 6.Go to step 6. 
5
Check the power line of left domain control module for open circuit.

  1. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness plug of left domain control unit BG64(A)_4–1 and the wiring harness plug of fuse in the front compartment fuse box B46_2–1.
    Plug Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    BG64(A)_4-1 B46_2-1 Throughout Lower than 1Ω
  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness. Go to step 18.Go to step 18. 
6
Check the ground line of left domain control module.

  1. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness plugs of left domain control unit KG64(E)-8 and KG64(E)-20 and the ground.
  2. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness plug of left domain control unit BG64(B)-43 and the ground.
    Connector Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    KG64(E)-8 Ground Throughout Lower than 1Ω
    KG64(E)-20
    BG64(B)-43
  3. Check whether the results are normal.
    4. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
7
Check the left domain control module.
  1. Replace the left domain control module.
  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. At this time, the system is normal and the diagnosis is finished.
8
Check the front wiper motor ground.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF
  2. Disconnect the front wiper motor wiring harness connector B04.
  3. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness of front wiper motor plug B04-4 and the ground.
    Plug Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    B04-4 Ground Throughout Lower than 1Ω
  4. Check whether the results are normal.
    5. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
9
Check the front wiper motor
  1. Remove the front wiper motor.
  2. Connect the battery power to test the operation of the front wiper motor.
    Plug Condition Operating condition
    (+) (-)
    B04-4 B04-1 - High Speed
    B04-4 B04-2 - Low Speed
  3. Check whether the results are normal.
    4. Replace the front wiper motor .
10
Check the instrument fuse box fuse.
  1. Check whether the instrument fuse box fuse F2/11 (7.5 A) is normal.
    2. Replace the fuse.
11
Check the wiring harness connector of combination switch.
  1. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of combination switch G02.
  2. Check whether the wiring harness and connector are normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
12
Check the constant power of combination switch.
  1. Measure the voltage between the wiring harness plug of combination switch G02-5 and the ground.
    Plug Condition Voltage
    (+) (-)
    G02-5 Ground Throughout 11~14V
  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. Go to step 14.Go to step 14. 
13
Check the constant power of combination switch for open circuit.

  1. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness plug of combination switch G02-5 and the instrument fuse box wiring harness plug G82-12.
    Plug Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    G02-5 G82-12 Throughout Lower than 1Ω
  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness. Replace the instrument fuse box.
14
Check the ground circuit of combination switch start button.
  1. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness plug of combination switch G02-3 and the ground.
    Plug Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    G02-3 Ground Throughout Lower than 1Ω
  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
15
Check the front compartment fuse box wiper switch relay fuse.
  1. Check whether the front compartment fuse box fuse F1/21 (30 A) is normal.
    2. Replace the fuse.
16
Check the wiper switch relay control for open circuit.

  1. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness plug of left domain control module BG64(B)-19 and the wiring harness plug of front compartment fuse box B1D-12.
    Plug Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    BG64(B)-19 B1D-12 Throughout Lower than 1Ω
  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
17
Check the wiper switch relay control for short to ground.
  1. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness plug of left domain control module BG64(B)-19 and the ground.
    Plug Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    BG64(B)-19 Ground Throughout Above 10K Ω
  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
18
Replace the front compartment electrical box.
  1. Replace the front compartment fuse box .
  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. At this time, the system is normal and the diagnosis is finished. Replace the instrument panel cluster .
