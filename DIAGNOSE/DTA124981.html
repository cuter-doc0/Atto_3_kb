Diagnostic Steps

1
Check the communication network.
  1. Connect a VDS to the data link connector.
  2. Set the START/STOP button to ON
  3. Use a VDS to execute the network test.
  4. Check whether the intelligent integrated control module passes the network detection.
    5. Go to step 9.Go to step 9. 
2
Check the fuses in the front compartment fuse box.
  1. Check whether the fuses F1/19 (15 A) and F1/33 (15 A) in the front compartment fuse box are normal.
    2. Replace the fuse.
3
Check the wiring harness and plug of intelligent integrated control module.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF
  2. Disconnect the wiring harness plug of intelligent integrated control module BK45(B).
  3. Check whether the wiring harness plug of intelligent integrated control module is normal.
    4. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
4
Check the constant power of integrated intelligent front drive control module.

  1. Measure the voltage between the wiring harness plug of integrated intelligent front drive control module BK45(B)-20 and the ground.
  2. Measure the voltage between the wiring harness plug of integrated intelligent front drive control module BK45(B)-21 and the ground.
  3. Measure the voltage between the wiring harness plug of integrated intelligent front drive control module BK45(B)-26 and the ground.
  4. Measure the voltage between the wiring harness plug of integrated intelligent front drive control module BK45(B)-27 and the ground.

    6. Go to step 6.Go to step 6. 
5

  2. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness plug of integrated intelligent front drive control module BK45(B)-20 and B1C-25.
  3. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness plug of integrated intelligent front drive control module BK45(B)-26 and B1C-25.
  4. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness plug of integrated intelligent front drive control module BK45(B)-27 and B1D-20.
  5. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness plug of integrated intelligent front drive control module BK45(B)-21 and B1D-20.
