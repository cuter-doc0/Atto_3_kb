U016400 Communication with Air Conditioner Failed

DTC Description

U016400 Communication with Air Conditioner Failed

Symptom

-

Possible cause

  1. Fuse fault.

  2. Wiring harness or connector fault.

  3. The right domain control module fails.

  4. Power battery packet fault.

Set fault condition

Abnormal communication with air conditioner

Fault triggering condition

When the vehicle is powered on, and the battery execution and sampling unit does not receive any messages from the air conditioning system, the DTC will be generated.

Diagnostic Steps

1
Check the communication network.
  1. Connect a VDS to the data link connector.
  2. Set the START/STOP button to ON
  3. Use a VDS to execute the network test.
  4. Check whether the right domain control module has passed the network test.
    5. Go to step 8.Go to step 8. 
2
Check the fuse of right domain control module.
  1. Check the fuse F4/04(100A) in the positive fuse box for normal function.
    2. Replace the fuse.
3
Check the wiring harness and connector of right domain control module.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF
  2. Disconnect the power terminal of right domain control module BG86(A).
  3. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of right domain control module BG86(B).
  4. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of right domain control module KG86(D).
  5. Check the power terminal and connector of right domain control module of normal function.
    6. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
4
Check the constant power of right domain control module.

  1. Set the START/STOP button to ON
  2. Measure the voltage between the power terminal of right domain control module BG86(A)-1 and the ground.
    Connector Condition Voltage
    (+) (-)
    BG86(A)-1 Ground Throughout 11~14V
  3. Check whether the results are normal.
    4. Go to step 6.Go to step 6. 
5
Check the power of right domain control module for open circuit.

  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF
  2. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of positive fuse box B46_5.
  3. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness connectors of right domain control module BG86(A)-1 and B46_5-1.
    Connector Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    BG86(A)-1 B46_5-1 Throughout Lower than 1Ω
  4. Check whether the results are normal.
    5. Maintain or replace the wiring harness. Replace the positive fuse box.
6
Check the ground line of right domain control module.

  1. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness connector of right domain control module BG86(B)-1 and the ground.
  2. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness connector of right domain control module BG86(B)-43 and the ground.
  3. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness connector of right domain control module KG86(D)-1 and the ground.
    Connector Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    BG86(B)-1 Ground Throughout Lower than 1Ω
    BG86(B)-43
    KG86(D)-1
  4. Check whether the results are normal.
    5. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
7
Check the CAN line of right domain control module.

  1. Set the START/STOP button to ON
  2. Measure the voltage between the wiring harness connector of right domain control module BG86(B)-25 and the ground.
  3. Measure the voltage between the wiring harness connector of right domain control module BG86(B)-26 and the ground.
    Connector Condition Voltage
    (+) (-)
    BG86(B)-25 Ground Throughout 2.5~3.5V
    BG86(B)-26 1.5~2.5V
  4. Check whether the results are normal.
    5. Go to "CAN" for diagnosis Replace the right domain control module .
8
Check the DTC of right domain control module.
  1. Go to right domain control module. Read and clear the DTC.
  2. Set the START/STOP button to OFF and wait for a few seconds.
  3. Set the START/STOP button to ON again.
  4. Go to right domain control module again and read the DTC.
  5. Check whether there is a DTC.
    6. Go to “Right Domain Control Module" for diagnosis.
9
Check the DTC of battery execution and sampling unit.
  1. Go to battery execution and sampling unit. Read and clear the DTC.
  2. Set the START/STOP button to OFF and wait for a few seconds.
  3. Set the START/STOP button to ON again.
  4. Go to battery execution and sampling unit DTC again and read the DTC.
  5. Check whether other DTC exists.
    6. Go to "Battery Execution and Sampling Unit" for diagnosis.
10
Check the DTC of other modules.
  1. Use the VDS to read the DTC of other modules.
  2. Check whether the DTC of failed communication with air conditioner is read in other modules.
    3. Replace the right domain control module . Replace the power battery pack .
