U02A200 Communication with Active Release Module Failed

DTC Description

U02A200 Communication with Active Release Module Failed

Symptom

-

Possible cause

  1. Wiring harness or connector fault.

  2. Integrated intelligent front drive control module fault.

  3. Power battery packet fault.

Set fault condition

Abnormal communication with electronic control and PDC.

Fault triggering condition

When the vehicle is powered on and the communication between electric control and PDC is abnormal, the DTC will be generated.

Diagnostic Steps

1
Check the DTC of battery execution and sampling unit.
  1. Connect a VDS to the data link connector.
  2. Set the START/STOP button to ON
  3. Scan the ECU module.
  4. Go to battery execution and sampling unit. Read and clear the DTC.
  5. Set the START/STOP button to OFF and wait for a few seconds.
  6. Set the START/STOP button to ON again.
  7. Go to battery execution and sampling unit DTC again and read the DTC.
  8. Check whether the same DTC is displayed.
    9. Check for “intermittent fault”.
2
Inspection of the battery state
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF
  2. Perform a battery condition test.
  3. Does the status of the battery pass the test?
    4. Replace the battery .
3
Check the wiring harness connector of power battery pack.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF
  2. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of power battery pack BK51.
  3. Check whether the wiring harness connector of power battery pack is normal.
    4. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
4
Check the wiring harness connector of integrated intelligent front drive control module.
  1. Disconnect the wiring harness connectors of integrated intelligent front drive control module BK45(A) and BK45(B).
  2. Check whether the wiring harness connector of integrated intelligent front drive control module is normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
5
Check the power line of power battery pack.

  1. Connect the wiring harness connectors of integrated intelligent front drive control module BK45(A) and BK45(B).
  2. Set the START/STOP button to ON.
  3. Measure the voltage between the wiring harness pack connector BK51-4 of power battery and the ground.
  4. Measure the voltage between the wiring harness connector of power battery pack BK51-5 and the ground.
  5. Measure the voltage between the wiring harness connector of power battery pack BK51-6 and the ground.

7
Check the ground line of power battery pack for open circuit.

  1. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness connector of power battery pack BK51-17 and the ground.

  2. Set the START/STOP button to ON
  3. Measure the voltage between the wiring harness pack connector BK51-8 of power battery and the ground.
  4. Measure the voltage between the wiring harness pack connector BK51-9 of power battery and the ground.

9
Replace the integrated intelligent front drive control module.
  1. Replace the integrated intelligent front drive control module,and recover the car.
  2. Set the START/STOP button to ON
  3. Use the VDS to read the DTC of battery execution and sampling unit.
  4. Clear DTCs.
  5. Set the START/STOP button to ON again, and read the DTC.
  6. Check whether the same DTC is displayed.
    7. The system is normal. Replace the power battery pack .
