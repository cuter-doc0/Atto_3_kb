DTC Description

Diagnostic Steps

1
Check the communication network.
  1. Connect a VDS to the data link connector.
  2. Set the START/STOP button to ON
  3. Use a VDS to execute the network test.
  4. Check whether the intelligent brake control module passes the network detection.
    5. Go to step 11.Go to step 11. 
2
Check the fuses in the front compartment fuse box.
  1. Check the fuses in the front compartment fuse box F1/42(5A), F1/30(60A) and F1/44(60A) for normal function.
    2. Replace the fuse.
3
Check the wiring harness and plug of intelligent brake control module.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF
  2. Disconnect the wiring harness plug of intelligent brake control module B03.
  3. Check whether the wiring harness plug of intelligent brake control module is normal.
    4. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
4
Check the power of intelligent brake control module.

  1. Measure the voltage between the wiring harness plug of intelligent brake control module B03-14, B03-46 and the ground.
    Plug Condition Voltage
    (+) (-)
    B03-14 Ground Throughout 11~14V
    B03-46
  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. Go to step 6.Go to step 6. 
5
Check the constant power of intelligent brake control module for open circuit.

  1. Disconnect the wiring harness plug B1C from the front compartment fuse box.
  2. Set the START/STOP button to OK.
  3. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness plug of intelligent brake control module B03-14 and the wiring harness connector of front compartment fuse box B1C-4.
  4. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness plug of intelligent brake control module B03-46 and the wiring harness connector of front compartment fuse box B1C-1.
    Plug Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    B03-14 B1C-4 Throughout Lower than 1Ω
    B03-46 B1C-1
  5. Check whether the results are normal.
    6. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
6
Check the ON power of intelligent brake control module.

  1. Set the START/STOP button to ON
  2. Measure the voltage between the wiring harness plug of intelligent brake control module B03-36 and the ground.
    Plug Condition Voltage
    (+) (-)
    B03-36 Ground Throughout 11~14V
  3. Check whether the results are normal.
    4. Go to step 8.Go to step 8. 
7
Check the ON power of intelligent brake control module for open circuit.

  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF
  2. Disconnect the wiring harness plug B1C from the front compartment fuse box.
  3. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness plug of intelligent brake control module B03-36 and the wiring harness connector of front compartment fuse box B1C-10.
    Plug Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    B03-36 B1C-10 Throughout Lower than 1Ω
  4. Check whether the results are normal.
    5. Maintain or replace the wiring harness. Replace the front compartment fuse box .
8
Check the smart brake control module ground.

  1. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness plug of intelligent brake control module B03-1 and the ground.
  2. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness plug of intelligent brake control module B03-30 and the ground.
    Plug Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    B03-1 Ground Throughout Lower than 1Ω
    B03-30
  3. Check whether the results are normal.
    4. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
9
Check CAN-H line of intelligent brake control module.

  1. Set the START/STOP button to ON
  2. Measure the voltage between the wiring harness plug of intelligent brake control module B03-5 and the ground.
    Plug Condition Voltage
    (+) (-)
    B03-5 Ground Throughout 2.5~3.5V
  3. Check whether the results are normal.
    4. Go to "CAN" for diagnosis
10
Check CAN-L line of intelligent brake control module.

  1. Measure the voltage between the wiring harness plug of intelligent brake control module B03-19 and the ground.
    Plug Condition Voltage
    (+) (-)
    B03-19 Ground Throughout 1.5~2.5V
  2. Check whether the results are normal.
    3. Go to "CAN" for diagnosis Replace the smart brake control module .
11
Check the DTC of smart brake control module.
  1. Use the VDS to read the DTC of smart brake control module.
  2. Check whether there is a DTC.
    3. Go to "Brake Intelligent Control Module" for diagnosis.
12
Check the DTC of electronic power steering control module.
  1. Use the VDS to read the DTC of electronic power steering control module.
  2. Check whether there is a DTC.
    3. Go to "Electronic Power Steering Control Module" for diagnosis.
13
Check the DTC of other modules.
  1. Does the other module read the loss of communication with the intelligent brake control module?
    2. Replace the smart brake control module . Replace the electronic power steering control module .
