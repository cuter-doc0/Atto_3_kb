B164B11 Front Passenger Seat Belt Pretensioner Shorted to Ground

DTC Description

B164B11 Front Passenger Seat Belt Pretensioner Shorted to Ground

Symptom

Some functions of supplemental restraint system failed.

Possible cause

  1. Wiring harness or connector fault.

  2. Front passenger seat belt pretensioner 1 fault.

  3. Airbag control module fault.

Set fault condition

The front passenger seat belt pretensioner 1 is shorted to ground.

Fault triggering condition

When the airbag control module receives the signal that the front passenger seat belt pretensioner 1 is shorted to ground, the DTC will be generated.

Diagnostic Steps

1
Check the DTC of supplemental restraint system.
  1. Connect a VDS to the data link connector.
  2. Set the START/STOP button to ON
  3. Clear DTCs.
  4. Set the START/STOP button to OFF, and wait for a few seconds.
  5. Set the START/STOP button to ON again, and read the DTC.
  6. Check whether the same DTC is displayed.
    7. Check for “intermittent fault”.
2
Check the wiring harness connector of front passenger seat belt pretensioner 1.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF
  2. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of front passenger seat belt pretensioner 1 K04(B).
  3. Check the wiring harness connector of front passenger seat belt pretensioner 1 for normal function.
    4. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
3
Check the wiring harness and connector of airbag control module.
  1. Disconnect the wiring harness connector of airbag control module KG10_1.
  2. Check whether the wiring harness and connector of airbag control module are normal.
    3. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
4
Check the line of front passenger seat belt pretensioner 1 for short to ground.

  1. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness connector of front passenger seat belt pretensioner 1 K04(B)-1 and the ground.
  2. Measure the resistance between the wiring harness connector of front passenger seat belt pretensioner 1 K04(B)-2 and the ground.
    Connector Condition Resistance
    (+) (-)
    K04(B)-1 Ground Throughout Above 10K Ω
    K04(B)-2
  3. Check whether the results are normal.
    4. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
5
Replace the front passenger seat belt pretensioner 1, and check the DTC.
  1. Replace the front passenger seat belt pretensioner 1, and restore the vehicle.
  2. Set the START/STOP button to ON.
  3. Read the DTC of supplemental restraint system.
  4. Clear DTCs.
  5. Set the START/STOP button to OFF and wait for a few seconds.
  6. Set the START/STOP button to ON again and read the supplemental restraint system DTC.
  7. Check whether the same DTC is displayed.
    8. The system is normal. Replace the airbag control module .
