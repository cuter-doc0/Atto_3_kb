P1BBF00 Front Drive Motor Rotary Transformer Fault - Signal Lost

DTC Description

P1BBF00 Front Drive Motor Rotary Transformer Fault - Signal Lost

Symptom

-

Possible cause

  1. Wiring harness or connector fault.

  2. Drive motor fault.

  3. Motor control module fault.

Set fault condition

loss of front drive motor rotary transformer signal.

Fault triggering condition

When the vehicle is powered on and the system receives that the rotary transformer signal of the front drive motor is lost, the DTC will be generated.

Diagnostic Steps

1
Check the DTC of front drive motor control unit.
  1. Connect a VDS to the data link connector.
  2. Set the START/STOP button to ON
  3. Scan the ECU module.
  4. Go to front drive motor control module. Read and clear the DTC.
  5. Set the START/STOP button to OFF and wait for a few seconds.
  6. Set the START/STOP button to ON again.
  7. Go to front drive motor control module again and read the DTC.
  8. Check whether the same DTC is displayed.
    9. Check for “intermittent fault”.
2
Check the motor rotary transformer connector wiring harness and connector.
  1. Set the START/STOP button to OFF
  2. Remove the end cover at the connection between the integrated intelligent front drive control module and the drive motor copper bar.
  3. Check whether the motor rotary transformer wiring harness and connector is normal.
    4. Maintain or replace the wiring harness.
3
Check the motor rotary transformer connector resistance.
  1. Measure the resistance between terminals 3 and 8 of the rotary transformer connector.
  2. Measure the resistance between terminals 4 and 9 of the rotary transformer connector.
  3. Measure the resistance between terminals 5 and 10 of the rotary transformer connector.
    Connector Wire color Resistance
    (+) (-)
    3 8 White and green

    Excitation: 14.5Ω±2Ω
    4 9 Red and black Cosine: 42Ω±4Ω
    5 10 Yellow and blue Sine: 36Ω±4Ω
  4. Check whether the results are normal.
    5. Replace the drive motor assembly. .Replace the integrated intelligent front drive control module. .
