P15834B Temperature Sampling 2 High

DTC Description

P15834B Temperature Sampling 2 High
Symptom Fail to Charge.
Possible cause

  1. Lack of coolant.

  2. The coolant pump works improperly.

  3. The fan works improperly.

  4. Internal fault of on-board charger.
Set fault condition The temperature of the channel temperature sensor is higher than the specified threshold.
Fault triggering condition When the vehicle is under AC charging state, and the channel temperature sensor is more than the specified threshold, the DTC will be generated.

Diagnostic Steps

1
Check the DTC of on-board charger.
  1. Connect a VDS to the data link connector.
  2. Set the START/STOP button to ON
  3. Scan the ECU module.
  4. Go to on-board charger. Read and clear the DTC.
  5. Set the START/STOP button to OFF and wait for a few seconds.
  6. Set the START/STOP button to ON again.
  7. Go to on-board charger again and read the DTC.
  8. Check whether the same DTC is displayed.
    9. Check for “intermittent fault”.
2
Inspection of coolant
  1. Check whether there is sufficient coolant.
    2. Add coolant and check the cooling system for leaks.
3
Check the working condition of the coolant pump.
  1. VDS enters the pump work support and operates the pump work.
  2. Check whether the coolant pump is working properly.
    3. Go to “Coolant Pump Not Working" for diagnosis.
4
Check the fan operation.
  1. VDS enters the fan operation support and operates the fan work.
  2. Check whether the fan is working properly.
    3. Go to "Fan Failure" for diagnosis. Replace the integrated intelligent front drive control module. .
