Diagnosis Description

The sunroof system of this vehicle is a panoramic sunroof, and the sunroof control motor assembly (integrated into the right domain control module) can open, close, tilt up and tilt down the sunroof by controlling the forward and reverse rotation of the motor.

The vehicle is also equipped with an electric blinder system, which can open and close the blinder according to the sunroof switch state and the blinder control switch.

In case of system faults, understand and get familiar with the working principle of the sunroof system before diagnosing it. If the cause cannot be identified through visual inspection, communicate with the customer patiently. Customers are the best source of information for such faults, especially for intermittent faults. By talking with customers, we can find out the current symptoms and occurrence of faults. It also helps to determine whether the situation described by the customer is normal. Start with "traditional" problems, which is one of the best ways to troubleshoot the sunroof system, and check the relevant service bulletin.

General equipment

  • Socket wrench kit

  • Torque wrench

  • Long hex screwdriver bit

  • Screwdriver

  • VDS

  • Multimeter

Reminder

  • When using a multimeter to measure the voltage, select the closest range.

  • When using a multimeter to measure the resistance, select the closest range.

Precautions

Warning

  • In case of accidental blockage during the use of the electric sunroof/blinder, cut off the power supply of the starter iron battery to prevent the sunroof/blinder motor from burning out.

  • Never pull the wiring harness when disconnecting the connector for fear of damage to the wiring harness.

Caution

  • If operation in vehicle is required, cover the seat, steering wheel and other parts to avoid dirt.

  • Wear gloves when maintaining the blinder to avoid polluting the blinder fabric.

  • After the installation, check whether the specified torques are applied to the fasteners to ensure that each part functions normally.

  • After the installation, self-learning of sunroof/blinder is required.
