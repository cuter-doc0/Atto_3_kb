Diagnosis Description

Introduction

　　When diagnosing the instrument panel cluster faults, in order to understand and get familiar with the working principle of instrument panel cluster, go to the description and operation overview. Confirm the faults described by the customer before diagnosis, and then analyze the cause of the instrument panel cluster fault, so as to confirm the correct fault diagnosis procedure. When inspecting and measuring the lines and components of the instrument panel cluster, it is preferred to apply the data flow to improve the diagnosis efficiency and shorten the maintenance time. After the fault is confirmed, the precautions and warnings should be understood when maintaining the instrument panel cluster, and the standard operation procedure should be implemented. Check the instrument panel cluster and confirm its working condition after the maintenance.
